Prof. Cordelia Beattie is giving a paper deriving from the Alice Thornton's Books project to the University of Glasgow's [Centre for Gender History research seminar](https://www.gla.ac.uk/research/az/genderhistory/events/headline_1223142_en.html) on Monday 23 February 2026, 1-3pm.

The paper is entitled 'Alice Thornton’s Books and Remembering Gender-based Violence in Seventeenth-century England'.
