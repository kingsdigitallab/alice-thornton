On Saturday 18 April 2026 Prof. Cordelia Beattie was interviewed for Stuart Saturday Live about the life and legacy of Alice Thornton by Andrea Zuvich aka the Seventeenth Century Lady. You can catch up on Andrea's [YouTube](https://www.youtube.com/live/wFBYzI1NRyo?si=4kQspKJ0MCSODkl1) channel or listen via [Spotify](https://open.spotify.com/episode/49btuYXF40iDlPVjVayQKq). 

Andrea Zuvich is a British-American historian of the seventeenth century, specialising in the Late Stuart period. She has appeared as a consultant on both TV (BBC 4's Downfall: Charles I) and radio (BBC Woman's Hour) and is the author of numerous magazine articles and several books including *Ravenous: A Life of Barbara Villiers, Charles II's Most Infamous Mistress* (2024).
