The time which Mr Bennett sent him to Stongrave was in
The yeare 1663 about the month of October.

Affter he was receaved into the Parish & Preached constantly
both ends of the day: besides constant Prayer on frydays and on
wednesday. he expounded methodically u ppon every Holyday
throught the yeare. and Catechized all the Children and youth
in the whole Parish. which we had not bin used to the good way
tho much desird it. & the youth much improved by his Catechising.

Att the first he was Tabled att Stongrave att George Mastr-nan
from thence he removed to mess to Mr Tullye s who was much in
love with his Person & Preaching soe contineued for some time there
which was a great deale of Paines. he tooke in comming from thence
to stongrave soe offten in a weeke beeing 2 miles from the Church.
