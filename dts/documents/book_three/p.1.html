  16682Editorial Note Thornton became a widow on 17 September 1668. There is a later addition on this page: ‘A manuscript written by my Dear Grandmother Mrs Thornton’ (see image). Thornton left her Books to her daughter, Alice Comber, in her will, and this is probably Thomas Comber (1688-1765), eldest son of Alice and Thomas Comber. See ‘Will of Alice Thornton, 10 April 1705’, in The Autobiography of Mrs. Alice Thornton of East Newton, Co. York, ed. Charles Jackson, Surtees Society 32 (Durham: Andrews & Co., 1875), 338.
