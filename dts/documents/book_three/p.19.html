Heare my Prayer O Lord, and hide not thy face
from my teares, but deliver me in this needfull time of
trouble, O forsake me not in my affliction; now thou hast
brought me to the midle time of my Age through many
& great tribulations, which many of my forefathers never saw
nor was, I worthy to passe soe offten from the gates of death.
