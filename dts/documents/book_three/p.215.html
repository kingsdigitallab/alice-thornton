Therefore that I might use all lawfull & comendable meanes

I writt to my good Freind Mr Renold Grame at London

to give him a full account of Proceedings & what Conditions I

was left in to By Debts who was truly sencable of my suffrings

as appeared in his letter to my uncle will.m when he writt to him

to gett my husbands Bond from him my letter Dated June 20 69.

Two Letters of myne the Copyes of them allso extant To my

Lady yorke . affter Publication to that same Effect she beeing soe

much conserned to breake the match uppon falls lyes and sugestion

of my bitter & malicious Enymys was uppon more serious thoughts

and true Information much afflicted for my wrongs and highly

sattisfied with all our honest Procedings.

My letter allso to my good neece Faire fax found great

acceptance with her nor had she ever a misdeemeng thought of me

or any conserned but wished us all immaginable Joy & comfort

These dated June 21 1669.
