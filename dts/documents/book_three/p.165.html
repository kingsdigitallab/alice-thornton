to make me yeald to these unjust demands knowing how
low my husband s Estate was judged we could not obteine it
by Course of Law or have any right from him.

This sad oppression was very greivous over my weake
spirritts who had non in the world to take my part or to assist
me to gett my dues. nor was it judged fitt to advise with my
deare uncle Sir Ed. osborne s Relations about this conserne

hinder the payment of the Annuity for Mr Th. Debts.
