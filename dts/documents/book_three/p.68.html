O my God I humbly beg of thy glorious Mercy to Pardon & forgive

all my impatience, & ungovrnable sorrow which came on me for the loss

of my holy good name which thou hadest given me all my daies & that great

and precious oyntment I injoyed beeing the comfort of my life in all

my other afflictions.

But this beeing soe fallsly imputed uppon thy handmaide with out

any cause or tendancy, could not but be more bitter then death which

I had much rather have chosen. that I might have given up my body to

my heavenly Father undefiled with out the very spott of uncleaness

or immodesty, haveing ever made it my indeavour to walke before

God in what Estate of innocency I was capabable Sic. in this life Ever in-

deavouring to keepe my Consience voyd of offence, both before God &

man, for which grace I humbly blesse & Praise the lord for Ever.

by restoring this thy good name given me & my want of Conside
