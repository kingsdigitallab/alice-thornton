Jan.3 53 My Eldest Daughter Alice Thornton , Borne att Hipswell

att my deare mothers Joynture. in the yeare: 1653 And in Jan. 3d

she beeing obteined (by Gods great and miraculous mercyes; & goodnes

in her delivrances from many deaths; & convoltions & dangers has now

bin preserved to me. to live to this time, and of the Age, of (Jan. 3d

1668) Fifteene years, and fouer months old.

For which great mercy I humbly bless & praise, & glorify the great

God of heaven for spareing her to me, thus Long. & beg she may live

to thy glory, & my Comfort & her owne, salvation. Amen.
