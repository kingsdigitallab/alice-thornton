Affter I receaved this most Comfortable letter from my
deare and only Aunt, I Receaved annother letter from good
Dafeny of her acquainting my other freinds with the marriage
of my deare Childe and of her procuring monney for me to
Pay Mr Thorntons Debts & to lett me know she had shewed all
my Papers conserning the occasion and Transaction of that
