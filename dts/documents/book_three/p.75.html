Haveing bin soe large uppon this subject of having the

blessing of God bestowed uppon this Family & Parish wherein

I live I cannot with hold my selfe from doeing that duty

Salvation.

sattisfaction of the necesity of receaving the holy Communion of

the Lord Jesus Christ.
