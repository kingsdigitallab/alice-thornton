& give me the spiritt of Praier:, & supplications
& direct my heart aright to thee the Almighty
God of Salvation; O let the words of my mouth
And the thoughts of my heart, & meditations
be acceptable to thy devine Majesty: now & at all
times, O God my strength & my Salvation.
