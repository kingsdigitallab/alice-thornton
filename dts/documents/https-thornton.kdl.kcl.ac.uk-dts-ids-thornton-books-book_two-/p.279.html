That I fell into a sad Trembling & shaking of an Ague and as I walked with my Brothr Denton in the hall & discoursed of this I found my selfe extreamely ill in my bodie as well as minde. And so proceded to great Extreamity of Those which broke sudainly on me & began to be violent & weakned me soe greatly That I could not expect but my death should proceed. & Tho Dr witty was called to use his best Art and skill yett such was my Inward greife & affliction for this unkinde part towards me & the Children of my wombe that I could take noe Ease & rest joyning with my daily expectatn of Death & that againe agravated by the knowing there was nothing fixed for non of my Children neithr Son nor Daughters And that my cutting my selfe & mine of the Binifitt of my 1000l out of Ireland to pay Debts & to purchase for my 2 daughtes I had
