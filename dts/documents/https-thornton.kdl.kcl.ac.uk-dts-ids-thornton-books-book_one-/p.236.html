why all Mrs Danbys freinds, that non of them could in dure
her of her husbands side, but I that looket at her or shewd any
respect. I said that I could not soe well know the reason

reconsiled because of kitts marriage to her against his
knowledge or consent. Then Mrs Batte said it was true
