And to that end my Brother Denton with Mr Comber
& my selfe was exceedingly imployed att all times
almost when he was in a condittion for bussinesse.
Hastening the Draughts of writtings, & settlements, with
Councell how to give all Persons sattisfaction in there
Just Debts. And not till the 28th day of may: 1668
was our settlement Perfected from the time of my Co.
Covills beeing destroyed. for either Portions or mainte
nance. which went most sadly with me. but now by the great
Paines & industery of my Brother Denton. & Mr Comber
who we did for my Childrens good imploy , as a
freind to them in Generall, as well as in his Perticuler
