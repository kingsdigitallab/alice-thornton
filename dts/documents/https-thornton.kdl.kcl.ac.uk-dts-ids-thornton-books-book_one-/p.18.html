O give thanks unto the Lord, for hee is gracious: &
his mercys endureth for ever. Let them give than
-ks whom the Lord hath redeemed, & delivered from
the Stormes, & mighty tempests. he has delivred
us from this distresse, when the deepe had opened
her mouth to swallow us up. then was the hand
of the Lord mighty to deliver, & preserve us from
drowning, Oh that we would therefore praise the Lord
for his goodnesse, & speake good of his name, &
declaire the wonders that he hath don to us Children.
