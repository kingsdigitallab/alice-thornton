13 1631.

After this, it pleased the Lord to

begin to come into my soule by some

beames of his mercy, in puting good

Thoughts into my mind to consider

his Great & miraculous Power in

the Creation of the Heavens. the Earth

& all therein. contained, upon the

Reading of my daily Psalmes for the

months, which hapened that day to be Psal

147 verce the 4th :

He counteth the Starres & caleth

them all by there names. from

whence there came a foresable consid

-eration of the Incomprehencable Powr

& infinit majestie of Almighty

God. who made all things. in the Hea

-vens & the Earth. beeing above all his

Creaturs in the world. & knew what

was.
