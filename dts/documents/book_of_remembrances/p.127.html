I hope gained such beleife that the Lord has
apeared to stand on my side & therefore I
need not caire what men can say against
me. but will give all possible Glory & praise
adoration & thanks to my Gracious God that
would not suffer me to depart this life with
any blott upon my person. but to aprove my
