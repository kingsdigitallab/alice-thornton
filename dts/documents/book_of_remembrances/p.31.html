29 1657

I was delivered of my fifth

Child Childe being beeing a goodly Son Sonne

upon uppon the 10th day of Decemb December

1657 between betweene the hours houres

of two 2 and three 3 o'clock a clocke in the

morning upon uppon Thursday

having haveing had very sore travel

endanger indanger of my Life from

that time in the morning on

wednesday. caused by the

Childs coming into the world with

his feet feete first, and so caused

him to be strangled strangeled almost allmost

in the birth. he lived about

half halfe an hour houer so died, and

was buried in Cattericke Church

the same day by Mr Syddall Siddall :

he was Turned wrong in my womb wombe

by the fall I had in Sept. before
