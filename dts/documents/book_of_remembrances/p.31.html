29 1657

I was delivered of my fifth
Childe beeing a goodly Sonne
uppon the 10th day of Decemb December
1657 betweene the houres
of 2 and 3 a clocke in the
morning uppon Thursday
haveing had very sore travel
indanger of my Life from
that time in the morning on
wednesday. caused by the
Childs coming into the world with
his feete first, and so caused
him to be strangeled allmost
in the birth. he lived about
halfe an houer so died, and
was buried in Cattericke Church
the same day by Mr Siddall :
he was Turned wrong in my wombe
by the fall I had in Sept. before
