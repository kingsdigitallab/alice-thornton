31 1659.

It pleased God to vissit my
deare & hon.red honoured Mother the Lady
Wandesford with her last sickenesse
upon Friday the 17th of november 1659 beging beginning
then 18th an execeeding great Cough
tormenting her bodie by stitches in
her breast, & short breathing;
these stitches contineued a bout 14
daies with the Cough hindreing her from
almost any sleepe. when upon the
use of bagges with fried oates, butter
& Camomiell chopt layed to her sides
the stitches removed, any the Cough
abated as to the extreamity thereof.
but then she was seized with a more
dangerous suemtome symptom , of a hard lump
contracted in her stomack that laid on
her heart; with great paine, & riseing
up to her throat allmost stoping her
breath when she either swalowed any
thing or laid to sleepe.
