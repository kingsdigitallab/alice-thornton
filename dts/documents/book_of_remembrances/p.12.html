When I was left at Richmond in under

the care & deare love of my beloved &

gracious Aunt Norton , upon my fathers

going to London . It pleased God to

bring me into a very great weaknesse

& sickenesse upon the accident of a surfit

on some ill dejested meate, causing an

extreame vomiting, whose violence drove

me into a feavor & the measells , which

brought me so low that my Aunt & Sara

Tomlinson , our maide , allmost despaired
