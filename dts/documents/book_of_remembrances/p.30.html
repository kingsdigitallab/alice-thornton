28 1657.

6 months &. 21 days. was

buried the same day at Catericke

Church by Mr Siddall .

I gotte a great fall over the

Threshold in the hall at Hipswell

beeing great with Childe of my

fifth Childe wanting but

tenn weeks . before of my time

the 14th of September: 1657

which cast me into an ill fitt

of a feaver, and the Jaundis

about 3 weeks very weake

likely to have miscaried, but

it pleased God to restore me

of Dr witty who lett me blood

& I went to my full time. Blesed be

the most high God Devsiser deviser of Heavn & Earth &
