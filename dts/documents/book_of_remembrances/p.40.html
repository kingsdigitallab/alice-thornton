38 .1659.

She was interred upon Tuesday

following the 13th of December in the Chiefest Cheifest

place plase in her choir quire at Catrick Church

being Carried out of her house by

the Lord Darcy , . his son sonne Mr Coniers , .

Sir Christ. vivill & divers kindred

of quality. then from hipswell Green

her Tenants Tennants took tooke her so to the

tower's tours End of Catrick where the minist

ers appointed apointed by her did carry carrie r her

into the grave Church, & so to the grave

after affter sermon. The ministers names

were : .

Mr Samois , . Mr Kearton Kirton , Mr Edrington

Mr Binlows , . Mr Robinson , Mr Smith ,

Mr Farrer , Mr Brockell , Mr Parke .

Dr Witty was her Physician Phisician but

could not come only sent Dr Atie with diretis diuretics

For her. she had also allso Dr Smith at the begining

of her sickness sicknesse . but liked him not:
