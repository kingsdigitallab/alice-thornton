48 1661.

I was faint & heavy Laden.
he said come unto me all the that Labor
& are heavy Laden. & I will give
you rest. Lord if thou art pleasd
to call all that are weary that Labor
why should not I come. thou hast
promised to give me rest, I need it
I want it, I cry unto thee, out of
the whales belly of dispaire, O god
the father of Heaven heave mercy
upon me. o Christ the Reedeemer
of the world have mercy upon me, &
O Holy blesed & glorious spirtt
have pitty upon me, O most glorious
Triniity Trinity , 3 persons & one God, helpe
thy poore destressed weake handm
-aid plunged in the depth of sine
& misery which non but thy Almighty
power is alble to helpe deliver &
save from the Jawes of the Devill .
