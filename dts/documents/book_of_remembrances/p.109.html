107 1667

About 15th he began begain to alter for

the better so that a change was discerned diserned .

17 he began to see again againe . 18th he hee perfec-tly

Recovered his sight, & recovered very

fast. for which I most humbly bless blesse &

glorify glorifie our gracious God with all my soul soule

and magnify magnifie his mercies mercys to me in the de-

-liverance of this my dear deare & sweet sweete

Child Childe from death & giving him as a bles-

-sing to his father & mother & not quen

-ching my cole in this Family. O praise

the Lord O: my soul soule & forget not this

his favour to thee. forever for ever Amen.

24th Robin first got up & on his feet feete

& was perfectly well uell growing daily daly strong

& was not disfigured at all with the smallpox Smal

Pox . only he never since recovered his

sweet sweete & beautiful beautifull favour & pure

colour couler in his cheeks. but his face drew

longer than then before. nor did his hair haire

come off at all having haveing but few in

his head but the most was in his face.

he wanted nine 9 Months, of being beeing fvie five

years yeares old & some odd odde days daies .
