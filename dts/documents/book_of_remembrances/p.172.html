And heartely crave. at thy most

gracious hand, with all things in order

to Etternity . both for our soules & bodies

even for the Lord, Christ Jesus his

sake, the righteous. to whom with the

holy Spiritt . the Etternal & Glorious

Father, Incomprehensable & ever

Blessed Trinity. Be ascribed all Hon.or

Glory. Power, might; majestie thank

-sgive, Praise, addoration & domminion

by all things in Heaven, & Earth.

of men & Angells, both now & to

all etternity for evermore.

Amen.
