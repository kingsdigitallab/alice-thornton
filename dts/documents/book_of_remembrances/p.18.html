It pleased God to give me a

safe passage with my mother and & her

family familie into Ireland about a bout the year yeir

1632 , : my father Father being beeing there before

and sent for us over. Where where I enjoyed injoyed

great happiness happinesse and & comfort during

my father's fathers life, and had the opportuni

ty of the best education that kingdom Kingdom

could afford in the sweet sweete and & excellent excelent

company of my Lord of Srafords Strafford's

daughters Daughters , . the most virtuous vertuous

Lady Anne Wentworth and & Arabella Arbella ,

learning those qualities with them

my father Father pleased to order me, as:
