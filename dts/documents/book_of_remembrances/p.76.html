74

I found that the often offten experience

of the failing of the things of this

life had brought our Estate into

an uncertain incertaine condition condittion & daily

our Estate was lessened leserned and those

monies momyes left me by my dear deare mother

was laid out for Mr Thorntons

use with which I could have helped

my daughters, to the sum soume of 300l

so being beeing gone gon . & the Estate of Burn

Park Parke sold sould and those monies momyes

disbursed for Debts, together with

my own owne 1000l Portion which was

settled by Articles. & bond before

marriage upon my self selfe & children

Also was to be set sett over for debts &

all to clear cleare Mr Thorntons Engagements Ingagments

which I was no noe way guilty of drawing

him into. these things did much
