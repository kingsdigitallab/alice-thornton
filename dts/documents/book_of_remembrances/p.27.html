I began my greate Sickenesse affter

I came from Barne Parke the first time

about the 6th of August 1652 & miscar-

-ried of my first Childe beeing a

daughter the 27th of the same August 1652

being friday. & she was buried at

Easby Church neare Rich. the next morn

ing: the efects of which sicknesse lasted by

an Ague. feaver, & Jaundies 3 qrters quarters of a

yeare: at hipswell .

1653 and Babtized the 4th of the same.

norton and Cozen Yorke his Daughter
