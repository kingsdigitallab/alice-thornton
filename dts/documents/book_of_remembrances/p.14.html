12 1631.

Beeing removed from Richmond
to London by my Fathers & mothers
order to be with them. I fell into the
Small Pox haveing taken them of
my brother Christopher, we were both
sent into Kent with Sarah to one
Mr Baxters house wher we were being
much beloved & taken caire for by them
And by the blessing of God I recovered
very soone, nor was I very ill at
that time in thim. I will praise the
Lord our God for my Preservation &
deliverance that did not suffer that disseas
to rage & indanger my life. but
Raised me soone to my Parents again
O lett me speake good of the name of
the Lord & magnifie his goodnesse for
my selfe & my Brother .
