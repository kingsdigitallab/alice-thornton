12 1631.

Being Beeing removed from Richmond

to London , by my father's Fathers and & mother's mothers

order to be with them , . I fell into the

smallpox Small Pox , having haveing taken them of

my brother, Christopher , . We we were both

sent into Kent with Sarah , to one

Mr Baxter's Baxters house, where wher we were being

much beloved and & taken care caire for by them.

And by the blessing of God, I recovered

very soon soone , nor was I very ill at

that time in thim them . I will praise the

Lord our God, for my preservation Preservation and &

deliverance, that did not suffer that disease disseas

to rage and & endanger indanger my life. but

raised Raised me soon soone to my parents Parents again.

Oh O , let lett me speak speake good of the name of

the Lord and & magnify magnifie his goodness goodnesse for

my self selfe and & my brother Brother .
