My Soul's Soules Wish.

Oh: Had I of his love but Part, That chosen was by Gods own owne heart, That Princely Prophet Prophett David he hee , Whom in the word of truth I see. The King of Heaven so dearly dearely loved love'd , . As mercy beyond measure proved prov'ed ; Then should I neither Giant Gyant fear feare , Nor Lyon that my Soul Soule would tear teare ; Nor as the Philistines Philistims , nor such friends: As never, were true Christians friends: No Noe passions should my Spirit Spiritt vex vexe , . Nor sorrow so my mind minde perplex. But I should still all glory give , . Unto my God by whom I live. And to the Glory of his Name, Throughout the world divulge the Same: My walk walke should be but in his ways wayse , My talk talke but only onely in his Praise.;
