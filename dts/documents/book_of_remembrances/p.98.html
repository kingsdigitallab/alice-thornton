96

While I am in this body bodie of death

I find by sad experience. that as my days daies

increase so will sin grow up too rife in

these corruptions. I have tasted of the great

& unlimited mercies mercys & clemency of the

Great God of Heaven , . the Lord most

High. And like wise been beine under manifold

trials trialls , . sorrows sorowes , troubles, & afflictions Aflictions

both as to spirit & body.

But as when the hand of God is upon us we

are apt to be more discomfited & sad , . so shod should

I, and do doe desire to make it my utmost uttmost

endeavour to inquire what is it that God

aims aimes at by all his providences & dealings.

I find my heart to apt to forget what a

low condition I was brought into by sickness sicknese

& Satan sathan , . either as to Mr Thorntos or my

own owne trials trialls . And peradventure hath not

rendered rendred him that due praise for all our exceeding Exceding

deliverances that he hath vouchsafed to me in

particular perticuler or him.
