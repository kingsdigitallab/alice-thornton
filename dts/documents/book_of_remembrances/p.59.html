there being beeing no witnesses wittnesses but God

& my own owne innocency Innocency , still I begging

of God to hinder & prevent any thing thng

that might hurt him at last my ever

gracious father in heaven in mercy

was pleased to with draw my husband husbond

passionate hand against him self selfe &

us, & to appease apease his anger by degrees

but this unhappy unhapie accident wrought

so infinitely infinitly with grief greeife upon my heart

That I fancied I saw the very blood

upon his pen knife, & had very near neare

gone gon to make me miscarry at that instant

but my merciful mercifull God did preserve

me at that very time from miscarriage miscariege

also allso , O what shall I render unto the Lord

for all his goodness goodnesse declared unto me

weak weake worm worme unworthy to live upon

the earth, or have such infinite infinit mercies mercys

poured upon me. he heard my groanings gronings

at this time also & delvred delivered my husband
