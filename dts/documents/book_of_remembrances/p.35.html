33 1659.

In this condition of weakness weakinesse

was my dear deare mother , almost allmost

quite without with out food, rest, ease

or sleep sleepe for about a bout a week weeke , in

which time, as in all her sickness sickenesse

she expressed extraordinary extraordenary great

patience, still saying saing the Lord had

sent it to her & none non could take it

from her. & if he pleased he could ease

her & that the way to heaven was by the

gates of hell, she was an example

and pattern patern of Piety, faith, &

patience in her greatest torment.

still with Godly instructions, gentle

rebukes for Sin, a continual contineuall pray

ing of Psalms Psalmes suitable for her

condition, speaking speakeing to God in his own owne

praise phraise & word, saying saing that we could

not speak speake to him from our selves
