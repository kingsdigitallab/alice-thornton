36 1659.

Ever recommending recomending her soul soule with de

-sire to be dissolved desolved & so be with Christ

And all the friday night before she died

Come Lord Jesus, come quickly quickely .

she making makeing Dafeny to pray with her

the prayer which Dr Smith made in his

book booke for a Person at the point to dye

& took tooke great notice of each Petition

praying praing the same with zeal Zeale and earnestness Earnest

-nesse .

About Thursday at night she sent

for her Children to take her leave

when Mr Th . & my self selfe came, &

prayed praied with her, & so took tooke the saddest sadesst

Leave of my Dear Deare Parent as ever

Child Childe could, to part with so great

a comfort, she praying for us, our

Children. & all her friends freinds with her

blessings for us. both.
