30 1658

1658 The weakenesse of my bodie continued

So great and long affter my hard Child

birth of my sonne . that it brought me

allmost into a consumtion . non expect

-ing for manye daies together that I

1658 should at all recover. & when it was don

I was lame allmost a quarter of a yeare of

my left knee, that I gott in my labour.

but this was nothing to that which I

have deserved from the hand of God if

he in much mercy had not spared my

life .The Lord make me truly remembre

his goodnesse & that I may never forgett

this above all his mighty & stretched

out hand of deliverances to me his

poore Creature. that I may extoll &

praise the Lord with all my soule & never

let goe my hope from the God of my

salvation but live the remainder of the

life he gives me to his hon.or honoured and Glory

& at the last may praise him eternally in the

heavens. blesse the Lord My soule & forget

not all his benefitts Amen. Amen.
