Taken away my bed from frm under me mee

& those quick goods my dear mo.

gave for my relief releife & children's childrens

these men were very boisterous boysterous , &

rude threatening threatning to take my Person

if not satisfied sattisfied . Lo Loe in what a

sad case was I then in. & but that it

pleased God to give me Relief Releife I

had not escaped scaped with my Life.

& by reason that I feared the neglect of

this business bussnes to fall out thus, it did

somewhat some what arm arme me with patience to

endure indure it better having haveing thoughts

of it before. thus have I upon every evry

occasion occassion & act of my life fresh cause

to speak speake good of the Lord, who though

he pleases to suffer men to afflict

me all manner maner of ways waies yet with the aff

liction he maketh a way to escape. &

does not permit more to be laid upon me
