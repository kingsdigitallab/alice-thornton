47 1661.

I laboured under & was heavy

Laden under with the burden of my Sines.

under the bandage of Satan,

The one bringing me to slavery. the

other of shame & torment, perswa-

-ding me time was past for any helpe

but Loe, the Lion of the Tribe of Juda

put to flight this Prince of darkness

which deceaves the poore weake Christian

Making him doubt of that rich goodnes

that is Eternally in God as his very

beeing, and at this very time Christ

Jesus the righteous came into my

thoughts with healing under his wings

makeing it a peare that satan was a

lier; he calles, he invites; he perswad

-es me to coame unto him: he it

was that give me this scripture to stay

my drooping heart even ready to

faint for want of spirituall food.

I was weary. & he said come.
