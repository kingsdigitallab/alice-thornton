21 1640, 1641.

It pleased God to call for my dear deare

& Hon.red honoured Father by a fever feavour of

six 6 days daies continuance contineuance after a relapse relaps

by going goeing to church & came sick home.

who departed sweetly in the Lord, to the

infinite infint loss losse of me & the rest of our family

Dec. December 3d 40 at his house in dublin

in Ireland .

It was Twas amongst the many 1000 of others

Protestants in Ireland miraculously pre-

-served from that Horrid Rebellion in Ireld Ireland

which broke out, & was discovered discovred in Dublin

Octb. 3d 1641 October 23rd 1641 : but it was acted with

bitter malice. & fury against the English

in the country for many days daies together

fire & sword to all in general generall . till it

was known to be prevented in Dublin .

but we were tossed tosed with frights & Alarms

for 14 days daies after till my mother did

get over with all her family to Weschester

which blessed be God in great & wonderful wonderfull

mercy. we did & came safe to Land at

Neston Beere house , . from thence to the

City of Chester
