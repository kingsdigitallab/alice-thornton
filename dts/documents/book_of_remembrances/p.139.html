137. 1668.

Night when word was brought from Dr

wittie that I should be of good cheare for I

should have my deare heart home as well

to me as ever I had him in my life. so that

I did shewe up my hopes in God & power

out my Teares & Prayers in a bundance that

night for the life, & health of deare Husbad

with me if it did stand good with the will &

pleasure of our God. & got some litte slumbers

though with feares & tremblings & sad & dis-

-mall dreames. when in the morning my bro.

-ther Denton came home. & very discreetly

prepared me with good advice & councell to

entertaine the Lords determinate will in all

things with patience & submittion if the worst

should fall on me according to my feares.

but withall God Could raise him up againe

if he see it fitt. although my deare â™¥

beeing fearfull to be deprived of this my sole

delight in this world next under my good

God.
