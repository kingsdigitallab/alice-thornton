It was the good pleasure of God
to contineue me in the Land of the
liveing, And to bring forth my 6th
Child at St Nickolas upon the 12th
1660. of Aprill & affter hard Labour
& hazardus was in his mercy
delivered of a strong goodly Sonn
about 3 or 4 a clock in the morning
being Tuesday: 12 April. 1660 Tuesday: 17 April. 1660 :

The Childe being baptized that
day by Mr Kirton of Richmond
called william.
