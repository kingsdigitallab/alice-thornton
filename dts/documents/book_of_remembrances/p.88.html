being beeing received receaved by & from the mercy

of a gracious father who hath

at length bestowed on me his

handmaid this hopes of the continuance contineuce

of my Memory: by a blessed son

of my womb wombe . having obtained obteined

him from god by the fervent prayers praires

& tears teares of a poor poore mother:

To thee o Lord I dedicate this

Child Childe the son sonn of my womb wombe .

Let him be ever in thy sight for

good. & establish stablish thy covenant

with him made to Abraham & the faithful faithfull

As thou thoue hast given him the sign signe of it

so most mighty . give him the true

grace. that he may be established

before thee both here heere & hereafter heereafter

That for Jesus Christ our Ever blessed blesed

saviour :& Redeemer Reedemer . Amen. Amen.
