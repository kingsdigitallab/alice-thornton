Prayer.

Lord, lead me through the

Red Sea of this world World ,

into Into the land Land of promise Promisse ,

forget Forget my many manie iniquities,

pardon Pardon my sins Sinnes , which stands standeth

as As a cloud between thy most

gracious Gratious goodness goodnesse and my.

most extreme extreame misery. For for

our Lord Jesus Christ his sake Sake .

Amen.
