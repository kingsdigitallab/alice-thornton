22 1641, 42, 43, 44.

I got the smallpox smale Pox at att weschester

of my brother John . & was very near nere

death with them. but blessed blesed be the God

of mercies mercys who spared spaired my life at that

time also allso . with my bro: brother John who had them

but a poor poore boy F. Kelly died of them. then.

we were prevented from the siege at

york yorke by Mr Danbys advice being beeing

got half halfe way thither, 42.

I got a surfeit surfit at Richmond , with

eating a piece peice of Lobster. that day I

had taken Physic Phisick , . which had like to

have proved my last , . being beeing brought

exceeding exceading weak through vomiting

1643. & purging. but by the blessing of God

upon Mr mahums advices & my mother

And good Aunt Nortons care caire I escaped

& yet lives to render him the glory &

praise of all his wonderful wonderfull deliverances deliverences

& mercies mercys . Bless Blesse the Lord o my Soul Soule

and all that is within thee praise

his holy name forever for ever . Amen.

my Sister Danby died at Thorpe Sept September 10th

1645 of her 16 child childe . being beeing a son named

Francis which I baptised baptized
