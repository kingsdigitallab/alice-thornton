And lo loe when I was yet scarcely scarsly recovered recovrd

of my weakness weaknesse from this grief greife & had

kept my bed 14 days daies . it pleased God

in his infinite infinit and abundant aboundant mercy &

goodness goodnesse to begin to restore that most

valued valewed Jewel Jewell by me. (& which was indeu'ud

to be wronged, (my good name). by the

coming comming of my dear deare Aunt over to

newton . who had heard the vile vilde reports

blazed blased abroad by mary Breaks & lies

against my Innocent Soul Soule this woman

full of deep deepe dissembling dissemblnig & hipocricey-

- who could not prevail prevaile with her design designe upon up

-pon the Person of Mr Comber to have drawn drawne

him for marriage. & failing of that end

did prevented pervanted her plausible plaucible carriage into

an inveterate malice & hatred , . both against

him & my self selfe , which having haveing discovered discovred that

it was Mr Combers desires to obtain obteine my.

Daughter Alice in marriage, & that he was

made use on to assist us in the Drawing of

settlements & writings for Mr Thorntons

Estate on my Children which I was had

good cause to see done don , in regard of Mr

Thornton & my own owne dieing dienig
