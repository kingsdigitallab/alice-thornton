152 1668
Nearest Relations vidz

My Brother Thomas Thornton. My Brother Denton. My Brother Portington. My Cosen Willm. William Ascough. My Cosen Ralphe Crathorne. My Cozen John Crathorne. My Cosen Bullock. My Cozen Ed. Lassells.

There was a very great congregation
as could be at that time he beeing most
generally beloved of his whole County
& man of Great Piety. Peace &
Honnesty. & great was the Lamentati
on for such an one in generall but
myne canot be rightly weighed for
any thing in this life. yet must I
submit for the Lords sake with patience &
submiteon & resignation. in hopes of a
Joyfull resurection at the last day then
to be united in praising God for ever.
