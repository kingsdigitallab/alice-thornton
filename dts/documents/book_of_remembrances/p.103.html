1666.

It pleased God to deliver my daughter

Alice from a very dangerous & violent

illness illnesse coming comeing on in her sleep sleepe .

when she lay in bed with me. who was

suddenly sudainly wakened wakend in a great fright with the

noise noyse of her who almost allmost choked choaked

& the breath stopped stopt with undigested undejested fish.

( Turbot Turbett ) that was not fresh she eat the night

before, but praised be the Lord upon my

help helpe , she did recover, & other things given givn

this eased her after vomiting. blessed be

the most gracious mercy of my God for

Ever that hath raised this child childe up from

death very often offten even from a young Child

being beeing often offten in sounds upon the breeding

of her teeth: this fit fitt was June 13th 1665

at Newton . when she was ill she was even
