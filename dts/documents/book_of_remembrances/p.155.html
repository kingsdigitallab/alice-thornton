153 1668.

My dearest heart was Interred Interrd

in the Alley of his own owne at his own owne

Parish Parich Church in Stongrave near neare

his own owne Mother & two sweet sweete

children Joyce & Christopher .

he was Buried on Friday the 18th of

September. 1668 between bytweene the hours houers

of four 4 & five 5 by Mr Thomas Comber

who Preached his Funeral Funerall sermon.

Whose Whoes Text was in Ecclesiastes Eclesiastese : 12th

the 1st verse verce . Remember now thy

Creator in the days daies of thy youth, while

the Evil Evill days daies come not, nor the years yers

draw nigh; when thou shalt say,

I have no pleasure in them.

Lord he hee loves thee the less lesse , that

loves any thing with thee, which he hee

loves not for thee.

(St Austin.)
