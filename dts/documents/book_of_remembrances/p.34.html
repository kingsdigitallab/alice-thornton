32 1659.

Which lump lumpe was conceived conseaved to

be contracted of Phlegm Phleame & wind,

in the Stomach Stomacke for lack lacke of voidance voydance .

: She had also an exceeding exiding sore

throat & mouth so that she was

deprived of the benefit benifitt to swallow

all most any kind of food. Even Eaven

to a little drop of beer beere , which was

for four 4 days daies or five 5 the most she

took tooke inwardly. & that but with

a searing seringe . The Tongue Toung & mouth

at first was black blacke , . then turned turnd

white, so that with the Pains Paines my dear deare

mother took tooke in washing & cleansing clens-

-ing the skin skinn came of like a calac callous

Tongue Toung , & was raw & red till the

blood came. but this continued contineued

and in the end grew with a white

skin skinn all over.
