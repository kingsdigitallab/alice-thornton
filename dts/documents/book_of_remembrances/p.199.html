Thus did the great & gracious fathr
of mercys deliver me by giving me
warning by a dreame which did prevnt

There fore will I blese & Praise the
Name of God forever who watched
over me in my sleepe to keepe me from
that Ruine. And I will praise the Lord
for Ever more. Amen
