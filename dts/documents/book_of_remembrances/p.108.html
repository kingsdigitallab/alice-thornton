So that he prepared for his dissolution desolution every

day & that morning he departed kneeled in

bed & most devoutly prayed praied to God &

commended his soul soule to his Creator; &

after affter commending his dearest love to

me & Mr Thornton , he drew shorter each

minute minet & so sweetly fell a sleep sleepe in the Lord.

he was buried in the Parish Church where

he died at Hodghdon . & Mr Comber was

by providence there who preached preacht a funeral funerall

sermon for the instruction of living livnig .

he died December 2d & was buried the 3d 1666

About this christmas 1666 my cousin Cosen

will.m William Norton was murdered inhumanely inhumamly

at London .

The 5th of January Janeuary 1667 my dear deare

Robin Thornton began his sickness sicknesse of the

smallpox Smale Pox being beeing very ill & weak weake for a

two 2 days daies & the 7, 8, 9 days daies he was in

great danger of death they not coming

well out till the tenth day. on which day he

lost his sight by them who was come out

very much & was great ones, on the 14th his

fever feaveor & disease dissease was at the height hight .
