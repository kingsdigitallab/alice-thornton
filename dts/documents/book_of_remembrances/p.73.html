And that my soul soule might forever

sing unto him hallelujahs Aleluiahs of praise

in heaven, with Angels Angells & Arch

Angels .& all the Host of Heaven

I here heere prostrate my Soul Soule before the

Throne of Grace .that hath showed shewed me his

poor poore weak weake handmaid shush such

hopes, such mercies mercys , & opportunities oportunitys

or making my calling caling & election ellection sure

And he will for his grace sake fulfil full

-fill in me the full Perfection of Glory

with him in heaven. to behold the holy

Trinity with Joy, & do doe his will

perfectly there. which I cannot by reason

of my many frailties frailtys & sins sines do doe in this

world, but weakly & imperfectly.

but o that I might be freed from this pour

of sin sine that it may not get domain dommin oiver over

me to dishonour the name of my God.

Amen.
