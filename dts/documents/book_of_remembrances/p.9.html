These things be comely & pleasant

to see , & worthy of honnor from the

beholders ; A young Saint . An old

martyr; A Religious Souldier ; A

Conscionable Statesman; Agreat man

Courtious; A learned man humble;

A silent woman; A Childe undersand

-ing the Eye of his Parent; A merrey

Compannion without vanity; A friend

not Changed with honnor; A Sicke man

Cheerefull : And a departing Soule with

Comfort and Assurance.
