vidz videlicet The great fall I had at Kirklington

when I was three 3 years yers old in following Sarah Sara my

maid maide when she had my brother brothr Christophr

in her Arms Armes . I fell upon the corner of the

hearth harth stone in the Passage chamber chambr going goeing into

my d. mother's mothrs Chamber Chambr . The wound so soe Deep Deepe

that my mother mothr saw the skin of the brain brane when

the scull was broken broaken & had gone gon nigh night to have

killed killd me. But ever blessed be the name of my

gracious & Powerful Powerfull God who whoe did not cut

my life short in this moment being beeing but a little

betwixt me & Death. Oh that I night might live to

praise the Lord most high & profitable to my

own owne soul soule Amen.

The Lord gave me a dream dreame before the dea

th of my dear deare & honoured honrd father to warn warne me

of his loss losse : & he did foretell the sorrows

& sad times coming comeing on these Kingdoms to my

Dear Deare mother & my self selfe :

My Dear Deare mother mothr had a Terrible dream

of the day of Judgement in 39

she had one also Allso not Long before my Lord

of Straford & the holy King suffered suffred which she

told tould me often of. & before the Scotch Rebellion Rebeln

& that of Ireland . most dreadful dreadfull to her.
