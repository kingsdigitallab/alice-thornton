Glory. or shall I recive corrections from the same
hand. and still be insencable there of God
forbid. why or wherefore he thus deales
with me.

It was his infinit goodnesse in giving me
this sweete Infant, in giving her the
blessed oportunity of holy Baptisme. and
is the mercy the lesse in that he has pleasd
to deprive me of its injoyment any longer,
or by the prevention of Actuall sin in her.
O no: I humbly acknowledge all these his
mercys .& give him the glory due to him therein
my sines was ripe for punishment. & he
pleased thus it should be. to teach me by
his Rod & staffe to aprove myselfe an
obedient child to so good. so great, so loving
a father.
