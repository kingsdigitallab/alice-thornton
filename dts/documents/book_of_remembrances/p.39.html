37 1659.

It pleased God she continued contineued

till saturday satterday about noon noone when

she spoke to my uncle norton & recommended re-

comended her Children to his care caire

with much good prayers for him & his

then took tooke her leave of him.

Towards six 6 o'clock a clocke at night her

speech speach failed & still she could lift

up her hands to God. And Dafeny

prayed praied her that she would give them some som

sign signe that she found the comfort of Gods

spirit in her soul soule as with a taste of the

Joys Joyes of heaven, which she immediately immeadiatly

did and lift up both hands & eyes eies to

heaven three 3 times. & closing closeing her Eyes

her self. that sweet Saint fell asleep

in the Lord. between betweene the hours houers of 6 seven 7 & 7 nine 9

o'clock aClock at night upon vpon saturday satterday the

10th of Decemb December . 1659
