but I was never in all my Life nearer
death by forcing the Child so violently
by the midwife inso much as the neighbors
did feare I should bin devided from the
waste downewards & the Rest of all my
bodie. which caused inexpressable torment
& a dislocation of the Reines of the back
for a long time. & excessive paine in the
spleene & mother arrising both together
upon me in my Childe bed.
