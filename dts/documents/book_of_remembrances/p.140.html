138 1668.

So betwixt hopes & feares I rested

till the next messenger came which was

about 4 a clocke on Thursday in the

after noone. at which time I receavd the

newes of the most Terrible losse that ever

woman losst in beeing deprived of my

Sweete & most exceeding deare Husbad

that any creature could have. Such was

my extreamity of Passion & trouble

upon this change that I was allmost

changed into nothing & was ready

to goe into the grave with him whom God
