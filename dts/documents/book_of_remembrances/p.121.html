After which recovery of my health
I was very much in affliction about
my deare husbands illnesse & offten
relapses into his former Palaticke
fitts. which fell on him notwithstanding

used by Dr witties order, & with good
successe. so that from ye November 67 till
August 68 he had not missed one fort-
-night from a relapse or the degrees of
