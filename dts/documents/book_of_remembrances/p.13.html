11 1631

But it pleased the Lord in great

mercy, upon the hearty prayers Prayers and &

requests of my dear deare aunt Aunt , . that

I was spared spaired and & , upon the use of good

means meanes by his blessing there on , . I

recovered my health perfectly again.

Oh, that I may have my life

given me for a blessing and & that I may

live to the praise Praise of his holy name:

growing in grace Grace and & the knowledge of

our Lord Jesus Christ , being beeing a comfort comfot

to my dear deare parents Parents and & relations.

And & that I may dedicate my childhood Childhood , .

youth , . middle age Age , . and & old age Adge , . if

God shall spare spaire me so long, to his

glory Glory and & praise even to my life's lives end,

amen Amen . And & that for my saviour's Saviours sake Sake

alone , . the alpha Alpha and & omega Omega . Amen.
