27 1656

My brother. Richard Thornton

died in Dublin , in Ireland of the flux

country disease desease the 3d of July

1656. & buried in St Patrick's Patricks

Church the next day.

Katherine Thornton my fourth

Child Childe was born borne at Hipswell the

12th of June, 1656. being thursday

about half halfe an hour houer after affter four 4

o'clock a clocke in the afternoon affter noone , and was

baptised babtized the 1th of June. by Mr

Syddall Siddall . witnesses wittnesses , my mother ,

my Niece Neece K Danby , & Mr Thornton

Elizabeth Thornton my third 3d child

died the: 5th of September 1656.

betwixt the hours of five 5 and six 6 in the

morning, of a cough gotten at first

by an Ague. & much gone in the

Rickets Riketts caused by ill suck sucke at

two 2 nurses. her age was one 1 year yeare
