79 1662

Almighty God the wise disposer

of all good things both of heaven

& earth , . who sees what & how much

of the comforts of this mortal mortall life is

fit for us to enjoy injoy in this Earth , . did

at last give me such a mercy & dear deare

comfort after affter all the several severall afflictions aflictions

I had passed: As I no noe ways waies could hope

for or expect. making makeing me a Joyful Joyfull

mother of a sweet sweete Son . born borne at

full ful time. though with hard travail travill

& great peril perill to my life.

I Was delivered of my Son Sonne Robert

Thornton upon Friday the 19th of Sept

-ember 1662 born borne at Easte Newton

my husbands Chief Cheife house wher being

the first child that ever I bore at his

house. he was born borne betwixt the hours

of eight 8 & nine 9 o'clock a clocke at night having

been beine in Labour since the night before

till that time. he was my seventh

Child Childe .
