incomprehensibly incomprehensably dear deare & loving

father in Heaven. begging that his will

might be my will in life & death.

when he was about 14 days daies old my

pretty Child Childe broke broake out in little red

spots spotts like smallpox smale Pox which was the red

gum & upon Cold gotten through an

accident they stroke inwardly. & he fell

into a great looseness loosenesse , notwithstanding

all the means meanes I could use, & it continued contined

on him for four 4 days daies . when he was very

patient & quitt quiet . & being beeing a very strong

Child indured it with much struggling strugling but

at Length it pleased his saviour &

mine to deliver him after a sick sicke night

& day out of that misery, & he sweetly fell

asleep a sleepe on sunday at night about six 6 . or

seven 7 o'clock a clocke at night being beeing then the first of

December 1667 being beeing three 3 weeks old upon the

2d of december. he was Buried at Stongrave

on the 2d December decemb . by Mr Comber . who did

preach his funeral funerall Sermon. December 2d

1667.
