26 1654.
was Baptized the 16th of February by
Mr Antony. wittneses, my Mother
my Aunt Norton and my brother
Christopher wandesforde, Mrs Blacke
burne stood for my Mother being sicke
then.

1655.

my Mother Gates died at
Oswoldekirke of the voydance of
blood the 10th day of may. 1655.
& was buried at Stanegrave
the 11th of may 1655.

my father Gates died at Hull
the 18th of may 1655. and was
buried at hull the 18th of may
1655
