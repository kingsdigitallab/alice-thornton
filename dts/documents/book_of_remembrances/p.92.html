My sweet Sweet child Childe , Joyce , which was

my Eith eighth Eight child childe , was born borne on

Saturday Satterday about a bout four 4 o'clock a clocke in the

afternoon after noone . on September the 23 th rd

1665 , and she was baptised baptized on the

28th day . :

Her godfather Godfather was my Lord Frescheville Frechail ,

by Mr Comber who stood for him , .

Mr Rich. Richard Legard Ledgard , . Mrs Graham

& Mrs Cholmely .

It pleased my gracious Father still

to give me much comfort in the nursing

of this child childe , having received receaved more

strength to enable inable me for that service.

She she growing strong & thriving thriveing well,

through the blessing of God, upon my

endeavours endevours , to perform performe this duty;

therefore, do doe I praise the great God

of my life Life . for this temporal temporall mercy,

also for all good comes from him.
