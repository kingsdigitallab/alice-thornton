120 1668.

About the 20th of July 1668 . I had

a very great & dangerous sickness sickenesse

fell upon me in being beeing my perfect health

& strength. upon the occasion of a sudden sudain

grief greife & terror that I was seized seised upon

in my neece kitt Danbys chamber at

Newton when her maid maide Barbara Tod .

did impudently accuse me to be fore my

face of my Servant Hanna

Ableson for telling her from one Mary

Breakes of several severall stories storyes (which were

very great) lies & falsehoods fallshoods against

my self selfe , of such a nature as did

much unbecome any to hear heare & not

to have ou acquanited acquainted me with at the

first. which my maid maide did utterly deny

& cleared her self selfe & me upon her

oath, but the other woman having a

spleen spleene against her. did carry carrie so unhandsomely un

-hansomly & unchristianly towards me

in her bit ter aggravation agravation & in false

accusing the Hon.or honour of some of the Persons
