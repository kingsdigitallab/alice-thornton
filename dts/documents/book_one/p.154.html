About this August after our return returne from Scarbrough

it pleased God to give me much strength & health,

so soe that I conceived conceaved with Child Childe ; which after affter Mr Thornton

perceived, he with my mother greatly rejoiced rejoyced ; hoping hope-

-ing that I might at length be blessed with a son.

For 4 months monthes together, I enjoyed injoyed a great deal deale of

comfort & health, being beeing much stronger & lively

when I was with my Sons than then Daughters, having haveing

great cause to admire the goodness goodnesse of God, which even

contrary unto hope caused me to recover of that Sad

distemper, wherewith I was afflicted, and giving giveing

me hopes to bring forth a Son to be a Comfort to my

dear deare husband & us all.

A thanksgiving thanksgiveing after affter affter my recovery.

truth in the congregation of the saints. And now thou
