His life was spent in great Sedulity & watchfulness watch

-fullnesse , to discharge, a good conscience towards all

, towards God, & man. A true Labourer in Gods

Vineyard, in which he played plaied the good husbandman

And God at att last gave him his wages, even

the Crown Crowne of Glory for ever.

And guiding his ways waies with discretion discrestion of God gave

him a foresight of those changes was coming comming

upon uppon Church & State. And oft offt in my hearing

would he say to my dear deare Mother , in his health

, that, who whoe so soe should live to see it; should see

great changes, & Evils Evills , both upon uppon the Church

& State. Such was the Sins Sinnes & Pride of those dais

that there hung a cloud over this Kingdoms Kingdomes .

he prayed praied God to divert the same. and Establish

the same upon uppon those Excellent Excelent foundations on

which it was built.
