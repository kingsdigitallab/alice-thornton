My Mothers going goeing into Ireland 1632.

It pleased God to give my dear deare Mother , my two

younger brothers & my self selfe a safe passage into

Ireland , about the year yeare . 1632. My Father being beeing

there a year yeare before, & my Eldest brother George

In which place I enjoyed injoyed great happiness happienesse and

Comfort during dureing my hon.red honoured fathers life having haveing the

fortunate opportunity oppertunity in that time, & after affter , when

I stayed staied there, of the best education that Kingdom Kingdome

could afford. having haveing the advantage of Society Societie in the

sweet sweete & chaste company of the Earle of Straffords Daughters

The most virtuous Lady Anne . & The Lady Arbella

Wentworth . Learning those qualities with them which

my father ordered. Namely Namlie ,

The french Language. to write, & speak speake the same;

Singing. Dancing. Playing Plaieng on the Lute, & theorbo Theorboe .

learning such other accomplishments, of Working

Silks Silkes . gum work gummeworke . & Sweetmeats Sweetemeats & other

suitable sutable housewifery huswifery . As, by my mothers virtuous vertuous

provision provission , & care caire , she brought me up in what was

fit fitt for her quality qualitie , & my fathers Child Childe .

But above all things I accounted it my Chiefest Cheifest

happiness happinesse where in I was trained in those Pious Pieous
