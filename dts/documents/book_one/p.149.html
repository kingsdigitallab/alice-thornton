Upon Uppon my great fall I had, being beeing with child childe of my fifth 5th

September 14th 1657 at Hipswell .

I fell with child childe after affter my deare bettys death, having haveinge

my health very well, after affter quick quicke child childe & so soe continued

till I got gott a great fall over the threshold in the hall, at att Hips-

well , being beeing then great with child childe of my fifth , wanting but

Ten weeks before my time. on the 14th of september 1657

which fall cast me into an ill fit fitt of a fever feaver , and the Jaundice Jaundies

followed, & about a bout three 3 weeks was very weak weake , & in great

danger of death, & miscarriage. with the continual contineuall pains paines

& excessive motions of the child childe in me, which was turned wrong

in my womb wombe , by the fall, & might easily be discerned disearned , nor

could it be able to turn turne the head aright, although all though it kept

continual continuall bending its back backe & body bodie for it. so soe that Dr. witty

was sent for, & used all his skill in my preservation; and by

the blessing blesing of God upon the means meanes . I was delivered from

Death, and marvellously marvelously restored to strength at att 6 weeks

end; I was let lett blood by the Dr with other remedies remidies which made

me go goe to my full time. O what shall I render to the Lord

most high which had compassion on his weak weake handmaid handmaide .

Lord I am not worthy to live much less lesse to receive receave all these

miraculous mercies mercys from thy goodness goodnesse for which I adore thy name

for ever Amen.
