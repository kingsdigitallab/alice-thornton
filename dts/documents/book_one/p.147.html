Elizabeth Thorntons death; the 5th of September 1656.

It pleased God to take from me, my deare childe betty

which had been bin long in the rickets riketts & consumption gotten at

first by an Ague & much gone in the Rickets Ricketts which I concsed

was caused by ill milk milke at two 2 nurses, And did notwithstand-

-ing all the means meanes I used & had her with Naly at St Mun

gnos well . for it she grew weaker & at att the last in a most

desperate Cough that destroyed her lunges, she died.

Elisabeth Thornton , my third 3d Child Childe died the 5th of september

1656. betwixt the hours houers of five 5 . & six 6 . in the morning. her Age was

one year yeare six 6 . months & 21 days daies . was buried the same day at

Catterick by Mr Siddall .
