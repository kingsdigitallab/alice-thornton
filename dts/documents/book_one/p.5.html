Alice Wandesforde the fifth child childe of

Christopher Wandesforde Esquire late

Lord Deputy of Ireland. Was born borne at

Kirklington in the County of Yorke

the thirteenth day of February being beeing on a

monday munday , about a bout two o'clock of the clock in the

afternoon afternoone in the year yeare 1626. Baptised Baptized the

next day. The witnesses wittnesses were Mr

Lassells Minister of Kirklington .

Mrs Anne Norton . & Mrs Best .
