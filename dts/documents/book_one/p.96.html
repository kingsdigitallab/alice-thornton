My Cosen Julian Horton died at Richmond greene

at her fathers the 9 of April Aprill . 1649.

Upon Uppon the Death of my brother George Wandesforde .

March 31 1651 & of his Sequestration. &

other troubles, after affter his return returne into England .

The fatal fatall blow given to my Fathers Family Familie , by the

death of our excellent excelent brother , was very great, but the

effects thereof fell out most heavy upon my self selfe , in

the sad loss losse of so soe dear deare & loving brother . nay I may

say a father to us all.

He was a Gentleman exceedingly excedingly qualified, with suitable suttable

endowments indowments both natural naturall & acquired. giving giveing himself him-

selfe over in the qualifications for the service of his God

his Church. his King & country countery , and such as rendered rendred

him much beloved. & lamented at home & abroad.

for the great loss losse & sad conclusion of so soe brave a Person

Yet enjoyed injoyed he in his time, after affter my fathers death not

much comfort, for since his reture return out of France , in the

Public Publicke calamities of Church & State. he was driven to

many straits & hardships.
