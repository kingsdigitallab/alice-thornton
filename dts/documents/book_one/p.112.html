But on the contrary as we have hitherto, bin preserved

from such iniquities, by thy mercy. soe through thy

contineued Grace, we may be delivered from either doeing

such; & if it be thy will from suffering by & from such practi

-cies, as much as thou shalt see fitt in thy gracious prov

-idences. That soe we may still, live in this good Land,

injoying once againe thy peace, in the Restoration

of our Right Lawfull Soveraine. with the restoration of thy

Church, & holy good Bishops to feed & governe, & direct

this flocke, aright in the waies of thy truth; & salvation

of our Soules. That Errours, Schismes, & Rebellion, may be

extingushed, & exterp-ated. thy good Lawes Devine

& humaine, Reestablished. And we of these Kingdoms

Fixed upon those Axes againe, of truth, & Peace.

Nations, in true filliall feare. may againe injoy thy
