At this time did my deare Mother & whole family

receave grand preservation from the devine Providence

in delivering us from the Arrow that flieth by day, when

as 100ds hundreds died so neare us, & thousands, fell at noone

day, nay all that towne was allmost depopulated. how

did our good & great Lord preserve all us at Hipswell

, soe that noe infection seised upon any one that belonged us.

allthough the malice of the beggers was great to have

don harme by raggs, notwithstanding all her Charitable

Releife daily, with much meate, & monny. Blessed by the

great & ever mercifull father, who did not deliver

us up to this heavy judgment, of the Lord but did reb

in Richmond .
