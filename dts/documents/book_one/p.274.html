He departed on Thursday the 17th of September 1668 .

betwixt the hours howers of 11 & 12 at att noon noone . he being beeing on the 2d

day of June (68) forty fortey four fouer years yeares of Age. we having have

ing lived a dear deare & loving couple in holy marriage almost

seventeen seaventeene years yeares .

My dear deare husbands body bodie was brought home to Easte

Newton on friday the 18th of september in company of many of

our neighbours Gentry Gentrey , & other friends freinds those that were weare about

us, And I did desire that his interment might be deferred deferrd

till we could acquaint our remote friends freinds & relations. But

the Dr told tould them that he had laid not long sick sicke & taking

of Physic Phisicke would hinder that. Those that were helpers to bear beare

his Corpse Corpes were of his kindred & relations.

My Brother Thomas Thornton , my Brother Denton .

My brother Portington . my Cosen will.m William Ascough .

My Cosen Ralphe Crathorne . my Cosen John Craithorne .

My Cosen Bullocke . My Cosen Ed. Lassells .

There was a very great Congregation at that time he being beeing

most generally beloved of his Country Countrey . A man of great

Piety Pietie , Peace. Honesty. There was a great lamentation

for him God having haveing given givn him much love & affection.

But my sorrows sorrowes & laments cannot be weighed for him which

parted with the great & sole delight & comfort I esteemed esteed of my

Life. the Lord grant me some measure of Patience to sustain sustaine

that I may not displease the great Governor Governer of heaven heavn & Earth

but desire to submit for the Lord sake with resignation to his will

& in hopes of a Joyful Joyfull Resurrection Resurection , at the last day, then to

be united in hallelujahs haleluiahs to the God most High. for ever.

My dearest dearst heart was interred in his own owne Alley at stongrave

Church near neare his mother & two sweet sweete babes. Christofer

& Joyce . buried on friday the 18th of september between betweene four 4

& five 5 . o'clock a clocke . by Mr Thomas Comber . who Preached his

Funeral Funerall Sermon. The Text was in Ecclesiastes 12th v. 1st

Remember now thy Creator in the days daies of thy youth: &c.

applying applieing it fitly fittly to that: occasion.

Lord he hee loves thee the less lesse . that loves any thing with

thee, which he loves not for thee. (St Austin).
