The effects of this fever feaver remained by several severall distempers

successively. first after affter the Miscarriage. I fell into a most

terrible tirrable shaking shakeing Ague lasting one quarter of a year yeare . by fits fitts

each day twice in much violency, so that the sweats was great

with faintings. being beeing thereby weakened till I could not stand or go goe .

After the Ague left me upon a medicine medicin of London Treacle

I fell into the Jaundice Jandies , which used me very hardly one full

quarter & a half halfe more. I finding Dr witties judgment true that

it would prove a chronical cronnicall distemper. but blessed be the

Lord upon great & many means meanes used & all remedies remidies . I

was at length cured of all distempers & weaknesses which

from its beginning begining had lasted three 3 quarter of a year yeare full out.

Thus I had a sad entertainment & beginning begining of my Change

of life the comforts thereof being beeing turned into much dis-

comforts & weaknesses. but still I was upheld by an almighty All-

mighty Power. therefore will I praise the Lord my God. Amen.
