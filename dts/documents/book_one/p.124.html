Alice Wandesforde the Daughter of Christopher

Wandesford Esquire late Lord Deputy of Ireland , was

married to william Thornton Esquire , of Easte Nuton .

at att my Mothers house in Hipswell . by Mr Siddall

December the 15th 1651.

Mr Siddall made a most Pious Pieous & profitable exhortati

-on to us, showing shewing our duties, & teaching us the fear feare of

the Lord in this our new Estate of life, with many Zealous

prayers for us. My dear deare & hon.red honoured Mother gave me

in marriage, in the Presence of my owne brother John

Wand. my uncle Norton . My uncle Darley (Frances

my cozen Dodsworth of wattlosse . George Lightfoote

& dafeny . Robert webster . Martha Richison . Ralfe Janson

Robert Loftus the Ellder .

A Relation of the Remarkable passages of my Life

since my Marriage beginning begining from the 15th of December Decem . 1651.

1651.

After my Marriage it was my duty to humble my

soul soule in prayer praier & supplication suplication to the God of all the Earth

who had guided me in all my youth & Virgin Estate

to live in his fear feare & service. & directing direiting me to choose chuse

a Godly & religious husband with whom I might through

his blessing spend the rest of my days daies . & to this purpose

I poured powred out my humble petitions pettitions & requests requists with hearty

thanks & praise for all my deliverances both spiritual spirituall

& temporal temporall ever since I was born borne to that Present hour houre

beseeching him, that as he in abundant mercy had heard

my unworthy requests in the beginning begining of this treaty. so soe

he would now Multiply his free grace & loving kindness kind

and myself myselfe all those graces & spiritual spirituall comforts we
