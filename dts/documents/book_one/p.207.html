After this great mercy; in the renewing reneuing of our vows vowes &

Covenants with God: I was fully satisfied sattisfied in that condition condittion

whether for Life; or Death: having haveing committed my Soul Soule in

keeping keepeing to a faithful faithfull mediator meadiator : & Redeemer: hoping hopeing

for me to live, is Christ. & to die was gain gaine : when I should

exchange, sorrow, for Joy: & death for Life. & immortality immortallity

I was the most concerned conserned for my poor poore Children. who whoe might

peradventure want some helps helpes from their theire weak weake mother

& having haveing no noe Relation; or friend freind of my own owne ; that might take

care caire of them if their theire father should see cause to marry again a-

gaine , according as I had been bin told tould : that it would be Necessary Nesseary

for him for his health: I was indeed the more solicitous scolicitous for

my three 3 young Children Chidren ; casting in my mind what friend freind of

my own owne to desire to in trust with their theire Education if he did so soe

For my Son: The hope of my house: I humbly humblie committed him

into the Propection of Almighty God. as also allso his two sisters

And for his Education, into the care caire of my dear deare & honred honoured Uncle

My Lord frechvile : my Daughter Alice to: my dear deare Aunt

Norton : and my Daughter Katherine : to my deare neece

Best : with strict charge to bring them up in the nurture & fear feare

ledge through the due examination: & catechising of Mr

Next in order was It my duty to take care caire for the right

Settlement: & division devission of the goods & Personal Personall Estate of my

Dear Deare Mother according to her will & Testament which she left

me power to do doe by Deed amongst my Children.

Having Haveing left their theire Maintenance & Portions settled as by the

Deed Deede made formerly. in as good a condition condittion as could be for

So Soe that it only remained for me to deposit depositt the safe custody of
