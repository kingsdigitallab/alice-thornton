Fulfilling Fullfilling thy will here heere upon Earth, a few days daies

which thou hast appointed for us. to live. that at the last

wee may live with thee for ever.

And Lastly O Lord most holy & gracious I yield yeald

thee all humble & hearty thanks and praise.

for all those favours & mercies mercys . graces & gifts. with

the Blessings of this Life. & those spiritual spirituall . together

with the happy happie opportunities thou gavest to my

dear deare father . to serve thee in his generations. by

A happy happie success successe in all his weighty Callings

The good Providence. & prudence by which he added

more improvements to his Estate than then his progenitors Progenators

for his Justice, uprightness uprightnesse . mercifulness mercifullnesse , Charity

to his Relations & strangers straingers . in general generall .

for the favour & good will thou gavest him in his

own owne & a strange country countrey . And for all the good wher

ever thou enabled inabled him to do doe , either for our Souls Soules

or Bodies. These blessings, I humbly acknowledge

did alone proceed from thy divine devine goodness goodnesse from

whence comes every good & perfect gift guift .

Therefore O Lord I beseech accept my gratitude, & praise

for all. And as thy mercy has been bin so soe plentifully

bestowed to this family. add adde this one thing more to

us. That all thy dispensations may be sanctified to us

In giving giveing us grace to live accordingly. & may be a

holy people. as the redeemed of the Lord.
