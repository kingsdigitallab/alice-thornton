The birth of my Sonne Christopher , my ninth 9th Child Childe

no. 11 1667 & of his Death. Dec. 1st 1667.

Of my ninth 9th Child Childe ; it was the pleasure of God to give

me a weak weake & sickly sickely time in breeding from the February

till the 10th of may following. I not having haveing fully recruited recruted

my last Sept. weakness weakenesse ; & if it had been bin good in the Eyes

of my God. I should much rather (because of that) not to

have been bin in this condition condittion : but it is not a Christians

part. to choose chuse any thing of this nature. but what shall be the

will of our heavenly father, be it never so soe contrary

to our own owne desires. Therefore did I desire to submit

in this dispensation. & depend upon uppon his providence

for the preservation of my Life, who had delivered me

in all my extremities extreamities . & Afflictions.

I had not my health, till about the 10th of May. when I

perceived perceaved my self selfe with quick quicke Child Childe . afterwards affterwards during

the time of being beeing with Child Childe . till within a month of my de

-livery very well as of any other. walking walkeing a mile

to the Church each Sabbath Sabath day. I humbly bless blesse the God

of my Life & strength. that restored so soe much health &

strength again againe to his handmaid handmaide . Giving Giveing me great

comfort in my dear deare & beloved husband . who all this

summer & spring was so soe well & strong as he never had

been bin since his first ill fit fitt at Steersby . The Lord giving giveing

a blessing to those means meanes appointed by Dr Wittie wittie for his

preservation. which order we observed; till towards Sept.

following. But when I grew so soe big bigge & ill near neare my

delivery about Michaelmas Micklemas he intermitted those rules of di-

rections wanting my assistance about his Person to

in my Illness Illnes . he relapsed several severall times. And had the Dr

sent for to him very often offten . by which helps helpes through Gods

Blessing was restored to me again againe . Praised be our good.

& gracious Lord God for Ever. Amen.
