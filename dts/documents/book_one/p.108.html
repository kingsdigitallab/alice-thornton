A Lamentation, & Prayer, uppon the death of

my honoured brother George Wandesf. Esquire .

Alas O Lord, most great, & mighty, wonderfull in

thy Powerfull Attributes, & Judgements. what shall

I say or, doe unto thy glorious majestie. who hast

looked downe uppon us, with a mighty breach. adding

great sorrowes to our Publicke calamitys. Thou hast a

controvercy with this whole nation, & allso with this poore

Familie, by takeing away our brother by an unnatarall

death, when he was in hopes to have lived in peace and
