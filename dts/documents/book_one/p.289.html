A great deliverance from a second 2d fall

at att Sr Robert Merideths in Dublin

in Ireland 1637.

My Lady Anne Wentworth . & Lady Arbella with

Cozen Anne Hutton , Mrs Anne Loftus & my self selfe

being beeing invited to dinner to Sir Robt. merideths to dine

The ladies using ussing the custom costome to swing by the Arms Armes

for recreation & being good to exercise exercize the body

of Children Chilldren in growing, it was ordered by my Lady

Straford they should do doe it moderately moderatly & found good in

it: so soe that they used to swing each other gently to that

Purpose. They would make me being beeing a young girl girle

do doe the same with them & I did so soe & could hold very

well by the Arms Armes as they did & had never got gott no noe hurt

by it. I bless blesse god but found it did me good &

Butt at att this time very unfortunately some of the

young Ladies bid one of the Pages ( Called Calld don de Lan .)

a french boy That he should swing me being stronger

than then they & they weary with Play. Butt I cried cryed out

desiring them not to bid him but could not get gett off so soe

soon soone from him & deliver my self selfe from danger before befor

he had came to me. He immediately immeadiatly Pushed

me so soe violently from him with all his force as I was

swinging by my Arms Armes . That I was not able to hold

my hands on the swing. So Soe that he throwed me down

me downe upon the chamber Boards Bords . I fell down downe upon

my face fell to the ground grownd & light with such a violent

force with all my weight on my Chin Chinn Bone upward uppward
