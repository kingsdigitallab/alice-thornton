About this Christmas 1666 my Cozen william Norton

was inhumanly inhumainly murdered at London neare Grays Inn .

My Son Roberts having haveing the smallpox smale Pox . Jan. 5 1667

and of his recovery perfectly again againe .

The 5th of Jan. my deare Robin Thorntons began his sickness sicknes

of the smallpox smale Pox being beeing very ill & weak weake for two 2 days daies . the

7, 8, 9th he was in great danger of death. they not coming come

-ing well out till the 10th on which day he lost his sight by

them being very great ones & full.

About the 14th his fever feavor & disease dissease was at height hight . & on the

15th he altered for the better. so soe that a change was discerned

the 17th he began to see again againe . the 18 had his sight clearly

recovered. after affter which he recovered very fast. for which I

most humbly bless blesse God. with all my soul soule . & magnify magnifie

his mercies mercys to me in the deliverance of this my dear deare & sweet

Child Childe , in not quenching our cole in this Family. O Praise

the Lord o my soul soule & forget forgett not all his benefits benifitts . this his

favour to thee for ever. Amen. The 24th Robin first

got gott up. & was perfectly well. growing groweing strong & was not

so soe much disfigured As his sister Kate . But he never

recovered his sweet sweete beautiful beautifull favour & pure colour couler in

his cheeks. but his face grew longish. his hair haire did not fall

of. he wanted nine 9 months of being beeing five 5 years yeares old & some dais

Hanna Ableson & margery millbanke kept him in his

sickness sicknesse .

It Pleased God, after affter my sonne Robert was well. to

visit vissitt my daughter Alice Thornton . which began in her

sleepe with another annother dangerous fit fitt in her sleep sleepe . which much

awakned frighted me. she being beeing almost choked choaked by the

Phlegm Phlegme . making makeing her exceeding exceding sick sicke . before she vomited vomitt

up some ill digested meat meate & with extremity extreamity she was

black blacke in her face. but by the mercies mercys of our Gracious God

after affter she vomited vomitted . she did recover. though it kept her

weak weake . I will praise the Lord our God of our salvation for this

great deliverance of my Child Childe . Amen.
