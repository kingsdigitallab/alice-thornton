I humbly humblie beseech thy Majesty Majestie as thou hast given me this

being beeing . in this Earth & continuing contineuing the same thus long. &

hast prolonged my Life, notwithstanding all my unworthiness unworthines

& sins. That thou wilt willt pardon what ever I have offended

in thy sight ever since I was born borne . pity pittie my infirmities infirmitties &

strengthen my weakness weaknesse , & humaine frailties, heal heale my

Soul Soule & love me freely make me to understand thy word

& know thee to be a God hearing Prayer Praier , & wilt willt dwell in the

heart of those that are holy holie & humble, of a contrite heart. &

Trembles at att thy word. thy, Power, & Attributes. Give me

grace to Glorify Glorifie thy name & submit submitt cheerfully chearfully to this

heavy dispensation to thy handmaid handmaide . serving thee, not

with a slavish slaveish fear feare . but out of a true obediential obedienciall &

filial filliall duty. Love & affection, knowing that thou dost not

afflict willingly. but even in this separation seperation did it (I hope)

for the happiness happienesse of my husband , by freeing him from troubles

And that thou wilt willt thereby draw drawe me nearer to thy thy self selfe

by setting my affections more on heaven above & not on Earth;

I will also give thee the Glory & praise which hast kept me from

Presumptuous Presumtious sins, giving giveing me a tender heart, (I hope) for the

least offence against thee, That may endanger indanger a separation seperation
