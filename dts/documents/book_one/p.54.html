A Prayer to be said before wee Receive.



Made by my father . before he went into.

Ireland 1628.

O Eternall. Omnipotent, & most Mercifull Father

thou that openest, & no man shutteth, & shuttest

and no man openeth, lett the Wordes of our mouthes

& the Meditations of our hearts. be acceptable in thy

sight this time and for ever more.

O Lord God, by whome wee were created at the

first, by whome wee now live move & have our

being, Looke not upon us as wee were placed at the

first, by thy divine Power, in the State of Per-

-fection, adorned with those Heavenlie qualities

of knowledge & freedome of Will, whereby wee

had some remsemblance of thee our Great Creator.

Neither as wee lye in the loynes of our frist Parents

whoe, by eating that sower grape hath sett an Edge

upon our teeth. Much Lesse as wee are defiled with

our owne Pollutions. & uncleannes. But behold us

we beseech thee, in that State which thy divine Pro-

-vidence, through the Redemption of thy Sonne & our

Savioure Christ Jesus hath prepared for our Resto-

-ration, unto that happiness from whence we are fallen

that so wee whoe were altogether dead of our selves

, may by him be recovered unto that Everlasting Re-

-surection, which thou (that wishest the Perfection & continu

ance of what thou hast made). hast prepared for us before

the begining of the world, If wee feare thee & keepe.
