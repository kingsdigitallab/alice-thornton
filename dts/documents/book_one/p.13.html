Teachings. Reproofs Reproofes , & Godly Education, tending to

the welfare wellfaire , & Eternal Etternall happiness happinesse , & salvation

of my poor poore Soul Soule . Which I received receaved from both my Hon.rd honoured

Father & Mother . with the Examples of their theire Chaste Chast

and sober, wise & Prudent Conversations in all

things of this World. for which things, and infinitely infinit

-ly more opportunities opportunitys of Good to my wellbeing well beeing

than then I can express expresse . I Render my utmost uttmost Capacity

& Therefore do doe I most humbly, & heartily acknowledge acknowledg

my bounden duty of thanks thankes & praise to the great

God of Heaven, & Earth, from whence comes every

good & perfect gift guift , who is the Author, & finisher

of our faith, that he has put such good things into my

Hon.red honoured Parents hearts to bring us up in the fear feare of

the Lord. Next I humbly acknowledge, my faithful

thanks thankes & gratitude to my dear deare & Hon.red honoured Parents

for their theire love, care caire , affection & sedulity over

me from my birth till this present, & for their theire

good performances towards my education in all

things. Begging of God to give me the grace of the

means meanes , as well as the means meanes of his grace, afforded

me. that so I may walk walke in all holy holie & strict obedience,

in thy Laws Lawes , & their theire precepts, according to all these thes

goodnesses of God, & theirs theires , performing my cordial cordiall

duty to them in all godliness godlynesse & honesty honnesty , obeying my

Parents in the Lord to the end of my Life Amen.
