None of us ever having haveing received receaved any thing out of

that part of my fathers Estate, of Kirklington for

the same as was appointed appoynted by my Fathers, last will

& testament. Therefore will I give glory to our God

on high which still has preserved this dear deare & tender

Mother . thereby there by testifying testifieing his miraculous favours

to the desolate widow widdow , & Children, in all times of

desertion desersion & troubles. And being mindful mindfull of his

Servant my father, in the blessing poured powred down downe upon

his Family. The Lord our God make us ever grateful gratfull

and thankful thankfull to his gracious, Majesty Majestie for Ever. Amen.
