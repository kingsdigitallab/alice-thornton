He died about the month of July: in the year yeare 1646.

at att Keeveton in the farthest part of Yorkeshire .

My Cozen Edmund Norton , Eldest son to my Uncle Norton

was married to Mr Dudlys Daughter & heir heire of Chopwell

in the Bishopric Bishoprick of Durham Mrs Jane Dudly an excellent excelent fine

& good gentlewoman. Feb. 10th 1647. at att Chopwell .

My Cosen Edmund Norton died of a Pleurisy Plurisie at att Yorke

the 30th of November: 1648. A gentleman of a sweet sweete good

disposition disposittion to all, obedient & dutiful dutifull to his parents & true

friend freind in time of adversity. a religious young man,

A faithful faithfull subject to his Majesty Majestie , for whom he suffered suffred

much. he lived an honest honnest good, sober life, doing doeing good

to all, died Religiously, & is I hope now very happy happie in

peace & rest, loving a peaceable temper. & was beloved of

all that knew him & An immeasurable unmeasurable loss losse to his Parents .
