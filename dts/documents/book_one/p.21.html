The Earle of Strafforde haveing farre different designes
then those Secret plotters of Rapine, & Ruine.
could not longer be endured, because he stood in the
way to hinder, & prevent by his wise Councell. Soe
that till he was removed, they could not prevaille either
in Ireland , or England to compasse theire ends.
Nor could there be found, a better expedient for theire
purpose then to make a cloake of Religion, that under
such a Populer, specious, pretence, Theire horrible
practices might not be found out.
