O Lord I beseech thee except of my hearty Pourings out
in Praier & praise. & let me live to magnifie thy Name
for all these transsendant mercys, day & night. & Let
this be arguments of thy love & favour to me & my deare
Son whom thou hast given me, for Jesus Christ, the righteous
his sake; For the Lord God had great Pittie upon my distress
& gave me affter this a compotency of health & strength
to be able to give my Childe Sucke. which by his blessing
I did till Robin was above 2 yeares old he contineuing very
healthfull & strong: Soe that my Sorrowes much abated.
