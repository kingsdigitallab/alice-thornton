Meditations uppon the birth of my 3d Childe Eliz.
Thornton borne the 14th of February. 165 5 4 .

I recovered not very well, of the extreame weakenesse
that followed for a quarter of a yeare affter my childe, in soe
much that my milke was taken from me & soe I was hin-
-dred from doeing the naturall duty incumbant upon us
mothers which troubled me much; but I must be willing
to submitt to all Gods pleasure in every thing.
