The death of my Sister Danby : September 30th 1645 Sept. 10th 1645

at att her house at Thorpe .

About this year yeare my deare, & only Sister the Lady

Danby drew near neare her time for delivery of her 16th Child

Ten whereof had been bin baptised. the other six 6 . were stillborn still

borne , when she was above half halfe gone gon with them, she having have

-ing miscarried of them, all upon uppon frights by fire in her

Chamber, falls, & such like accidents happening. All her

Children were Sons. saving saveing my two nieces neeces , Katherine &

Alice Danby , & most sweet sweete , beautiful beautifull , & comely were they

all. The troubles, & distractions of those sad times
