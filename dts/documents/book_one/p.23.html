Horrible Paricides & murders. breaking forth

first in Ireland to the destruction of millions of the

Poore protestants Christians who suffered martyredom

for theire God, & his Religion.

And in England many 1000 suffered by the sword

both with theire King, & for him, & the truth of

Religion there Established. & for the Lawes.

But to returne to my Lord Lieutenant , while the

Pretences of Religion, so filled the eares of the Parliament

of England then sitting in the yeare 40 and false

suggestions of oppressions against this Noble

Earle . he was called before them to give an answer

to such Articles as his Enimies of all factions

had unjustly laid to his charge.

The whole transaction, of this bussinesse, was prose-

-cuted, with soe much malice, & Rigour of his Enimies

side. And so much, wisdome, prudence, & galantrie

on the Earles. that all the world (save his Enimies thirsting

his blood,) did admire his incomparable Wisdome &

Abilities, in his cleare, & brave defence, he made for

himself, notwithstanding they gave fresh charges each

day, which he had never heard of, nor had he any time

Spaired, to give in his answer, but was sett upon with

new blood hounds, as theire fancies pleased,
