The Articles of Agreement were according to the

President of his Fathers towards his mother . Vidz.

That All his Estate, should be passed by fine & reco

-very to enable inable him to entail intaile the same upon his Issue

by me, male or female femalle .

That his Lordship of Easte Newton then valued valewed

at the yearly yearely rent of 250l should be estated Estated on

me for A Jointure Joynture . & after affter the decease deceace of the longer

liver of us two, to descend decend upon uppon his sons & their theire

issue successively successivily . for want of such heirs male to his

heirs heires females by me. &c. without with out impeachment of

any manner of waste waiste .

That Laistrop valued valewed at the yearly yearely rent of 160l

after affter the decease of his mother , married to Mr Gate

& then her Jointure Joynture . was Settled on Mr Thornton for

his life, after affter his decease upon uppon his heirs male &

for default of such issue, to his daughters by me.

As for his Land at Cottingham. Richmond , called

Burne Parke the inheritance estated Estated as the other of L.

After Affter a long longe lease leace made for the Provision Provission of his

younger children's childrens Portions & Maintenance, paid out

of it. The value valew wast accounted 100l Per Annum.

As for the security of my Portion he was to receive Reieave

the sum summe of 1500l out of Kirklington which he might

dispose of for himself himselfe & his own owne use. Being Beeing secured to me

And for the 1000l payable out of Ireland . he gave

bond to my mother to purchase Land of inheritance

for me during my Life, & for my Children at my

decease.

And with all my mother was willing to give us our Table

with all our Family Familie for three 3 years yeares . these were the terms tearmes

betwixt them.
