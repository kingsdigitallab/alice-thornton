The Articles of Agreement were according to the
President of his Father s towards his mother . Vidz.
That All his Estate, should be passed by fine & reco
-very to inable him to intaile the same upon his Issue
by me, male or femalle.

That his Lordship of Easte Newton then valewed
at the yearely rent of 250l should be Estated on
me for A Joynture. & affter the deceace of the longer
liver of us two, to decend uppon his sons & theire
issue successivily. for want of such heirs male to his
heires females by me. &c. with out impeachment of
any manner of waiste.

That Laistrop valewed at the yearely rent of 160l
affter the decease of his mother , married to Mr Gate
& then her Joynture. was Settled on Mr Thornton for
his life, affter his decease uppon his heirs male &
for default of such issue, to his daughters by me.

As for his Land at Cottingham. Richmond , called
Burne Parke the inheritance Estated as the other of L.

[word] I.e., Laistrop.


Affter a longe leace made for the Provission of his
younger childrens Portions & Maintenance, paid out
of it. The valew wast accounted 100l Per Annum.

As for the security of my Portion he was to Reieave
the summe of 1500l out of Kirklington which he might
dispose of for himselfe & his owne use. Beeing secured to me
And for the 1000l payable out of Ireland . he gave
bond to my mother to purchase Land of inheritance
for me during my Life, & for my Children at my
decease.

And with all my mother was willing to give us our Table
with all our Familie for 3 yeares. these were the tearmes
betwixt them.
