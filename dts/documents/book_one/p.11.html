A deliverance from a fire in London 1631.

There was a great fire in the next House to ours in St

martens Lane in Lond. which burned a part of our house

being beeing near neare to have burnt it down downe , but through

the care caire of our Servants it was prevented. this was done don

at night when my father & mother was at att Court.

but wee were preserved that time of fright at my Lady

Levestones house being beeing carried caried by Sara our maid maide .

This fire seemed to me as if the day of judgement was

come causing causeing much fear feare & trembling. yet we were

all delivered from perishing though my father had

much loss losse . But blessed be the Lord my God who gave

us not over to perish by this fire, but preserved preservd

our Persons from Evil Evill at that time.
