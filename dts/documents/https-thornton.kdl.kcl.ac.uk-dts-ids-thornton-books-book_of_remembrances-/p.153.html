153 1668.

My dearest heart was Interrd
in the Alley of his owne at his owne
Parich Church in Stongrave neare
his owne Mother & two sweete
children Joyce & Christopher.
he was Buried on Friday the 18th of
September. 1668 bytweene the houers
of 4 & 5 by Mr Thomas Comber
who Preached his Funerall sermon.
Whoes Text was in Eclesiastese: 12th
the 1st verce. Remember now thy
Creator in the daies of thy youth, while
the Evill daies come not, nor the yers
draw nigh; when thou shalt say,
I have no pleasure in them.

Lord hee loves thee the lesse, that
loves any thing with thee, which hee
loves not for thee.

(St Austin.)
