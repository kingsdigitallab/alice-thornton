The 31th she fell blind & had intermiting till the 5th of february beeing exceeding dange- -rous in her throat & could not swallow well till the 6th of february & beeing very sorre & much pained by the Pox therein & her streat throat. getting very little sleepe till the 3d of february when she began to sleepe the 4th her eyes began to unclose. the 5th she did see perfectly & they began to blacken & die ripe. & her throat was pretty well her feaver did abate 8 she began to be pretty well & strong. the 18th Naly rose out of her bed and recover very fast grow-ing strong by degrees. she had very many & was full over her face & the haire came all off.
