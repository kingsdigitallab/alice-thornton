44 1661. in a lost condition, by reason of my sinns, & sorrows satan accusing me, & casting multitude of doubts into my heart, As that surely I did not belong to God because he follwd me with such great Crosses Afflictions & troubles, which seemed to be Curses, rather then, such trialls as he used to lay upon those that were his children & that. I had neglected all those off -er & tendirs of mercys which he had from time to time given me from my youth up. in the Examples, instruction admonition of my deare Parents first & then by his minnisters in the word & sacraments, his great deliverances all the daies of my life innumerable. yet all these had not wrought a through convertion & change of heart in me to walke acording to his motions
