I began a dangerous sickness at oswoldkirke affter my deare mother death, beeing caused by a cold I got in that. church, causing a very great and violent vomitting upon shrove tusday 1661 feb. 13th so contineuing till I was not able to receave any kind of sustenance, beeing in an intermi -ting feaver, was compelld to send for Dr Wittie, which lett me blood, & gave many cordialls to strengthen my stomack, but nothing would stay with me. till I dranke a draught of cold water which more refreshed my thirsty soule then what art could give, that night beeing the 17th of feb.
