148 1668 Blood of Christ. And all these humble pettitions I crave for the only merritts & blood sheding of my dearest Savior Jesus the Righteous who interseadeth for us at the Throne of thy Grace even for me & all those that love his appeering To whom with the Glorious Father & holy spiritt. Ever blessed & Infinitt blesed Trinity. be All Possible Praise. Addoration & thankesgiving of men & Angells. henceforth & for Evrr more. Amen & Amen.
