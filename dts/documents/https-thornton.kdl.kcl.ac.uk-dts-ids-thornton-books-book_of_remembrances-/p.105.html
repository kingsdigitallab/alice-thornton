It pleased God to vissit my deare Brother
John Wandesford at London 10th his last
sicknesse beeing an Ague. a joyned with fitts
of the stone. & some extreamitys of sickness
which about the 2d day of December deprived
him of his life, for severall yeares before
he had laid under the most sad & afflicting
hand of God by reason of the want of the
use of his understanding severall times
yet having a bundance of intervalls. in which
time he was very Religously desposed &
constant in dutyes with a consiencue
