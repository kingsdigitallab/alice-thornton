86 63 beeing receaved by & from the mercy of a gracious father who hath at length bestowed on me his handmaid this hopes of the contineuce of my Memory: by a blessed son of my wombe. having obteined him from god by the fervent praires & teares of a poore mother: To thee o Lord I dedicate this Childe the sonn of my wombe. Let him be ever in thy sight for good. & stablish thy covenant with him made to Abraham & the faithfull As thoue hast given him the signe of it so most mighty .give him the true grace. that he may be established before thee both heere & heereafter That for Jesus Christ our Ever blesed saviour :& Reedemer. Amen. Amen.
