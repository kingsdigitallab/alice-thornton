143 1668 of the Righteous yet he will delivr them out of all. doe not there oh my soule Repine against this great, wise. holy & good God: for if thou belongst to him he will give thee to know his minde & that it is the Lord he must doe as he will. and allthough this dispensa =tion is most bitter as the cutting of thy life besure thou doest not sin against him by impatience. or resist =ing his pleasure. And allthough flesh & blood cannot part willingly Remember thou must live by faith if ever thou expect salvation. hee was prepared for death. thou are not oh pray that the remainder of thy life may be a continuall preparednesse & know that this affliction is to this very end & that as we did in this life suffer so may we Reigne together with him who is the Author & finisher of our faith.
