Oh that my soule may forever be thankful to the most high God that had regard to his poore & humble handmaid. what am I o Lord that should have the testimonys of thy mercy. I will give thee the glory of thy works. mercys & favours for ever; & most humbly begge, on the account of my Xsts Intercession that I may have the grace of perseverance, & a truly thankfull ♥ to walke worthy of these inestimable mercys & glorifie thee in the midst of all my trialls and sufferings that makest me way to Escape now Praise the Lord o my soule, & forgett not all his benifitts. Amen. Amen
