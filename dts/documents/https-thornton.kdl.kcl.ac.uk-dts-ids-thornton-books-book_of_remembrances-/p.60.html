This allso must be imputed to the goodnesse of God. who would not leave such a remarke of our offence but gently to put us in mind of our folly & provocation of his wrath. I humbly offer my unfe igned thanks & praise to his holy name, which heard my humble suplication in this perticuler by removing this unkindly & unusuall remembrance which caused greife at each sight there of. but yet my Soule doe not in the least measure prove ungratefull for the many mercys receaved at the hand of God; nor bury them in forgettfulnesse, &
