50 1661. desire unfained, & hope of thy clemency & sweetest mercy. that it will not be in vaine that this thy word came into my heart. this was from thy goodnesse, to let me see that the Tme was not past to come unto thee. Thou most swetly calling me at that present when my heart was in doubt.

I come o my god, and take up thy sweete yoake & follow thee. O Learne me to be patient meeke & lowly in heart, for thy yoke is easy, & thy burden is light. yea thy wayes is perfect freedonne. Lett me for ever mak thee my patterne, my comfort, my life & direction, so shall I find rest to my soule. rest, o, how sweete is that word to my Languishng
