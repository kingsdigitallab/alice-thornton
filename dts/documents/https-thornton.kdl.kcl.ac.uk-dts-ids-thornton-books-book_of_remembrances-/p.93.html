Assistance, advice, & councell of Mr Comber
whom my gracious God had givn me at
that time to be a meanes to suport my
fainting spiritts under this most sad afflicti
-on. when my father & mother forsooke
me (by death) the Lord taketh me up. &
suportes me from falling totally. though
he sufers me to be brought very low. & that
justly for my transgressions & offences.
yet hath he not given me over to death
or despaire.

Looke when we were the nearest death behold
the goodnesse of God was intreated for us to
restore both our lives and let me ever return
him the glory of his power. & the returne of our
Prayers. be ascribed to his majestie. that imm-
-eadiatly gave us a hopes of recovry upon
our calling upon his name. for that evry day
my husband did miraculously change, &
each day recovred so fast that it was a wonder
to all beholders. Oh what shall I say or doe
to set forth the infinit mercys & loving kindness
of the Lord our God.
