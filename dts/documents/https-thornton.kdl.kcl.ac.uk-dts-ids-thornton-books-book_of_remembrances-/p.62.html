62 to Prevent Nettleton from breaking up an excecution against his Estate for a Dept of my fathers which Mr Thornton had beine advised by some to enter into & to ingage for, af ter he had infortunatly taken up -on him the Assignment of my uncle maior norton of my fathers Estate In Ireland which was to pay his debts & Portions. A bussinesse which I must cleare, both my Deare Mother & my selfe from. that we had noe hand in the least in it, to advise him there to which we knew was noe way suitable neither to his Person nor Estate, being of great trouble, & with out that which my.
