68 in the whole course of my life, & of thankfullnesse to God for the daly renuall of the same.

I found by sad experiencs that the many troubles had fallen upon me of this world had diverted my stricter cours of walking with my God as he had required at my hand. & that I had many times broken those solomner vowes of Baptismall, & sacrament -all to frequently. & too offten by negligence, worldly imployments, & willfull sines. so that I had great impulses upon my heart. & desires to renew this grand ordenance of God & in true & unfained repentance, faith hope & charity. to be maid a pertaker of this strengthning. this comforting
