I now doe want those good & Pieous prayers of him for me & mine which I have injoyed for many yeares, what can I say, or what can I doe, each remembrance brings in a fresh flood of teares. I water my Couch. & widdowed desolate bed. for my selfe & Children. my thinke I heare him say weepe not for me but weepe for yourselves & Children.
