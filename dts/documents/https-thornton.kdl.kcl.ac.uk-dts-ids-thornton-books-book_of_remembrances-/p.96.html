96

While I am in this bodie of death
I find by sad experience. that as my daies
increase so will sin grow up too rife in
these corruptions. I have tasted of the great
& unlimited mercys & clemency of the
Great God of Heaven. the Lord most
High. And like wise beine under manifold
trialls. sorowes, troubles, & Aflictions
both as to spirit & body.

But as when the hand of God is upon us we
are apt to be more discomfited & sad. so shod
I, and doe desire to make it my uttmost
endeavour to inquire what is it that God
aimes at by all his providences & dealings
I find my heart to apt to forget what a
low condition I was brought into by sicknese
& sathan. either as to Mr Thorntos or my
owne trialls. And peradventure hath not
rendred him that due praise for all our Exceding
deliverances that he hath vouchsafed to me in
perticuler or him.
