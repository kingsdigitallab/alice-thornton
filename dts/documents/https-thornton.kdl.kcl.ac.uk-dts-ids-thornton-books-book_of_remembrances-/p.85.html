85 1662

My Sonne Robert was baptized on Satterday the 20th of September: 1662 by Mr Luckock at Newton. his Godfather & Godmother were my Lady Cholmely: Dr wittie: & Mr Thornton for my Nephew Best:

The God of All consolations & comforts preserve his life & health. with the opor -tunitys of vertuous & holy Education that he may be the instrument of greate Glory to God comfort to his Parents & relations. & for the building up in Righteousness & holinesse his fathers Family. to Possterity for many genera -tions. And at the end of his life. he may receave the Comfort of a sanctified old Age. with a Crowne of Glory to praise his Reedmer & mine for ever & ever: finding what the goodnese of God has beine to me in giveing me the request of my heart &
