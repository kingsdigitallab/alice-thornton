Kate Thornton had a deliverance from beeing choaked with swallowing a pin & got crosse her throat at St nickolas in the yeare 1659 when I was a meanes by Gods blessing to save her life by putting My finger in her mouth & thrusting the pin side way & got it out of her throat. for which I humbly praise God in this her preservation from beeing destroyed by her owne folly.

About the yeare that Kate Thornton was 10 yeares old. she was plaing in the Corne barne att Newton & swinging on a Rope that hung crosse. from which she got so high & cruell a fall by the boys her cosens flinging her that she fell downe with a great force & was taken up dead. hanging like a Calfe beeing with blacke in the face & not taking breath a long time which put me into a greiveous fright. till at last it pleased God in much mercy to restore her life & affter halfe an houer time
