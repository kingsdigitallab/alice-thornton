76 1662 I humbly cast my self at the Throne of Grace, who had ever bin my guide & director in all my waies & in every grand action of my Life when I made my earnest addresses. to him; so my pettitions & requests to the Almighty was that he would direct me in this greatest Temporall consernes for the good of my Children that he would please to put in to my heart what course to take & to direct me to such meanes as should be for this very end, that I might goe to my grave in Peace; having seen the mercyfull Provission of God, in a hopefull way settled for theire futur Provission & maintenance. in this world.
