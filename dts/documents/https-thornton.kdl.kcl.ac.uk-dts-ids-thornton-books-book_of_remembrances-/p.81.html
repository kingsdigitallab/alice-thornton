It pleasd my gracious Lord at last to bring into my remembrance a pouder which I had formerly by his blessing don good to many in the like kind. so I gott out the name of it to my Lady Yorke with much adoe to be heard for my weak nesse was such. but affter she had given me some of it I found the good effect of it thorough the tender mercy of my Redeemer. who healed all that came to him. by it he helped me. some what so that the flux was a little staied by degrees. till Dr Wittie was come. soe upon the same blessing & use of the meanes I was spaired at that time but brought soe weake that the remander
