This blessed mother of mine was thus gathered in

to her Grave; having lived many Peaceable years

together with my Father , brought him a competent

number of Children, being the support of his house and

Family, Preserving it & the branches under her care

& Prudence, living his Chaste wife; (& widow for

above 19 years.

her whole Age where in she lived, was Threescore

& seven years, and 11 months, & ode days

so that she died in a good old Age; full of good works

& virtue & hon.or our to all of her Family & Country

To the Lords most Infinite Majesty be all Glory

& praise for his great goodness & mercy extended

to me, & us all through this dear Parent of ours

He make us to possess those graces & virtues which he

Bestowed upon her; that we may be the better capable

to doe him true & faithful service to our lives End.

Amen.

Several Prayers made by my Mother proper for the

time of the holy sacrament. as Ejaculations.

Before receiving of the bread.

O most gracious God. which hath Sacrificed thine

only begotten son to appease thy just wrath for

my sins, & to ransom my Soul from hell; Seal

unto me by this blessed Sacrament, thy promise &

covenant made in Christ, that thou wilt receive

me, a penitent Sinner & true believer into thy Grace

& mercy, & that for the death & Passion of my

Dear Saviour; my Sins past, & present, may

be remitted & forgiven, as verily, as I shall now be

partaker of this blessed Sacrament. Amen.

Before Receiving of the wine. after consecration
