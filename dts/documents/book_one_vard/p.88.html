For the Restoration of the King, the Church, & the Kingdoms

peace, with such patheticall, & Jelous Expressions that

all did glorify God, for things he had don for her.

After which she did in a manner Prophesie, that God would

humble the Kingdom by Afflictions for there sin &

security. but after that when we were humbled and

reformed, whosoever should live to see it (for she should

not) should enjoy, happy days for Church & state.

thus she continued, & with prayers for our Enemies &

for they stood in need of our praires, for the forgiveness

of all their Evils.

She called her children, exhorted them abundantly

to fear God, serve him, & love one another, be obedient

to their father. with admonishing them, & her family;

she was kinde, & dearely affectionate to her husband , to whom

under God she left the care of her 7 young children .

Some times, she did express abundant joy in God, &

would sweetely, with a melodious voice sing aloud his praise

& Glory, in Anthems, & Psallmes proper for her condi-

-tion, with many sweet verces praising him for all things

, Nor was she in the least concerned to part with her husband

or Children, nor any thing in this world, having her hope

& desires fixed upon God. Leaveing her Children freely

to the Providence of her God, who had releived her soul

out of all her distress. who had promised to be a father

to the fatherlesse. All her words weere full of sweetnesse

& Affection. giving me manny hearty thanks for all

my pains, & care I took with her. & watching a whole

week together. if she lived she would requite my love
