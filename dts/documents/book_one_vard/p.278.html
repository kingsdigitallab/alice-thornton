Endow me thy handmaid with suitable qualifications

to serve thee in there Godly & Religious Educations.

And to serve thee the Searcher of hearts with Zeal & a

constant Regular devotion. Restore, & preserve us in the

life of Righteousness, Sobriety. & humility & Chastity

in our words & Actions. Blessing me & mine with happy

opportunities of Religion, all our days & doing thee

faithful service that we are capable of in this Life.

That I may redeem the time past; & by thy Grace may

grow Rich in good works always abounding in the work

of the Lord; That when thou shalt demand my Soul

to be rendered up into thy hands. My Soul may not

be abhorred of thee, Nor suffer thy terrors, but may

feel an Eternity of blessings in the Resurrection of the

Just. And further my humble request is o Father. that

Thou wilt shed thy Spirit of grace into the hearts of

all my Children & my Self that we may be firmly uni

ted to thee & each other in the bands of faith & Charity

That it may not be in Satan's Power, nor his instruments

to disunite our hearts from thee & each other, but that we

may continue for ever in Truth, unity, & Peace &

Concord that the God of Peace, may give us his Peace through

his holy Spirit of Sanctification. And all these most

humble requests I beg at the Throne of thy Grace, with
