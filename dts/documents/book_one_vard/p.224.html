About this Christmas 1666 my Cozen william Norton

was inhumanly murdered at London neare Grays Inn .

My Son Robert s having the small Pox. Jan. 5 1667

and of his recovery perfectly again.

The 5th of Jan. my deare Robin Thornton s began his sickness

of the small Pox being very ill & weak for 2 days. the

7, 8, 9th he was in great danger of death. they not come

-ing well out till the 10th on which day he lost his sight by

them being very great ones & full.

About the 14th his fever & disease was at height. & on the

15th he altered for the better. so that a change was discerned

the 17th he began to see again. the 18 had his sight clearly

recovered. after which he recovered very fast. for which I

most humbly bless God. with all my soul. & magnify

his mercies to me in the deliverance of this my dear & sweet

Child , in not quenching our cole in this Family. O Praise

the Lord o my soul & forget not all his benefits. this his

favour to thee for ever. Amen. The 24th Robin first

got up. & was perfectly well. growing strong & was not

so much disfigured As his sister Kate . But he never

recovered his sweet beautiful favour & pure colour in

his cheeks. but his face grew longish. his hair did not fall

of. he wanted 9 months of being 5 years old & some dais

Hanna Ableson & margery millbanke kept him in his

sickness.

It Pleased God, after my sonne Robert was well. to

visit my daughter Alice Thornton . which began in her

sleepe with another dangerous fit in her sleep. which much

awakned frighted me. she being almost choked by the

Phlegm. making her exceeding sick. before she vomited

up some ill digested meat & with extremity she was

black in her face. but by the mercies of our Gracious God

after she vomited. she did recover. though it kept her

weak. I will praise the Lord our God of our salvation for this

great deliverance of my Child. Amen.
