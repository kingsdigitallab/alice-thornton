Meditations upon the birth of my 3d Child Eliz.

Thornton borne the 14th of February. 165 5 4 .

I recovered not very well, of the extreme weakness

that followed for a quarter of a year after my child, in so

much that my milk was taken from me & so I was hin-

-dred from doing the natural duty incumbent upon us

mothers which troubled me much; but I must be willing

to submit to all Gods pleasure in every thing.
