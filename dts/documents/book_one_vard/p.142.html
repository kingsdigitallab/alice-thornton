Meditation upon the birth of my 4th childe Katherin

Thornton June 12th 1656. borne at hipswell .

After I was with quick child of my fourth child I

had pretty good health, considering my condition, till

I was with in a month of my time. & then I grew very

heavy, big, & weary. full of pain; & the labour each

day was on me. I found the child more weighty & not

so nimble as naly & betty . so that she caused me to endure

more in a long pain before then the rest & I was one whole

week in travail very strong insomuch that I endured more

in that time & in the extremity, then of my other, & my fears

was much greater then formerly & I had greater ex-

-pectancy of my dissolution. my dear mother also did

fear me very much & she was pleased to assist me in

pouring out our requests to God for which she did in this

Prayer following:
