Beloved Son Christ Jesus, in whom thou hast decla

red thy self to be well pleased, and to accept the

Cordiall devotions of thy faithful servants; In his

blessed name we we return to render unto thy Sacred

Majesty our bounden duty of praise and Thanksgiving

for thy wonderful, powerfull, and merciful preservati

-on & deliverance of thy distressed handmaid & servant

from the pains & Perrills of childebirth, And hath vou-

-chsafed to give her a comelie child perfectly formed.

safe from harm; wee humbly beseeching thee, O Lord to

continue thy love and favour to this thy weak servant

in restoring her to her former health and strength if it

be thy blessed will that she may live to praise and

glorify thy great name, for this and the like preservati

on of her, To bring up this infant and the rest of her Children

in the true faith & fear of God, That her womb may be

a Nurrsery for thy Kingdom, strengthen her faith in

this weakness, that the Devill may not have power to tempt

her beyond her strength derived from thee, neither her

own frailties prevail in any insuing distempers, sub-

ject to those in her condition, but in mercy dispose her

to quiet rest, and give a blessing in the moderate use of

thy good creatures for her foode & Refreshment, with

an unfeyned thankful heart for all thy mercies, a

contented mind with thy most wise dispensations,

however it shall please thee to dispose of her self, her

Infant, or any other pledges of thy love, further wee

continue our prayers for this infant , that as thou O

Lord hast brought it by thy power, and in mercy forth

of the mothers womb to behold the light of this world,

So we beseech thee to give it thy seale in holy Baptism

Sacrament of Baptisme, Thy holy spirits Illumination

to sanctify that Laver for its Regeneration, from Orriginall

sinn to actuall Righteousness, as it shall live to years

of capacitie.
