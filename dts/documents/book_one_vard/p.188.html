Blessed Portion of Scripture. which our dear Saviour spake

in St Matt. the 11 chap. & 28, 29, 30 verses; which like the

Lightning breaking in to a dark place; thus it pierced

into the secret darkness of my sorrowful heart & dejected

Soul; bringing refreshment & some hopes of relief & comfort

being increased in the consideration of those heavenly mercies

contained in that scripture. Looking upon this as his sweet

& effectual call to my soul, wherein he then bid me come

unto him, & alone seek of him that had strength, power, & mercy

to relieve all poor sinful dejected souls, which laboured &

were heavy Laden, with sins the saddest burden then to be borne

Come unto me all yea that labour, & are heavy Laden

and I will give you rest.

Behold the handmaid of the Lord: I laboured under, & was

heavy Laden with the burden of my sins. under the burden of

Satan's bondage; & slavery of fears. the one bringing me to

wards despair, the other of torment, persuading that time was

past for any help from God, that the Devil might wreak his ancient

malice against souls, on me, in my sickness & weakness

then, being the fittest opportunity to prevail: when he shakes

there faith: Butt Lo in this my distress; the Lion of the

Tribe of Judah put to flight for me, as he had don for him-

selfe, when he tempted him to question his sonship; The

Prince of darkness: with this word of his, Come unto me. show

ing thereby that he was a liar. & that it was not to late for me

to repent & believe; nor him to accept of my soul, & so

by this he vanquished his strongest stratagem by which he

surpriseth the poor doubting Christian when at the Lowest Ebb

this he trieth if by any means he can make him doubt or

question the rich goodness that is Eternally in God as his very

being, & so commit the great sin against his mercy, from

whence flows mans Salvation. & then he is lost forever.

But at this time, when I was weakest Christ Jesus the Righ

teous came into my heart with healing under his wings

making it appear that Satan was a liar, & that he never refuseth

any who come unto him; according to that place of: those that

come unto me I will in no wise cast of. He calls, he invites,
