A Prayer After the death of my 3d child Betty. Thornton

O Lord most high, whose is the right of Creation &

Governor of all the Earth. I beseech thy gracious Maj

estie to pardon our great & crying sins, who hath

Provoked thee to deprive us of this sweet Child thy

Blessing to us. let thy favour over look our offences

& pardon wherein we have don amiss in all accidents

& occurrences of our lives, before, & since we knew each

other, forgive my impatience, or murmuring at thy

heavy hand. who yet in Judgement hast remembered

mercy & hast not pressed us to destruction. Lord look

not upon us as in our selves for then no living can stand

in thy presence. but appear polluted, defiled, & by

depraved nature Abominable in thy sight. but look

on us in thy dear Son Jesus Christ, our redeemer. and

pardon all our sinful compliances towards each other

since our married Estate if there have bin any such as

has displeased thee O Lord, for thou art a God of purity

& holiness, therefore cleanse all our hearts, & sanctify this sad

cross for our good & thy glory. to which I humbly submit

& freely, seeing thy goodness in delivering this my sweet

child from all its Miseries in this world. of Infancy

Childhood, & folly. And bless thy divine Majesty

who hast taken her a way, before her soul was polluted

with actual sins. hoping in my dear Saviour who recea
