A Prayer After the death of my 3d child Betty. Thornton

O Lord most high, whose is the right of Creation &

Governour of all the Earth. I beseech thy gracious Maj

estie to pardon our great & crieing sins, who hath

Provoaked thee to deprive us of this sweet Child thy

Blessing to us. let thy favour over look our offences

& pardon wherein we have don amisse in all accidents

& occurrances of our lives, before, & since we knew each

other, forgive my impatiency, or murmuring at thy

heavy hand. who yet in Judgement hast remembred

mercy & hast not pressed us to destruction. Lord look

not upon us as in our selves for then no living can stand

in thy preasence. but apeare poluted, defiled, & by

depraved nature Abominable in thy sight. but look

on us in thy dear Son Jesus Christ, our redeemer. and

pardon all our sinful compliances towards each other

since our married Estate if there have bin any such as

has displeased thee O Lord, for thou art a God of purity

& holiness, therefore clence all our hearts, & sanctify this sad

cross for our good & thy glory. to which I humbly submit

& freely, seeing thy goodness in delivering this my swet

child from all its Miseries in this world. of Infancy

Childhood, & folly. And bless thy divine Majesty

who hast taken her a way, before her soul was poluted

with actuall sins. hopeing in my dear Saviour who recea

, that her Soul is acceptable in thy sight, whom thou hast

taken so early into thy Kingdom. O Lord give us grace
