Which lumpe was conceived to be contracted

She had also an Exceeding sore throate &

Mouth, so that she was deprived of the benefit of eate-

-ing or swallowing, almost any kind of food save

a little drope of beere, being the most she took inw-

her Tongue & mouth, at first was blacke. then it

turned white, so that with the pains my dear mother

took in washing & cleanseing the skinn came of

& was red till the blood came, this contineuing, till

in the end her mouth grew white all over.

In this most sad condition of weakness was

my dear mother , almost quite with out food, Rest

ease, or sleep for about a week. In which time, as

also in all the rest of her sickness, she expressed

extraordinary patience. still saying, it was the

Lord that sent it to her, & none else could take it from

her, & if he pleased to see it fit, he could ease her

or give her patience to indure his hand.
