Upon my Husband , & Families Removal from St

Nickolas , to Oswold Church. neare Newton . June 10 1660.

After my strength was again Recruited through Gods

mercy for travail; We removed: with my Husband & those

Children, the Lord had blessed me with Alice , my Second, &

then Eldest, & Katherine , my 4th Child; and came to Osw--

oldkirke living a fortnight at my Sister Denton s

house till our own in that Town was ready.

Then we got to it with all my Dear Mothers household

stuff which was brought thither, where we lived two years

After I had taken leave of my own country, & dear

friends & Relations; parting with them with a sad heart

Amongst whom I had lived many comfortable years.

But God was pleased to go a long with me in a strange Place

making me to find many sweet influences of his favour

both in sickness & in health, and giving me comfort of

my Husbands friends, instead of my own. & to find a

great deal of favour amongst strangers where I lived

so that in all Places, & at all times, & upon all occasions.

I have daily fresh cause, & occasions to admire the infinite

goodness of that God whom I serve, desiring to speak well of

his name, who has pity upon his weak servant.

Here I lived for 2 years till our own house at Easte-

Newton could be finished, which we had bin in building.
