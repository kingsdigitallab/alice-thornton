A Lamentation, & Prayer, upon the death of

my hon.red brother George Wandesford Esquire .

thy Powerfull Attributes, & Judgements. what shall

I say or, doe unto thy glorious majesty. who hast

looked down upon us, with a mighty breach. adding

great sorrows to our Public calamitys. Thou hast a

controvercy with this whole nation, & also with this poor

Family, by taking away our brother by an unnatarall

death, when he was in hopes to have lived in peace and
