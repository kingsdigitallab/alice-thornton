My mother Gates , who was my husbands mother; died

at Oswoldkirke of a flux of blood by siege. as it was

supposed to have a vein broken inwardly which by fits

troubled her many years, having broken it by a vomit

of Antimony to strong for her stomach. she departed the

10th of May 1655. & was buried at Stongrave in her

husbands Alley, my father Thornton , whom she had out

lived 17 years, having all so bin married to him 17 years

& was buried on the 11th of May. 1655.

My Husbands Father in Law Mr Geffery Gates died.

at Hull the 18th of May. 1655 &, was buried at Hull the

same next day: May 19th 1655.
