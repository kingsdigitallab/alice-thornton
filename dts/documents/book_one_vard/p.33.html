He had the perfect use of Reason, & clear understanding

as, in all his life, which was an infinite mercy afforded

him. most quick & acute in all faculties, as in

perfect health.

The entertainment in his sickness was full of

divine meditations, Ejaculations, & prayers, with

praises to his God. & preparations for death.

he gave many instructions to his son George , to be

diligent in the service of God, obedient to his comm-andent

obedient, & dutiful, to his dear mother . who had

bin a faithful, tender, loving wife to him & his

Children, he commanded him to love; Hon.or obey her

in all things, all his days, due to her for her wisdom

& virtue. & doing this God would bless him the better

charged him to suffer his will to be performed, which

was just & equal, there being right don to him

& all persons else. ending with many good advices

to fear & love that dreadful Lord God. & he would

bless him. & provide for him as he had don to him-selfe

When he laid slumbering, still would he be, as if

discoursing in judicature, that he would doe uprightly

to all in his power. if the poor mans cause be right

he should not suffer for his poverty. nor the Rich, gain

for being so. if his cause were bad. neither could

he respect the persons of the Rich or poor. but doe up-

-rightly according to the Laws of God, & man.
