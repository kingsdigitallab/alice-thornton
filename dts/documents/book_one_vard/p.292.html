Dec. 10 1640. My brother Christopher Wand.

being in the Church called Christs Church in

Dublin hearing the great & dreadful cry that

the Irish made at my dear fathers Funeral

was so frighted that he fell into the most grievous

fits of the Spleen which much tormented him for many

years after & had like to have taken his life away

butt blessed be the gracious God by my dear mother

excellent cares. cost, & pains he was cured & became

a very strong man. & lived to be the Father of that Fam

of which he was descended & was my beloved brother

living to the Age of 61 years & died at London

feb. 23d 1686. buried at Kirklington by his Antien
