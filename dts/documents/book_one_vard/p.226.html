67.

My brother Denton s Son John fell into great extreamity

upon the taking of Physic on the 25th of Jan. which did not

work kindly. & so continued 3 days deprived of sleep

or food. falling into a fever. with violent pain. all which

deprived him of his understanding being in a dangerous

condition. but it pleased God at last getting some sleep

about 4 a clock in the morning he knew every one when

the Pox appeared the fever abated & he recovered very

fast having but a few. blessed be God for his deliverance.

The 8th of April: 1667 Nally had a Pearl on her Eye

after the small Pox. which endangered the loss of her Sight

But by waters. & a medicine to the wrests of her Arms

which sweet Mrs Bucke did advise she recovered that danger

being well again about the 30 April. 1667. I humbly

bless the Lord for the least, & greatest testimonies of his daily

favours to me & mine. O that these might set forth his

praises for ever more Amen.

In the year 1659 when I was at St Nickolas . after the

death of my dear mother Kate Thornton was preserved

from choking with a pin which got cross her throat. which by

the blessing of God I got out by my finger; when she was

nigh death: as also through his Providence. I preserved

her maid Anne Robinson from the like death by a piece

of a goose wing pinion which was cross so long: that her

breath was stopped. & almost dead. at Oswoldkirke . 1661.

About the year when Kate was 10 years old playing with

her cousins in Newton Barn & swing cross by a Rope she

got so high a fall by his swinging her from him. (J. D) that

she was taken up dead. being black & with out breath for

a long time at which sudden blow I was much affrighted for

my Child , rubbing & using all means for her recovery,

and it was half an hour before any signs of Life appeared

But it pleased God in mercy. at last she did breath:
