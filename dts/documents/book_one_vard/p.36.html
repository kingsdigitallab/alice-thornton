His body being imbowelled, was afterwards

Imballmed, & all the noble parts was very Sound

& perfect saveing the heart which was decaied

of one side. It was thought tis proceeded from

much study & business, which his weighty &

great imployments called him to.

Great watchfullnesse & pains in the faithful

discharge in his Offices.

He was the Maister of the Rowles in Ireland 7

years. One of his Majesty privey council. A

Judge in the Kings Bench . once Lord chief

Justice of that Nation. And Lastly he died the

Lord Deputy of that Kingdom of Ireland .

being the only Man in that place (as was observd

which died untouched, or peaceably in their beds

He was found faithful & so beloved of his

Prince & countrey. A most generally lamented

Person in that Kingdom who had found the swete

nesse of his Government in much meekenesse &

clemency. He was also the Last Deputy for

many years, being the last in King Charles the

first s time, the warres following after his death

The next Lawfull governer there was the Earle

of Ormond in King Charles the 2ds time of Restora
