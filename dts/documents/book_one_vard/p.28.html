In his time there were many causes determined

& decided, of great concernment betweene parties

which had depended. some 10. 20. 30. years. & the

cause of the widdowes, & oppressed, strangers &

the fatherlesse, was rightly adjudged & deter-

-mined.

Which through the imbesility of the Parties. the Power

of the adversaries. or corruption of the under Officers

, had bin till then neglected. but to the comfort

of the injured was rightly settled. & also to the sattisfa

-ction of the other parties. who being convinced

by the pains, & Christian, advices, & wise Just

mannagerey of his government. they confessed

the equity of his determinations, to be Just

Legall & right.

Yea such was the sweet affability, & prudence of

his carriage, in general. that none which went from

England gained so much upon affections of that

Nation. & all whom he lived amongst.

His Life was given for a Public good, to that Kingdome

as well, as to be a bllissing in his own Family.

Who was exceeding happy in such a Father &

Head. His dear & beloved wife , most blessed in

such a comfort, support, & Husband, as the world

could not paralell. in all chaste. pious. dear
