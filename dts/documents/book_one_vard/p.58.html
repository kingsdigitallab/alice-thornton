Neighbours: for wee be commanded to provide for

our inferiors by embracing them with a fatherly

affection, by providing for their maintenance and

Education, by instructing them in the fear & nurture

of thee our heavenly father, by giving them all good

examples, in our religious & honest carriage &

behaviour; by chastising them for their offences

And to hon.or our superiors in heart word & deed

& gesture; to yield all obedience unto them, to

imitate their virtues, to express our thankfulness

to them in word & deed, & to wink at their

imperfections & infirmities: Contrary to those

duties, we have wanted of that Natural affection

where with wee ought to abound; wee have neglected

our duties by contempt our scorn, to much indulgence

and rigour, by lightness & immodesty, & too much

boasting. Neither can wee excuse our selves. O Lord

Com.

6th from hatred, anger, evil will, desire of revenge, wai-

wardness, peevishness, inhumanity, wrathful looks

and such like misbehaviours against our neighbours,

Whereas thou hast commanded us unfeigned love,

one towards another, benevolence, compassion,

meekness, long suffering, affability, & all kind of

courteous behauiour.

Com.
