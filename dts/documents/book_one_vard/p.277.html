O let me beg it of thy Majesty with out offence. that I

might be in a more prepared condition to meet thee

the sweet Bridegroom of my Soul being found doing

the work of my Lord. And in the interim let me show forth

the loving kindness of the Lord amongst the Redeemed ones

in the land of the Living: for the living the living he shall

Praise thee & confess thy holiness, & the mercies of thy

holy Name. O hide not thy face from me any more

but give me sufficient Sustentation & support to enable

with fortitude to endure thy fatherly chastisements. That

Thou maist have the Glory of all, & my Soul reap the beni-

fitt of thy Rod, by amendment of life, in better obedience

I humbly beseech thee o Lord my God to give me also a

healthy temper of body, To be the better enabled to doe my

duty which thou hast called me unto. As a faithful, &

careful mother & head of this Family, praying thee to

continue it still if it be thy pleasure for many Generations

to glorify thy Name on Earth, & made members of thy

Mystical body in heaven, of thy Church Triumphant.

Dear Lord I pray thee blesge my three Children with all

blessings in order to Eternity. with Grace, wisdom & under-

-standing. be thou their Portions & inheritance for Ever

for whom thou blessedest they shall be blessed, O sanctify them

with thy Spirit from their youth, & preserve them by thy

Power from vicious humours & corrupt inclination of the

Old Adam. but putting on the Lord Jesus Christ. Neither let them

want any thing in this life with out the which they cannot

serve thee, nor let them be Rebellious or disobedient. To

their Spiritual, Politic, or natural Parents. Make

them obedient to thy Laws & Precepts. Divine & moral

And teach thou me by thy Spirit, that I may instruct them

in those duties thou hast commanded; that so I may be

assisted to discharge a good conscience in all holy precepts

Give them also I beseech thee an obedient Ear and a

willing heart, in love and affection, & submit to Those

instructions from thee. That they may be comforts to thy

servant in this her disconsolate condition.
