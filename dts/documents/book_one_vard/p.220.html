(However it was.) this poor Brother was the greatest suff-

& fully restored, was again brought down, through troubles

And want, he sustained. nor was it in my power to help

or relieve him otherwise then by my Prayers & Tears for him.

our own sufferings being so great.

Yet did the God of mercies give him many intervals in which

times he was religiously disposed. & constant in duties. with

a conscience to spend his days holily & uprightly; & in great

penitence for any thing that might offend his God.

About half a year before his death he was more strict in

his severe duties of Piety, saying that he should not live long

so that he prepared for his desolation each day.
