My son Robert Thornton preserved July: 25 1668.

It pleased God to give my sweete Robin Thornton a very

great deliverance upon the 25 of July. In his play with

his Sister Kate & cosen willy Denton standing in the wind-

-ow in the Hay laith at Newton , which is above 4 yeards from

the Earth. he fell down in to the laine. near a great stone

which if he had light on might have killed him falling so

high. so that the danger was very great. & his deliverance also

& ought to be haad

[word] SiC ??? i.e., had.

in remembrance with gratitude and

hearty thanks to the God of Heaven, which sent his Angell to

preserve my poor Child from death, or any harm save

a lumpe on his face.

The Glorious name of Jehovah, be praised & magnified

for his Life & the Preservations thereof from all casulties

, dangers, Sicknesses. dislocations. & Evils. And giving

him a compotent shaire of understanding, witt: memory

a loving & affable nature. with several other good guifts

tending to the accomplishment of his Person with naturall in-

dowments. but I doe adore the Lords name & mercy which

hath begun some dawning hopes of his grace in his heart

Appearing in his being affected with good instructions in the

knowledge & fear of God. & his desire to be informed of all

things concerning God. with notions of fear in hearing his

Judgments. with several pathaticall expressions of god &

his wayes. one day being about 4 years old he told me

of his own acord: That God was a pure, holy, wise,

& merciful Spritt. But the Deivill was a wicked lieing

malicious Spritt. was it not better to believe this holy

good God & serve him. then that wicked Evil Spirit which

would destroy us.; I must therefore with humble gratitud

take notice with comfort in his mercy which did not dispie the

prayers of his handmaid but given me a gracious answer

to my humble suplications, when I wanted a son. for this

blessing I begged of the Lord as Hanna did Samuell. And

has dedicated him to his service even all his days. further

craveing the continuance of his favour & Grace of his Spritt

upon my Sonn . Endowing his ??? from his Childehoode with all

Christian virtues, faith knowledge wisdom. & true under

standing. to guide & direct
