But Mrs Danby was not inclinable to accept of her sisters

offers. but stood upon her high horse. She would either

have 80l a year settled upon her and her Debts promised

to be paid, or she would never inslave her self again to live

as she had don with her Children. she could tell how to live

otherwise if she might not live hansomly with them.
