The birth of my Sonne Christopher , my 9th Child

no. 11 1667 & of his Death. Dec. 1st 1667.

Of my 9th Child ; it was the pleasure of God to give

me a weak & sickely time in breeding from the February

till the 10th of may following. I not having fully recruited

my last Sept. weakness; & if it had bin good in the Eyes

of my God. I should much rather (because of that) not to

have bin in this condition: but it is not a Christians

part. to chuse any thing of this nature. but what shall be the

will of our heavenly father, be it never so contrary

to our own desires. Therefore did I desire to submit

in this dispensation. & depend upon his providence

for the preservation of my Life, who had delivered me

in all my extreamities. & Afflictions.

I had not my health, till about the 10th of May. when I

perceived my self with quick Child. affterwards during

the time of being with Child. till within a month of my de

-livery very well as of any other. walkeing a mile

to the Church each Sabath day. I humbly bless the God

of my Life & strength. that restored so much health &

strength again to his handmaid. Giving me great

comfort in my dear & beloved husband . who all this

summer & spring was so well & strong as he never had

bin since his first ill fit at Steersby . The Lord giving

a blessing to those means appointed by Dr wittie for his

preservation. which order we observed; till towards Sept.

following. But when I grew so bigge & ill near my

delivery about Micklemas he intermitted those rules of di-

rections wanting my assistance about his Person to

in my Illnes. he relapsed several times. And had the Dr

sent for to him very often. by which helpes through Gods

Blessing was restored to me again. Praised be our good.

& gracious Lord God for Ever. Amen.
