Thus was it in the time of this Prophet Isaiah when

God had a controversy with his people of Iraell. denou

-ncing & threatening his Judgements against them

by this, & others of his prophets. yet would they not

hear nor return.

Then did he move theme by his sweetest mercies,

those was in vain. how long shall I call, & yea will

not answer. and all the day long. I have stretched

out my hand to a gainsaying people. these will not

move. And now, he sends his last warning Pieces

that they might take notice. he was bringing an

great Evils upon them, for the hardness of their

hearts, this was the Sign.

The Righteous perisheth. yea they perish from of

this Earth. But what doe they lose, that loseth

a Righteous man. A holy man, from their place

whose, Souls, &, bodies, has bin kept from impiety

& pollutions, of this world the flesh, & the devil, which

are true Israelites indeed. Pouring out daily

their petitions & prayers to God for the Kingdom

place, & Families. & with Moses stood before the People

to turn away his wrathful displeasure against

them. with strong cries & tears to God to spare his

People from destruction.

Great are the benefits which are enjoyed in the Life, &

Examples, Prayers, & supplications, Government, &

instructions, of a holy, Righteous King. Father.
