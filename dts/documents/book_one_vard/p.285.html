A Recollection of Memmorable accidents, &

Passages. forgotten to be Entred into my Book.

this must be placed In the first Place Page 8th before the

The mesels in 1629

At Kirklington 1629

[+] Upon my deliverance from death by a fall.

when I was 3 years old. when I cutt a great wound in my forehead of above an Inch long

My Father , & mother , living at Kirklington ;

where I was borne; & my brother Christopher also

The same maid servant attended upon him, and was

his dry nursse, ( Sara Tomlinson ) which kept me. after I

was weaned; being like wise both nurrsed by one

wett nurrse. tho upon having a fresh milk, she had

a Child betwixt the nuriseing my brother & my self

And having bin very good & cairefull of the first Child

my Parents saw it fit she should nurrse the 2d Child too.

discharging the duty so well to me. I heard it observed that

I was both a strong & healthfull child all a long. never

having had either the Ricketts or any other desease.

for which I most humbly & heartily give thanks to the

God of my salvation, which still had his gracious Eye,

of Providence over me both at my birth, when my

dear mother brought me forth in great Perrill of her

life she being weak. upon the birth of all her Children

having had seaven in all: 4 Sons & 3 Daughters.

Yet the Lord gave me a Sound healthfull body streight

Limbes & of a resonable understanding Praised be his

glorious name for ever.

Yet has his goodness bin more extended to me in this

& all other preservations That I might not forget his

mercies for ever. And that hath manny ways of the extent of

of his favours to young Infants in there deliverances

from death & destruction in this world besides that of sicke'n

esses & weakness of body. for if his divine Providence did

not send his Angels to keep & gaurd little Children They

could not continue not be preserved from all evil Actident-ts
