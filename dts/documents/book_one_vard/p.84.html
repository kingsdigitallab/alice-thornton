Respite or ease till I could take any thing, & this

continued, all that night, & the next day, till night;

butt by the gracious blessing of God, upon some respite &

things given by Mr Mahum . with my dear mothers care

I escaped that desperate fit, & by degrees was cured,

only it brought me very weak & faint.

Praised be, the God of all mercies for this his merciful

providence, in delivering my Soul from the gates of

death even adding this favour to the number of the

rest & giving me great & fresh occasions to lift

up my voice in gratitude to the Lord of our salvation

O give thanks unto the Lord most high which is great

wonderful, & holy, the Lord increase my faith in thy

name & thankfulness to thy goodness. for these daily

deliverances & new lives given to me. Bless the Lord O

my Soul. with all thy powers, of Soul & body, all that is

within thee, bless his holy Name, & magnify him for

ever, which has delivered also me, & all my mothers

house out of the violency of our Enemies which hates us

with out a cause, Lord bless us & all thy servants that

profess the truth of thy Religion, & suffers for a good cause

that they may be delivered at the last. & thy church & King

may be preserved to praise, & adore thy name, & that I

may be bettered by all these trials & troubles that thou see

fit to chastise me with all in my youth, to bring me near

to thy Majesty in faith, & godliness, all my days

for my Lord Jesus Christ. Amen.
