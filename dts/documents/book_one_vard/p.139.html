Elizabeth Thornton my third Child was borne at Hipsw

the 14th of february. 1655 being on wednesday half hour

after 11 a clock in the forenoone; she was Baptised the 16th

of February by Mr Anthony . wittnesses, my mother , my

Aunt Norton , and my brother Christopher Wandesforde

Mrs Blackburne stood for my mother being sick then.

A thanksgiving after the birth of my 3d childe Betty

Oh, what shall I render unto the Lord most high for his

goodness, & loving, kindness, & beniffits, showed unto me

or how shall I set forth all his praise, which am not able to

reckon up the least of all his noble acts, nor can I count them for

the number far exceeding the starres for multitude, that he

hath showed to me his weak handmaid. having added

many years to my days, & many new lives to my years

in his late manifold deliverances. I am not able to yeald

his majesty suficient thanks for his former mercies. yet

now has he heaped up a multitude of fresh deliverances

& preservations to my self & children, whom the Lord has

given me. I will sing of the loving kindness, of the Lord; &

with his corrections he will mixe his mercy, & sweet smiles

of his countenance upon me. Therefore thou O Lord

most high art to be praised, & in thy Temple shall every

one speak of thy praise. Admirable in wisdom fearefull

in praises & doing wonders. O Lord, our God whose mercy

reacheth to the Heavens, as great & many as the moments

of Eternity, yet hast thou humbled thy self to behold the

wreched children of men, & amongst the rest, on me the vildest

person by reason of my Sins. And hast opened thy hand

wide to fill me with blessings from a bove & the daly sweet

effects of thy loving kindenesse. Thou O Lord hast heard
