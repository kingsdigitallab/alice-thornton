Two Children with thy graces & favours, long life & health

with all opportunitys to doe thee service, here being freed

from the bondage of sin, by thy sufferings. having grace

in their hearts. Bless also thy servant my husband

& my self with all mercies, graces, spiritual, & temporal,

fit us by thy Rod & support us by thy staffe for thy heavenly

Kingdom, that we may in the meane time, possesess our vessells

in holiness; & Righteousness all the days of our lives, and

All for Jesus Christ our Lord his Sake. Amen.

Upon my great fall I had, being with child of my 5th

September 14th 1657 at Hipswell .

I fell with child after my deare betty s death, haveinge

my health very well, after quick child & so continued

till I got a great fall over the threshold in the hall, at Hips-

well , being then great with child of my fifth , wanting but

Ten weeks before my time. on the 14th of september 1657

which fall cast me into an ill fit of a fever, and the Jaundies

followed, & a bout 3 weeks was very weak, & in great

danger of death, & miscarriage. with the contineuall pains

& excessive motions of the child in me, which was turned wrong

in my womb, by the fall, & might easily be disearned, nor

could it be able to turn the head aright, all though it kept

continuall bending its back & body for it. so that Dr. witty

was sent for, & used all his skill in my preservation; and by

the blesing of God upon the means. I was delivered from

Death, and marvelously restored to strength at 6 weeks

end; I was let blood by the Dr with other remidies which made

me go to my full time. O what shall I render to the Lord

most high which had compassion on his weak handmaid.

Lord I am not worthy to live much lesse to receive all these

miraculous mercies from thy goodness for which I adore thy name

for ever Amen.
