rejoice at my deliverance from the late weakness & ill-

-nesse both of the Plague of Slanderous Tongues & the

faintings abated something after Dafeny Lightfoote

came to see me.

But on that day when first I Arose out of bed. I had the

sad news of my dear & tenderly loving husbands

falling sick at Mallton brought to me in a letter to my

Brother Denton . Which so suddenly surprised my spirit

yet exceeding weak. That I fell to a great trembling

with excessive grief. & fears upon me for his life, and

safety. so that I went sick thereupon to my sorrowful bed

Immediately Sending for Dr wittie to come to him. each

day & night posting thither to let me know how he did.
