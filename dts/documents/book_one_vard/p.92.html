good life, an high Esteem in his Country. & of a great

fame for virtue; & much lamented in his death.

Making a sweet & comfortable conclusion, of his life

with an abundance of Pious & Religious Expressions.

He died about the month of July: in the year 1646.

at Keeveton in the farthest part of Yorkeshire .

My Cozen Edmund Norton , Eldest son to my Uncle Norton

was married to Mr Dudly s Daughter & heir of Chopwell

in the Bishopric of Durham Mrs Jane Dudly an excellent fine

& good gentlewoman. Feb. 10th 1647. at Chopwell .

My Cosen Edmund Norton died of a Pleurisy at Yorke

the 30th of November: 1648. A gentleman of a sweet good

disposition to all, obedient & dutiful to his parents & true

friend in time of adversity. a religious young man,

A faithful subject to his Majesty , for whom he suffered

much. he lived an honest good, sober life, doing good

to all, died Religiously, & is I hope now very happy in

peace & rest, loving a peaceable temper. & was beloved of

all that knew him & An immeasurable loss to his Parents .
