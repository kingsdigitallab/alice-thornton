And although thou hast pleased to deprive us of

his Life the guide of our ways. & head of us all.

Yet, O Lord I pray thee leave us not to our selves. to

Swerve from thy precepts. & Righteous Laws thou

hast set before us, either in thy word; or the Counsels

of our dear Parents.

And since it has pleased thee. to take my father

away. for reasons known to they own wisdom. Let

a double portion of thy Spirit. be showered down up

-pon the Head of my dear. Mother . Lord give her health

of body & Soul. strengthen her faith, & patience.

Comfort her in all her sorrows & sufferings.

Preserve her from all Evils & dangers. be thou her

Husband & guide in all her ways. And she trusting

in thee with all her ??? may never be forsaken. but blessed

with a general Supply of thy graces requisite in all her

weighty concerns. & business of this world.

That she may be preserved from the malice & Power of all

known & secret Enemies. let neither she nor us O Lord

want any thing with out the which we cannot serve thee in which

conditions thou hast called us unto. for thou alone canst

bless a little, & cause the widow's oil not to fail, & we

shall be blessed. giving us safe resting places, in the

distractions that is coming upon us in these lands. that we

may sing praises to thy holy name.
