The Articles of Agreement were according to the

President of his Fathers towards his mother . Vidz.

That All his Estate, should be passed by fine & reco

-very to enable him to entail the same upon his Issue

by me, male or female.

That his Lordship of Easte Newton then valued

at the yearly rent of 250l should be Estated on

me for A Jointure. & after the decease of the longer

liver of us two, to descend upon his sons & their

issue successively. for want of such heirs male to his

heirs females by me. &c. with out impeachment of

any manner of waste.

That Laistrop valued at the yearly rent of 160l

after the decease of his mother , married to Mr Gate

& then her Jointure. was Settled on Mr Thornton for

his life, after his decease upon his heirs male &

for default of such issue, to his daughters by me.

As for his Land at Cottingham. Richmond , called

Burne Parke the inheritance Estated as the other of L.

After a long lease made for the Provision of his

younger children's Portions & Maintenance, paid out

of it. The value wast accounted 100l Per Annum.

As for the security of my Portion he was to Reieave

the sum of 1500l out of Kirklington which he might

dispose of for himself & his own use. Being secured to me

And for the 1000l payable out of Ireland . he gave

bond to my mother to purchase Land of inheritance

for me during my Life, & for my Children at my

decease.

And with all my mother was willing to give us our Table

with all our Family for 3 years. these were the terms

betwixt them.
