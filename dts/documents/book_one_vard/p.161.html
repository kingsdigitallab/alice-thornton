Noble soul with all, lived in great peace, tranquillity, &

Charity, full of meekness, humility, Chastity, Modesty

sobriety & gravity; yea was she endowed with great wisdom

in the constant course of her life, of a sweet & pleasant com-

-posure of spirit, not sullenly sad; nor vainly light

but of an Excellent Temper in soul & Body, neither of

them wanting those due ornaments, which might make

her lovely in the Eyes of God & man, And indeed Exact

-ly studious to advance the interest of her duties, in Piety

& Religion, in her self and all her Children, whose care

was very sedulous for their souls' happiness, as well as

the embellishments of their Persons; desiring to yield her

accounts to God in Righteousness & truth. according to the

sincerity of her soul in his service.

Thus were wee happy. & Blessed; that wee are Children

& offspring of such a holy & sanctified a Couple; whom

God Almighty had filled with such a measure of his Spirit

making them great ornaments of Religion.

After my dear fathers decease she lived his Widow

till her death; which was the space of 19 years & 7 days;

dieing in that same month of December & in old Age.

But she was not one of those that lived in pleasure, or

spent her days in vanity ; for what time could be spair

red from works of necessity, & duty, to her Children, &

family, All the rest was given to the service of her God,

either as works of Piety. & devotion; in private & Public

or Charity towards her brethren, whom she saw did stand

in need, & necessity. Especially having a due regard, &

compassion upon those Clergy, which through the Rigour

of those times of oppression, were banished from their own

homes; wanting all manner of relief, with their families

very often, & frequently found the bowels of a good sam-

-aritaine in hers; she opening her Arms, to receive Christ

in his poor members, Accounting it a great happiness
