And now most graciously hast strengthened miraculous

ly thy servant to bring forth this sweet babe. though

with much hazard to her self & me, yet o Lord thine

Almighty power did alone work this great Miracles &

wonders in me & my Infant . O Lord to the hon.or of thy

Name be all glory ascribed that hast vouchsafed to

hear the desires of my soul, & bitterness of my dollers &

anguish, when I lifted up my cryings with my voice

uttered in sad distresses.

There is none able to doe as thou dost; who hast made

heaven & Earth & all things therein, on thee dependest

all things, & with thy outstretched Arm thou upholdest

all things that are. Holy is the Lord most high which has had

regard to his weak handmaid. O Lord my mouth is

filled with thy praise. Let me be ever in thy sight a than-

kfull grateful creature, And that thou maist receive

glory of men & Angels for this mercy to me.

I most humbly beseech thee, o merciful father, since

there is none besides thee who killest & makest alive let

let our lives be given to me & this infant for a prey. to be

a blessing to our generations. Sanctify & bless her from

the womb & she shall be blessed. Lord let her be a vessel

of glory to thy name & much comfort to her parents.

make her to be filled with wisdom, Chastity. holiness

& Piety to the Lord all her days, who hast had compassion

on her in this her tender Age & birth sparing her life

And Lord let me never be unthankful for these thy

infinite compassions & mercies with all thy former favours

at all times in all dangers & distresses.
