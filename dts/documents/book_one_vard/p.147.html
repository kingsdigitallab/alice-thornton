Elizabeth Thornton s death; the 5th of September 1656.

It pleased God to take from me, my deare childe betty

which had bin long in the rickets & consumption gotten at

first by an Ague & much gone in the Rickets which I concsed

was caused by ill milk at 2 nurses , And did notwithstand-

-ing all the means I used & had her with Naly at St Mun

gnos well . for it she grew weaker & at the last in a most

desperate Cough that destroyed her lunges, she died.

Elisabeth Thornton , my 3d Child died the 5th of september

1656. betwixt the hours of 5. & 6. in the morning. her Age was

one year 6. months & 21 days. was buried the same day at

Catterick by Mr Siddall .
