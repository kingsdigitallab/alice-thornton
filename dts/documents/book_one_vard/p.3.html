3

The dedication.

I'll dedicate my Soul unto my God. My Childhood, non-Age, youth is by his Rod To be directed. his Staff to uphold, My Age, & riper years; till it has told, The gracious goodness of our blessed God. What he has don for me, who by his Word, Raised my drooping Spirits often times Pardoned my Sins, delivering me from crimes. And by his Blood Shed purchased Heaven. For humblest Souls. his Grace has given. The gift is free, nor can wee merit. Ought of our selves for to Inherit, But what by the fall is made our own, The wages of Sins, Damnation. Then oh my Soul doe not decline, This Heavenly Pilgrimage divine. Rise up my heart to Heaven above, And let thy Lord, now prove thy love. Spring up a maine & let his holy Spirit, Give thee a Crown of Glory to Inherit.
