answer, much saving that I could rather 1000 times have

lost my life then to be robed of those inestimable Jewels

as the hon.or of my dear & blessed Parents , & friends: And

that I hoped God would judge my cause & theirs. against all

these slanders of the Devil's inventions. & it like to have

cost my Life then: had I not had comfort in God, and the testimony

of my own Conscience to bear me witness, of mine &

their innocencies: And I was so highly concerned to hear

those holy persons graves defiled by unjust calumnies which

had they bin nipped in the bud there might have bin redemption

but from the first raisers, but now they had don me the most

highest act of malice to conceal these lies which they laid

upon one Mary Breaks , which was gon to Richmond : & they

requited me thus by there abominable abusing my Charity

in relieving her under my Roof. Making themselves

instruments in my persecution under pretence of the accu-

sing my maid Hanna . which yet was endeavoured to be drawn

into the plots against me. But these highest aggravations of

miseries don by my own bosom friend, is more treacherous

& ungrateful then I can express. & had almost broke

my heart to be thus dealt with by her that knew my Innocency

which shows she had a mind to revenge some Peake against

us to hear her husbands family thus abused & sat as

a Judge to condemn but not rightly to determine.
