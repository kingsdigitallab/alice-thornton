It pleased God she continued till Saturday ; About

noon she spoke to my uncle Norton , & recommending

My self & all her Children to his care with much

good prayers for him & his she then took her leave

of him. About 4 a clock my Aunt Norton came to

see her, when she saluted her gladly, biding her well

-come dear Sister, what comfort is it to me to see my

dear & hon.red husbands Sister with her at that time

there ever having bin a strict league of affection &

friendship betwixt them, she was then come to see her make

her last end; & Scene of her life whom she had known

near 40 years. & so took her solemn farewell. of her

I forgot to declare that about wednesday before. she called

for her Last will. it being made a year before that And

made it be read all over before her; confirming, and

Ratifying the same, & publicly declared the same to be

her last will & Testament before my husband & my

self & Many other witnesses, making the same to be

endorsed on the back of her Will &c.

To return to her last actions in this Life, About

6 a clock at night this sweet Saint of God began

to be speechless; having still all that time employed that

Tongue in nothing but prayers, Praises & Petitions to

God. with most heavenly Spiritual & pathaticall

recommendations of her self to the Lord ever saying

Come Lord Jesus make haste & receive my Soul

& at the last immediately before her speech failed

Lord Jesus into thy hands I commend my Spirit.

And when it failed still lifting up to heaven her

Eyes & hands to God. And Dafeny perceived she

drew her breath short & going to depart prayed her

That She would give them that was with her a some

signs that she found the comfort of Gods Spirit in her

Soul; with a taste of the Joys of Heaven;
