This was the beginning of troubles in our Family

after which followed the breaking out of the Rebellion of

Ireland , being about 9 months after my father

died in October 23d 1641.

Meditations & Prayer upon the Death of my

Hon.red Father the Lord Deputie Wandesforde .

Isaiah

57

v. 1st 2d The righteous perisheth & no man laieth it to

heart; & the merciful men are taken away, none

considering that the righteous is taken away from the

evil to come.

He shall enter into peace: they shall rest in their

beds, each one walkeing in his uprightness.

Death is the common lot of all mankind, since that

fatal fall of Adam. & so none can be exempted, can

be free from that sting of Sin, which comes into this world.

A Land of troubles. & a place for tears, & sorrows,

yea A Chaos of confusion. Job: saith Man that is borne of

a woman hath but a short time here. It is Appointed

for all men once to die &c. Again, death passes upon

all men. So then it is not in any's Power to be immortall

here, be they never so Great, & Eminent, full of Honore

Wisdom, Riches. graces, and endowments.
