52

Fulfilling thy will here upon Earth, a few days

which thou hast appointed for us. to live. that at the last

wee may live with thee for ever.

And Lastly O Lord most holy & gracious I yield

thee all humble & hearty thanks and praise.

for all those favours & mercies. graces & gifts. with

the Blessings of this Life. & those spiritual. together

with the happy opportunities thou gavest to my

dear father . to serve thee in his generations. by

A happy success in all his weighty Callings

The good Providence. & prudence by which he added

more improvements to his Estate then his Progenators

for his Justice, uprightness. mercifulness, Charity

to his Relations & strangers. in general.

for the favour & good will thou gavest him in his

own & a strange country. And for all the good wher

ever thou enabled him to doe, either for our Souls

or Bodies. These blessings, I humbly acknowledge

did alone proceed from thy divine goodness from

whence comes every good & perfect gift.

Therefore O Lord I beseech accept my gratitude, & praise

for all. And as thy mercy has bin so plentifully

bestowed to this family. add this one thing more to

us. That all thy dispensations may be sanctified to us

In giving us grace to live accordingly. & may be a

holy people. as the redeemed of the Lord.
