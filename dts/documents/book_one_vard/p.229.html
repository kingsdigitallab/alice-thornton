The birth of my 9 child was very Perillous to me, & I

hardly escaped with my life. falling into pangs of Labour

about the 4th of no. being ill. contineuing that week. And on

Munday the 11 of November 1667. I fell in travell, being

delivered betwixt the hours of 10 & 11 a clock at night

I was to my own apprehension & others, never nearer

Death. by the midwives inforcement of the Child so violently

which caused a grand dislocation of the back & reines by the

inexpressable torments I endured: But in the midest of

my distresses & anguish of Soul & boyie,

[word] I.e. bodie.

The great and

Powerfull Lord God pittied my soul & had compassion on

me at that time, most Miraculously & wonderfully preser

-ving me from death when I was stept into the grave

even these 10 times, nay million of times may I give

give thanks. unto the Lord. who turned my sorrows into

Joy. that no death has had the upper hand of me still.

O my soul love thou the Lord & Praise him & magnify

him for ever. delight in him all thy life. he is thy Joy

thy Life. salvation & refuge. I called unto him in my

distress & he heard & delivered me out of the Pitt of

destruction & death. preserving me from fears & torments

Thou art O Lord the guide of my youth. the succorrour in my

sorrows. thou caires for me, because thy mercy is in-

finitt & uncearchable. & I have depended on thee ever

since I was borne. and put my trust in thee alone. O

Most hight. let me still have thee for my God in the ^ sweet

influences of thy Power & mercy by which the sonnes of men

are upheld & delivered. that we might magnify thy

glory & praise for ever. Thou hast given me a goodly

strong & sweet son, to mittigate those sorrows. &

addeing the blessings of the breasts, to that of the womb. O

Praise the Lord with me for ever. Amen.

It pleased the Lord to give another mercy that night

for my daughter Alice with fear & grief for me fell so

sick in my Labour that she was in much danger of death

but blessed be the Lord which preserved her then & recovered

her from that illnesse. the next day. no. 12, 67

Christopher Thornton my 9th Child was borne at newton

on munday the 11 no. 67 Baptized the 12th at newton.
