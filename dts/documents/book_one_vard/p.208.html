That they should be delivered in keeping to Mr Comber

Haueing Entrusted all the Chief Concerns & Evidences

for my dear Children with in a little Red Leather Trunk

Locked. I delivered the same to him according to our agrement

Laying a great charge & injunction: with my earnest desire

that he would keep that Trunk safe. and if it pleased

God to take me; then to deliver the same with his own hand

to my Lord: as I had charged the same to George Light

foote & his wife Dafeny with it: in my sickness of my son

I bore at St Nickolas ; after my mothers death:

for in it was contained all Children's Provision & sub-

sistance: & if any thing should happen to that Trunk they

might be Ruined:

It pleased my gracious Lord God to give me space: & time

to doe all these things as well as I could; after which I fell

into travail being one day & night in Travail. of

My Eight Child , & then it pleased the Lord to make me

happy in a goodly strong Child a daughter after an

exceeding sharpe & perilous time. being in the same con-

dittion of weakness after I came into bed & of my son

Robert , which I escaped very narrowly the blow of Death

But by the providence of God I was prepared with a remedy

which prevented the extremity, & with in 14 days I began

to be in a hopeful condition of recovery. Blessed be the

great & gracious Father of mercies. he also giving me

strength & the blessing of the breasts to give suck, with much

comfort in my Infant , with my dear husband & Children

who then had increased my number to 4 sweet Children
