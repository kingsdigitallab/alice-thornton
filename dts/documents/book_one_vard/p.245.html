Be not a witness against thy neighbour with out cause:

& deceive not with thy lips. Too sadly have I experienced

this Pro. 25: 18, 19. Confidence in an unfaithful man in

time of trouble, is like a broken tooth & a Sd foot out

of joint. A man that beareth false witness against his

Neighbour, is a maule, & a sword, & a sharpe Arrow.

Yet such has bin my hard, miserable fortune to meet with all

in this time of my life, when the other heavy afflictions & trials

from God lay upon my self, dear husband & Children.
