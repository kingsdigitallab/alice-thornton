Who though her years were but young being 14th then

yet such was his great affection towards her; that he was

desirous to attend for her 7 years. if he might by his leave

obteine her at last: having, discerted all other opportunitys

for her sake & the favours he had from him. And also that what

ever her fortune was. It should be wholey for her to use & hers.

with all he did not dispaire, but that God who had Preservd

him, hitherto would provide a hansome compotency; & to mak

a better provision for My daughter . by adding to her own.

This propossittion was answered by a fair respect of Mr Th.

side & he was incouraged to proseed.

Nor could I be satisfied in a business of this nature. on which

might depend the wellbeeing of our Family. with out A serious &

deep consideration of all our Affairs, & the condition that our Estate

was in; which being well known to my Good Brother denton ,

more then to any in the world. I consulted, and advised, with

him allalang in this transaction.

Whose prudence & discression, was a great support to Mr Th.

& my self in all our business & Actions. he also having had

knowledge & acquaintance of this Gentleman for several yers

might the better judge of his life & conversation, together with his

qualifications for the great & high calling of the Ministery.

Which in it self considered carries allong with it. the most noble

Title that man is capable of in this world, & wherein the highest,

Acts of Piety & Religion is vested. being hon.ord by God

himself in the old & new Testament. Above all other Dignitys

& having the greatest opportunity of drawing near to the Throne

of Grace from whence proseeds every good & perfect gift.

shewing to us mortals the way to Everlasting Life.

After the consideration of all these motives as Principally to be

regarded. I was in the next place; confirmed in our resolves to

proceed in this buisnesse for my Daughter whom I hoped to

be placed near us & her friends at stongrave being our own

Church; where I might have better advantage for my sonne

Robert & his sisters instruction.

Also I perceived by this last unsettling of that Estate from

my Daughters & yonger Children. they were brought into an

Incertainty for there Provision. which might fall too heavily

out for them. & be the worse for them after my Deceace.
