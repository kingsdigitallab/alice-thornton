49

Mercies to them that loves thy Majesty through thy

dear Son Jesus Christ our Saviour. I thy poor servant

sinful dust & Ashes, doe beseech thee, to make me truly

& sincerely humbled, & repentant for those Sins

& frailties, disobedience, to thy Commandments &

holy Laws, & childish follies of what nature so ever

that has offended thy pure Eyes. & caused thy Majesty

to be displeased, & to take my dear father from my

head. In whose wellfaire & life, my good & happiness

in this life did consist. And very much conduced too

my Eternal consernes, & good of my poor ignorant

Soul. By whose Providence. care & wisdom I have

bin upheld, succoured & maintained under thee

ever since I was borne. Injoyeing many blessings

& opportunitis of learning, to know & serve thee,

being taught by him, & my dear Mother what

things are necessary for my Salvation. he being

a grand instrument of good to all us his children.

O Lord forgive all our follies & Sins against thy Self

& him. pardon all our iniquities for the Lord Jesus Christ his

sake. Sanctify our hearts. & amend our lives by thy

grace & Spirit. And sanctify this great loss &

Affliction to me, & us all. learning us by thy corrections

to know thy power & serve thee with fear & love.

That thou maist return with a blessing in the keeping safe

my dear mother . & that our Sins Might not provoke thee
