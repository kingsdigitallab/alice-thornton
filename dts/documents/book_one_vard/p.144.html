Sutable to her sufferings: Send to thy poor Afflicted

Servant a safe deliverance, of a comly fruit of her

womb, of what Sex seemeth best to thy devine-

wisdome, Onely we beseech thee grant that it may be an

Elect Vessell to Salvation that may live to be received in

to thy Church Millitant by holy Baptisme. And so

long after as thou hast appointed for the praise of

thy great name & a comfort of its Parents, further

wee are humble sutors to thy Majesty, to direct and

assist thy servant the midwife, that she may faithfully

discharge her office to the mother and the child, with

prudent wisdom and tenderness. bless her with

health & strength to finish this great work as thou

hast don heeretofore, for many more, for which great

mercy both she, & wee here present doe praise thy

holy name, & doe likewise pray unto thee O Lord, to

bless our labours in the behalfe of this our sick Sister,

by directing us all to those actions as may be most

proper for her assistance. But O Lord wee confesse our

selves to be very great sinners in thought, word &

deed, we doe unfeynedly bewale our Iniquities the

rememembrances of them is grievous, the burthen

of them is intolerable O Lambe of God have Mercy up

-pon us, & wash them away with thy most precious

blood; here & accept these our penitent prayers for

our Souls, & this thy Languishing Servant in perill of death

in Childebirth, Oh most Merciful God; Lay not hir Sins

to her charge, but according to the multitude of thy tender

mercies doe away her offences, and accept her tears of

repentance & cryes in Agony of Spirit for a safe &

speedy deliverance. And Lastly O Lord most gracious

I humbly submitt my self to thy most blessed will

& pleasure, either for Life or death as it most Propor-
