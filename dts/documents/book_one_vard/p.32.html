And finished a good while before. he had it then

all read over to him in the presence of divers Persons

of quality. as my Lord Bishop of Derrey an Executor

the Earle of Ormond ; the Lord Dillon , & several others

Before, whom he Ratified & confirmed the same

declaring it publicly to be his last Will &

Testament.

Commanding his Executor to see it fulfilled, & per

formed, to my mother , & all his Children. & that

all his Just debts, whether by bill, or bond, &

justly proved, should be paid; again charged

them to be careful of his wife, & children.

About wednesday, my mother desired the Physicians

to give her a true state of his condition, whom

she perceived grew weaker. but they would

not deal truly, nor acknowledge his desperate

case. Albeit they found by his blood that it was

corrupted, & most fatal signs.

That night Pigeons cut, was laid to his soles

of the feet, when my father saw it, he smiled, &

said, are you come to the the last remedy. but I shall

prevent your Skill. for all along this sickness

he expected his change, although he would not

acquaint my mother for increasing her grief

All the time of his Sickness, till the last period.
