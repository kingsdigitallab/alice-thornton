After which humble petitions it Pleased our gracious Father

to grant our requests in a great measure for my pains of

travail increased, and although I endured hard Labour

I was delivered the next day.

Katherine Thornton my fourth child was borne at Hips--well

near Richmond in Yorkeshire : the 12th of June, 1656. being

on thursday, about half an hour after 4 a clock in the

afternoone, and was baptized the 14th of June. by Mr Siddall

wittnesses, my mother , my neece Katherine Danby & Mr

Thornton .

A Thanksgiving after the delivery of my,

Daughter Alice Thornton of her being my fourth child.

June 12th 1656. Baptized the 14th

O most holy, Just and Merciful Lord God, who hast

Created all things in Heaven and earth for thy Glory,

Preserved them by thy Power, and hast appoynted man

kind to increase and multiply for the Propagation of thy

Church Millitant and increase of thy Church Triumphant;

Wee thy poor sinful Creatures, Dust and Ashes doe reno

-wnce all worthinesse in ourselves to approach before thy

infinite Majesty, & therefore doe most humbly present our

prayers, praises, and thanksgiveings unto thee in the name

mediation & Intercession of thy dearely
