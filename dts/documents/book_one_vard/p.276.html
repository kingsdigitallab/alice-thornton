A double proportion, of thy holy Spirit, to be my

Guide into the way of the truth, and an Illuminations of

my heart & stepes in thy ways, & a direction in this

my sadest Pilgramage; give me thy word to be a

Lanthorne to my paths, & a light unto my ways

that I tread not the stept of Death. Neither let me go down

into the darke, nor my life into the place where all things

are forgotten. Though thou hast pleased to afflict me soe

sore in the loss of my dear husband & the comforts I in

joyed in him yet let me plead thy gracious promise.

& be thou the husband to the widow & a Father to the

Fatherlesse Children; & desolate.

make me thankful for so long injoyment, as also

for those eminent gifts & Graces, which thou in mercy had

indowed him with; To thee be the Hon.or given, due unto thy

Name, & o Lord give me the power of thy grace, & strength

to immitate his virtues, which was called to thy service in

our youths, & from our youths by thy miraculous Provid

ence preserved from those inormities which hundreds comit

to their own perdittion. O give me therefore now thy

perservering grace to the end of my days; & that I may take

up thy Cross dailie & follow thee, being meeke & lowlie

in Spirit submissive with a true & Catholique resignation

to all thy wise dispensations seeme they to cross my

perverse will never so much give me patience through

out, in all the course of my Life, faith, true wisdom, hope

& Charitie. Let me not lacke, any thing, which may add

orne my Soul in thy Sight, making it lovely in the sight

of my Redeemer, who purchased it with his bloodshed

write in my heart a new name sequestred as much as

may be from this world. & all its delights & vanities.

Lord give me grace to continue a pure mind in a Chaste

body cleansed by thy precious blood. And if it be

thy good pleasure to continue me yet a while longer

to doe thee service here, give me my Life for a prey, & keep

me from sins of presumtion, that they never get dominion

over me I had miritted destruction long erre this.
