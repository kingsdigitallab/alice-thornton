All his children infinitely happy, & blessed, in such a

Father & guide, in their youths. his Relations

friends & Tenants, were all blessed in him, doing

them what good he could. Let his rare & Excellent

Book of advice to his sonne George , Speak his great

Endowments. his Piety, knowledge in Parts, Divinity, &

Religion. his Wisdom & paternal care &

prudence, tender & dear love to his whole Family

& generation.

A grand Patron of the Church, & encourager of all

ingenious Scholars of what Age or degree soever . An in

couragement. & exemplar of Learning. Sobriety

Temperance; chastity; holiness, patience; humility

Charity; Justice & clemency was this Heroic

Soul replenished with all.

Rich in good works. lovely & desirable in his life

a dear & loving Brother to his brothers & Sisters. taking care

for their advantage in Education, & preferment

as branches from the same stock with himself. he had

A wise & prudential love, towards all his Children
