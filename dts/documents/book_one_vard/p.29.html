All his children infinitly happy, & blessed, in such a

Father & guide, in their youths. his Relations

friends & Tennants, were all blessed in him, doing

them what good he could. Let his raire & Excellent

Book of advice to his sonne George , Speak his great

Endowments. his Piety, knowlidge in Parts, Divinity, &

Religion. his Wisdom & paternall care &

prudence, tender & dear love to his whole Family

& generation.

A grand Patron of the Church, & incourager of all

ingenious Schollers of what Age or degree soever. An in

couragement. & exemplear of Learning. Sobriety

Temporance; chastety; holiness, patience; humility

Charity; Justice & clemency was thys Heroicke

Soul replenished with all.

Rich in good workes. lovely & desirable in his life

a dear & loving Brother to his brothers & Sisters. taking care

for their advantage in Education, & preferrment

as branches from the same stocke with himself. he had

A wise & prudentiall love, towards all his Children
