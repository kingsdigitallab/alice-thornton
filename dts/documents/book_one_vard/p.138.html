This ill fit hindered my milk much. but yet affter

-wards I recruited fast. & within a fortnight had gotten

the milk again into my breasts & my deare babe Betty

did suck every day of me & I was over joyed in that

great blessing. But it is usually so with me that I have

my comforts mixed with sadness. & many times my in

-joyments are woven with crosses. & sorrows.

For at the fortnights end. I was sitting in a chair & giving

my child suck, when immediately one of the maids

cried out of the nursery that My childe Naly was either

dead or dieing, which so affrighted me being but weak

that an Illness cam in force on me as I sat. & there was much

to doe to get me safe in to my bed, & there kept, betwixt

hopes & fears for my self & child 2 days. or more.

but at the length. through exceeding much mercy &

love of my God he brought us both from those weakness

& I recovered strength in time. and Naly had an

eye Tooth which broke flesh that had bin the cause of all that

danger to her. and often afterwards when her teeth

was cutting the gums she was ill again till she had

got them all.

But this second weakness of my own did so dis-

-courage my dear mother , that she would not let me

give suck, although I extremely desired it, & at

the months end I was forced to dry my breasts which grew

full & had endangered to bring me ill again.

And Dafeny Lightfoote gave my betty suck. till she

proved with child: which afterwards was the cause I put her

to another at about 3 quarter of a year old; but she did not deal

well with my child which was a most stately lovely girl

when she took her to nurse. nor was I able to go so

often then to see her because it was a mile off Hipswell

& I was then with child, so that I durst not adventure a

great walk or to ride when I grew so big.
