Considering that what he now enjoys he would not Exchange.

for ten thousands worlds. & thy present loss is his Eternal

advantage. as St Paul saith for me to live is Christ but

to die is gain. O desire to be made happy with him in the

Resurrection. when we shall appear together & be clothed

with Immortality, enjoying the fruition of that God head

as David Says in thy presence is fullness of Joy. & pleasure

at thy right hand for ever more.

And I may say with Job. Although worms consume this

body: yet with my Eyes shall I see God & beholding him

face to face. which this clayey body of dust cannot enjoy in

this vaile of flesh. till this vile body be changed & made

like his & this mortal put on immortality.

Oh that the Lord would now give me faith. & show himself to

his weak handmaid & servant making me to believe

what good things is laid up for them that love & fear him

And though great are the troubles of the Righteous here. yet

are they but in order to consign them to a better Kingdom

& he will deliver them out of all. Oh doe not then

repine or call in question the mercy & goodness of this

great wise. holy & gracious Father, for if thou belongest to

him he will give thee to know his mind. It is the Lord he

must doe what he will.

Although this dispensation is most bitter, as the cutting of

thy life. be silent, & doe not sin against him by impatience

or resisting his pleasure. has not the heavenly Potter power o-

over our Earthly clay, to doe what seemest him best.

What if flesh & blood cannot part so willingly. Remember

thou must live by faith. & say unfeignedly thy will be don.

in all things if ever thou expect salvation hereafter.

he was prepared for death. Thou art not, perhaps he has

some other work for thee to doe in this troublesome world. Pray

that thou might be assisted to perform his pleasure. and

keep thee in a continual preparedness. And that as we did

help in this Life to bear one another burdens. so may we

together Reign with Christ in Glory who is the Author & finisher

of our faith. Daily panting & breathing after him. to be
