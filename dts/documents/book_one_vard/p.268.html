Considering that what he now injoyes he would not Exchange.

for ten thousands worlds. & thy present loss is his Eternall

advantage. as St Paull saith for me to live is Christ but

to die is gaine. O desire to be made happy with him in the

Resurection. when we shall apeare together & be clothed

with Immortality, injoying the fruittion of that God head

as David Sayes in thy preasence is fullness of Joy. & pleasur

at thy right hand for ever more.

And I may say with Job. Although wormes consume this

body: yet with my Eyes shall I see God & beholding him

face to face. which this clayie body of dust cannot enjoy in

this vaile of flesh. till this vilde body be changed & made

like his & this mortall put on immortallity.

Oh that the Lord would now give me faith. & show himself to

his weak handmaid & servant making me to believe

what good things is laid up for them that love & fear him

And though great are the troubles of the Righteous here. yet

are they but in order to consigne them to a beter Kingdom

& he will deliver them out of all. Oh doe not then

repine or call in question the mercy & goodness of this

great wise. holy & gracious Father, for if thou belongest to

him he will give thee to know his mind. It is the Lord he

must doe what he will.

Although this dispensation is most bitter, as the cutting of

thy life. be silent, & doe not sin against him by impatience

or resisting his pleasure. has not the heavenly Potter power o-

over our Earthly clay, to doe what seemest him best.

What if flesh & blood cannot part so willingly. Remember

thou must live by faith. & say unfeinedly thy will be don.

in all things if ever thou expect salvation heereafter.

he was prepared for death. Thou art not, perhaps he has

some other work for thee to doe in this troublesom world. Pray

that thou might be assisted to performe his pleasure. and

keep thee in a continuall preparednesse. And that as we did

help in this Life to bear one another burdens. so may we

together Reigne with Christ in Glory who is the Author & finisher

of our faith. Daily panting & breathing after him. to be
