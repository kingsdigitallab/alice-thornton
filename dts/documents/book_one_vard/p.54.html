A Prayer to be said before wee Receive.



Made by my father . before he went into.

Ireland 1628.

O Eternal. Omnipotent, & most Merciful Father

thou that openest, & no man shutteth, & shuttest

and no man openeth, let the Words of our mouths

& the Meditations of our hearts. be acceptable in thy

sight this time and for ever more.

O Lord God, by whom wee were created at the

first, by whom wee now live move & have our

being, Look not upon us as wee were placed at the

first, by thy divine Power, in the State of Per-

-fection, adorned with those Heavenly qualities

of knowledge & freedom of Will, whereby wee

had some remsemblance of thee our Great Creator.

Neither as wee lye in the loins of our first Parents

who, by eating that sower grape hath set an Edge

upon our teeth. Much Less as wee are defiled with

our own Pollutions. & uncleanness. But behold us

we beseech thee, in that State which thy divine Pro-

-vidence, through the Redemption of thy Son & our

Saviour Christ Jesus hath prepared for our Resto-

-ration, unto that happiness from whence we are fallen

that so wee who were altogether dead of our selves

, may by him be recovered unto that Everlasting Re-

-surection, which thou (that wishest the Perfection & continu

ance of what thou hast made). hast prepared for us before

the begining of the world, If wee fear thee & keep.
