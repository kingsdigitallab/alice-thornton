Thus did the Lord deliver my dear father . in this

way. the best of all, most certainely it was, for

there the weary be at rest, & the wicked cease from trou

bling. He died, & was gathered to his Fathers in a

quiet & peaceable time. As he lived in Peace so

he departed in Peace, & to Peace, giveing him rest in his

Sleep, the sweet sleep of death to him, though sad,

to us, he left behind him.

It followes in the 2d v. He shall enter into peace:

they shall rest in their beds, each one walking in

his uprightnesse.

When the Righteous man leaves this turbulent

world, whither, shall he go, or what place, has

God prepaired for him. lo the Spirit of God tells

us, by his prophett, here the place. He shall enter

into peace. they shall rest in their beds.

And in this change, of my father was fullfilled

this prophecie; which had bin confirmed by our dear

Saviours, blessings to the Peacemakers; The poor in

Spirit: for theires is the kingdom of Heaven. to the

meeke: To the merciful. for they shall obtaine mercy

To the pure in heart: for they shall see God. the peace

makers: for they shall be called the children of

God.
