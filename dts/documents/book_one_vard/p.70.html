1643

But the wars falling out hot at that time, being wee

were beleaguered in Chester by Sir will.m Brewerton s

forces for the Parliament & there happened a strange

accident which raised that Siege July. 19th 1643

As I was informed; there was 3 grenadoes shot in

to the town but through providence hurt nobody.

The first, being shot into the Sconce of our Soldiers.

with in 2 men of the Captaine Manwaring . but having

an Oxes hide ready, clapped it there on. & it smot-

-hering away in shells did not spread. but went

out. The 2d light, short of the City , in a ditch, with in

a Pasture amongst a company of women milking

but was quenched with out doing them harm at all.

Praised be the Lord our God. The Last fell amongst

their own horse, short of the town . slaying many

of them, & by that means the Siege was Raised.

thus was we freed from great evils to befall that

town, while wee staid there. that succeeded after

we came into yorkeshire . which still the Lords hand was

Stretched out for our Preservation in each place

we came to it was a sanctuary to us, blessed be the Lord

most height for all his goodness towards us:
