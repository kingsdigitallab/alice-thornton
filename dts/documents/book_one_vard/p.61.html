Help us wee also humbly beseech thee dear feather, to

punish our worldlie sins, of covetousness, Ambition

Pride, thirsting after the outward comodities & advan-

-tages of this World, By making restitution of what

wee have got contrariely to thy lawe: by being

compassionate & bountifull to our fellow members;

Then, shall wee by thy goodness, not only be freed from

the imputation & present guilt where with, our fore

past lives have now defiled us, but bestow our

future course of lives, in walking the ways of Godlines

going from grace to grace, from good work to good

Work, till wee attaine to that measure of perfection, which

thou hast apointed to us, during the time of our

Earthly Pilgrimage here.

And lastly, Most gracious, & Merciful Father.

Wee are humble sutors unto thy heavenly Majesty: that

wee may be assisted, with thy divine & gratious Spirit.

in the dutifull ordering our selves, before during the

time. and after the receaving of this blessed Banquett;

That before, with all the forces & facculties of our fraile

Mindes, we may Extoll thy goodness & wisdom in

sending thy onlie Son to Redeeme us, that at that

time we may magnify his obedience to thee, & love to us

for shedding out his precious Blood for us that were thine

& his Enemies: And after being clensed with that

Blessed Lavacre, be cairefull to keep our selves unde-

-filed that so now wee may by his grace be clensed:

here after by his favour preserved, & at the last by his
