Help us wee also humbly beseech thee dear feather, to

punish our worldly sins, of covetousness, Ambition

Pride, thirsting after the outward commodities & advan-

-tages of this World, By making restitution of what

wee have got contrarily to thy law: by being

compassionate & bountiful to our fellow members;

Then, shall wee by thy goodness, not only be freed from

the imputation & present guilt where with, our fore

past lives have now defiled us, but bestow our

future course of lives, in walking the ways of Godliness

going from grace to grace, from good work to good

Work, till wee attain to that measure of perfection, which

thou hast appointed to us, during the time of our

Earthly Pilgrimage here.

And lastly, Most gracious, & Merciful Father.

Wee are humble suitors unto thy heavenly Majesty: that

wee may be assisted, with thy divine & gracious Spirit.

in the dutiful ordering our selves, before during the

time. and after the receiving of this blessed Banquet;

That before, with all the forces & faculties of our frail

Minds, we may Extol thy goodness & wisdom in

sending thy only Son to Redeem us, that at that

time we may magnify his obedience to thee, & love to us

for shedding out his precious Blood for us that were thine

& his Enemies: And after being cleansed with that

Blessed Lavacre, be careful to keep our selves unde-

-filed that so now wee may by his grace be cleansed:
