Establishment of this poor family in truth & holiness

& that even then; when all was hatched against me. with what

sorrow & affliction I lived in upon the griefs for my dear

husbands weakness. & the troubles of his Estate. God knows

but I leave her to the just, Judge & commit my cause to

his holy determination before whose Tribunal we must

all appear. And whose dread I set before my Eyes from

my youth: & whose mercy has never left me, never since

I was borne, but he has kept me from the strivings of the

ungodly: I may say as hannah did, Talk no more so ex

ceeding proudly, let not Arrogance proceed out of your

mouth. for the Lord is a God of knowledge; and by him

actions are weighed.

Well might this good woman say so; which was wronged

in the opinion of Eli and accused to be drunk. but she

said no my Lord. think not thy servant a daughter of

Belial: I am a woman of sorrowful spirit.

And even thus was I, a poor helpless creature. having

great burdens & temporal afflictions on me wrongfully abused

for the discharge of my duty, as a faithful wife; a mother

& mistress in my house; as this hannah which prayed for a

Son. And as the Lord did free this woman from the suspicion

of that sin: so I hope he will doe for me. which has never don

no more either positively, or intentionally, to deserve these

slanders. my soul being employed & desires more for the

things of another life then this. & how to meet my dearest

Saviour with a pure & holy heart;

Such was my dear Aunts care & charitable affection towards

me. seeing me in such a deplorable a condition of weakness

& grief for these infinite wrongs & slanders; that she see

I did not take any comfort in my life. & that I could not

sleep but still the frights & terror seized upon me, as if

those 2 persons which so abused me were standing ready to tear

my Eyes & as I fancied in my dreams would have killed me;

so grand an apprehension, I had of those words & actions I

have related: That my dear husband & my Aunt thought

there was almost no way left Left to take.
