at Richmond 1643.

Affer this when the scotts had helped to overthrow the Kings

Army at Yorke , for which designe they were called in to England

&, to destroy the Regall Power, of his Majesty . waiting upon

the Parliaments Motions, to fullfill the intent of the Scottish

Covenant, in Rooting out the Prelaticall Party, & the Establish

-ment of their Scottish Presbittery in the Ruin of the King

& Epicopacy. these scottch Rebells quartered them selves

All over, the Countery, Especially, in and, about Richmond .

forcing all People to take the Covenant, how contrary soe-

-ver it was to their duty of Aleagence, or conscience.

& those who would not; were forced to flie, or was impri-

-soned, & Ruined, so that my poore brother George was upon

this account compelled to live obscured, from all People, in-

regard that he would not be compelled to this treason, nor was

willing to be imprisoned, by them.
