Therefore endured I all the Rigour, & extremity of my

sickness, with such a shaire of Patience, as my God gave me.

As for my friends they were so much concerned for me

that upon the importunity of my husband , although I

was brought indeed very weak & desperately ill about,

11th day of my sickness . I did let him send for Dr wittie

if it were not too late.

The Dr came Post the next day. when he found me very

weak, & durst not, let me blood that night. but gave me

cordialls & till the next day, & if I got but one hours

rest that night he would doe it the morning following. that night

the two Drs had a dispute about the letting me blood. Mr Mahum

was against it & Dr wittie for it. but I soone desided

that dispute & told them if they would save my life

I must bleed. so the next day I had 6 or 7 ounces taken which

was turned very bad by my sickness. but I found a

change immediately in my sight which was exceding dime

before, & then I see as well as ever clearly. & my strength

began a little to return; these things I relate that I may

set forth the mercy of my ever gracious God, who had blessed

the means in such manner. who can sufficiently Extoll

his Majesty for his boundlesse mercies to me his weak Creatue
