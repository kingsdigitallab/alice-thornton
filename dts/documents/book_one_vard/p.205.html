Who had Compassion upon the vildest of his Creatures. yet

hath he heard my sorrows & granted my requests. which I

made before him; with my mouth will I ever set forth his

Maraculous Praise; & tell of his marvelous greatnesse.

from day to day; for who can doe as thou doest; there is

No God in Heaven & Earth but thee alone Most great &

holy. Let this stand as a Piller of Gratitude, which I errect

for all the deliverances & mercies. I & my Children have had

both in Soul & body. The Living shall praise thee in Heaven

& Earth for ever: Amen.

My Son Robert was baptized on Satterday the 20th of

September: 1662 by Mr Luckock at our House in Easte

Newton in my own Chamber, where the Lord gave me opportunity

to see his admittance into the Church Millittan. by holy Sacrament

of Baptisme; when I hope the Lord did enter into Covenant

with him to be his God: & he to be his faithful servant to his

lives end: The Lord give him also the Grace of this means

as well as the means by which he gives his Grace unto us. Amen

his Godfathers & Godmothers were Mr Thornton ; for my

my nephew Best . Dr wittie . My Lady Cholmely .

The God of all consolations & Comfort. Preserve his Life &

health: with happy opportunities of Religeous & holy. Educa-

tion. that he may be an instrument of great Glory to God,

Comfort to his Parents & relations. And for the building up in

Righteousness & hollinesse. his Fathers Family to Posterity

for many generations. And at the end of his life, he may

receive the comfort of a sanctified Old Age. with a Crown of

Glory to praise his Reddemer & mine for ever for our beings;

Finding what the goodness of God had beine to me in giving me

the requests of my heart; & being these mercies received by

& from a gracious father. who hath at length bestowed on

me his servant this hopes of contineuing our memories. by a

sweet son of my vowes. being obteined from God as Samuell

was by the fervent prayers & tears of a poor wreatched mothr

To thee o Lord I humbly dedicate my son of my womb.
