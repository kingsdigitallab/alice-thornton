And spared me as a man spared his only son.

O Lord let it be in mercy that thou hast preserved me in

these wonderful manners of all thy late & former de

-liverances. Make me I beseech thee a vessell fitted

for thy service here, that these signall deliverances

that thou hast showed to me in this Citty , & Ireland ,

from thy Enemies, who would have devoured us up

soone & swallowed us quick. from those imminent

dangers in the time of the seige, making me escape from

that Cannon bullett. giving me a kind affection among

-st these strangers. & those dear expressions of love

of my dear Mother , in all my weakness, & sickness

with all the means was used for my preservation.

Thou O Lord hast don it, and to the glory of thy name

I return my humble thanks & praise, where as I,

& my brother escaped, when this little boy in our house

was taken, & 100ds in this town died thereof.

O dear God since I have daily my life given me, a

new make me joyfull in thy salvation, wherein thy

great mercies are made more manifest. then to many

in the world. O let my soul be precious in thy sight

who hast delivered me with great Miracles. Sanctify me

from my youth, & all to thy service. that I may be free

from the follies thereof, & dedicate all the facculties of

my soul to thy service. Thou which hast brought me from the

womb to live to see the 15th year of my life run.

Alsoe I praise thy name principaly for thy great favours

in thy beginnings, in making thy self known to me in

the Receaveing thy most holy, & blessed Sacrament which
