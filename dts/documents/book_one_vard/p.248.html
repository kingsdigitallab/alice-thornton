I had not bin so irreparably abused: but have found

out a way to have righted my self long before I came to

the knowledge of being wronged.

But yet even this particular passage of revealing those

lies to my Brother. wee had too much cause to think them to have bin don

out of Policy against me; & insinuation to him. It did by

the mercy of God prove a greater Argument, of my wrong

and abuses; to him who had bin a daily witness of my

carriage for many years together. & those many things which

I was charged with (as crimes) he himself had bin made known

to, in every respect; & justified such actions against

my accusers : he being extremely grieved for my suffer

ings. & a faithful friend in my adversity.
