141 of Spirit Adding a new blessing to thy servant causi-

-ng me to bring forth my 3d child. of a comlie body

& streight limbes & proportion. when my soul was

even at deaths dore; thy mercy said reture.

[word] I.e., returne.

& gavest

me a new life blessed be thy holy name & merciful gooness

[word] I.e., goodness.

Oh Lord most glorious Trinity, how can I set forth

thy praise, thy glory who hast bin to me & mine pitti

-full as a father. tender as a mother. cairefull as a

guardian. & exceeding merciful to all those that call

upon thee & fear thy great name as they ought.

hast thou only cast me & my child down & not

raised us up, no, for thou upheld me by thy love &

restored us again many times by thy providence

& more perticulerly in these late extreamities when

could remove the same but thou alone.

I pray thee O my God to fill my soul with great app-

-rehensions & impresses of thy unspeakable goodness

long suffering, full of compassion & mercies that will not

alwaies be chiding for ever, nor dealt with us after our

sins; nor rewardeth me according to my wickedness

Lord has delivered me in my distress. Lord grant that

my gratitude may be as great as my needs of mercy

are. letting thy merciful loving kindness endure for

ever upon all thy servants & me thy handmaid &

on my two young children whom thou hast preserved

from death giving my little Infant life, health, & the

happy opportunity of holy Baptisme. I pray thee

dear Lord make it be consigned by grace to thy

service and a member of thy misticall body. that she may

be preserved for ever. Dear Lord keep no anger

in store for my husband. self & children, neither chide

us not in thy heavy displeasure for then we perish for

ever.
