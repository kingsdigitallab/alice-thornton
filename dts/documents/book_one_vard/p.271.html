I humbly beseech thy Majesty as thou hast given me this

being. in this Earth & contineuing the same thus long. &

hast prolonged my Life, notwithstanding all my unworthines

& sins. That thou willt pardon what ever I have offended

in thy sight ever since I was borne. pittie my infirmitties &

strengthen my weakness, & humaine frailties, heal my

Soul & love me freely make me to understand thy word

& know thee to be a God hearing Prayer, & willt dwell in the

heart of those that are holy & humble, of a contrite heart. &

Trembles at thy word. thy, Power, & Attributes. Give me

grace to Glorify thy name & submitt chearfully to this

heavy dispensation to thy handmaid. serving thee, not

with a slaveish fear. but out of a true obedienciall &

filliall duty. Love & affection, knowing that thou dost not

afflict willingly. but even in this seperation did it (I hope)

for the happienesse of my husband , by freeing him from troubles

And that thou willt thereby drawe me nearer to thy thy self

by setting my affections more on heaven above & not on Earth;

I will also give thee the Glory & praise which hast kept me from

Presumtious sins, giving me a tender heart, (I hope) for the

least offence against thee, That may indanger a seperation

betwixt my Soul & God. O Lord take me now to thy own

more immeadiate keeping & Tuittion, who hast now taken my

head; whom thou gavest to be a comfort & a guide & for whom

I bless thy name, that I enjoyed him so long. And let me not

sin in resisting thy pleasure or disputing thy Providence, who

canst doe all things. I pray thee now O Lord, bless thy

servant in this desolate Condition, whom thou hast let to

see thy various trials & afflictions. & hast bestowed so

much pains upon. here I am: Lord what willt thou have me

to doe. I resigne my will, to thy Glorious pleasure either in

life or death. make me be free from this body of sin & Death.

to serve thee with perfect freedom, of mind will, & affections

that I may be holy in Soul, body & spirit. Though I be sick

let me be sound in thee. Teach me thy Laws, & I shall Live.
