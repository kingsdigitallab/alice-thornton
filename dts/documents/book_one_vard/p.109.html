Ah-las Lord, our God, we have bin rebellious before thee, &

adding sin to iniquities, by our disputes, & disturbances

& now we have lost, a maine Piller, which preservd the peace

& quiett of us all. yea in a suddaine, & sad manner.

Oh what have we don, in displeasing, this great & dread-

full God, walked unworthy, of the mercies of so gracious

A father; whose dealings towards us, has bin in much

mercy & clemency. having preserved him & us all from

the violencys of our Enemies, & the Churches, in many great &

eminent deliverances. Yet hast thou now taken him

away, (who was the Joy of our hearts) in these sad times, to

our great discomfort. But what are we, O Lord; sinful

dust, & Ashes, in disputing thy pleasure, thy will be

don in us, & by, us, & on us in all things.
