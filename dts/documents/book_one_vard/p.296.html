It was some times a refreshment to me after I

had sitt up mauch with my dear weak mothr in

bid me walk out to Cowes with her maid to rest

my self so I used this some times. Butt this Captne

man who I curd came to me one day. Saying dere

God sake not to go out with the maides to Cowes. I

said why. he said again he was bound to tell me.
