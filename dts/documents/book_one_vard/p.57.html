Against us and our generations after us for ever.

Com.

3 But so guilty have wee bin O Lord of taking thy holy

Name in our mouths in vaine. That wee have not onlie

neglected that Duty of Honouring & reverenceing thy Glorious

Name, by invocation, prayer & thanksgiving, the acknow

-ledgment & publication of thy praises; the glorifieing

of thy holy Name upon all occassions. but by railings,

bannings, cursings blasphemies. malledictions and

imprecations wee have profaned thy Great Name, by

foreswearing, false sweareing, light & common swearing

foolish calling thee to witness, rash vowes, wiles, deceipt

, lies, & such like untruths, vainely dishonoured thy

great & glorious Name:

Com.

4 And whereas like a merciful father, thou hast provided

six days for our necessaries & reserved but one for thy

Sabboth, wherein thou hast injoyned us a frequent use

of thy worship. as well in public congregations, as by

our selves, an attentive hearing thy worde, & workes, &

meditating upon both, a frequent use of thy Saraments

A Charitable disposition, to thy poor members whom

thou hast left here amonge us. In visiting the Sick &

comforting the afflicted, & releiveing the poor; wee have

contrarilie absented our selves from thy sevice & worship

contemned thy ministrie & Sacraments. neglected the hearing

& reading of thy Scriptures, & instead of prayers. deeds of

Piety, mercy, & charitie, wee have intermedled with prophan

& ordenary affaires of those days.

Com.
