Against us and our generations after us for ever.

Com.

3 But so guilty have wee bin O Lord of taking thy holy

Name in our mouths in vain. That wee have not only

neglected that Duty of Honouring & reverencing thy Glorious

Name, by invocation, prayer & thanksgiving, the acknow

-ledgment & publication of thy praises; the glorifying

of thy holy Name upon all occasions. but by railings,

bannings, cursings blasphemies. maledictions and

imprecations wee have profaned thy Great Name, by

forswearing, false swearing, light & common swearing

foolish calling thee to witness, rash vows, wiles, deceit

, lies, & such like untruths, vainly dishonoured thy

great & glorious Name:

Com.

4 And whereas like a merciful father, thou hast provided

six days for our necessaries & reserved but one for thy

Sabbath, wherein thou hast enjoined us a frequent use

of thy worship. as well in public congregations, as by

our selves, an attentive hearing thy word, & works, &

meditating upon both, a frequent use of thy Saraments

A Charitable disposition, to thy poor members whom

thou hast left here among us. In visiting the Sick &

comforting the afflicted, & relieving the poor; wee have

contrarily absented our selves from thy service & worship

contemned thy ministry & Sacraments. neglected the hearing

& reading of thy Scriptures, & instead of prayers. deeds of

Piety, mercy, & charity, wee have intermeddled with profane

& ordinary affairs of those days.

Com.
