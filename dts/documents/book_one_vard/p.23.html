Horrible Parricides & murders. breaking forth

first in Ireland to the destruction of millions of the

Poor protestants Christians who suffered martyrdom

for their God, & his Religion.

And in England many 1000 suffered by the sword

both with their King, & for him, & the truth of

Religion there Established. & for the Laws.

But to returne to my Lord Lieutenant , while the

Pretences of Religion, so filled the ears of the Parliament

of England then sitting in the year 40 and false

suggestions of oppressions against this Noble

Earl . he was called before them to give an answer

to such Articles as his Enemies of all factions

had unjustly laid to his charge.

The whole transaction, of this business, was prose-

- cuted, with so much malice, & Rigour of his Enemies

side. And so much, wisdom, prudence, & gallantry

on the Earl's. that all the world (save his Enemies thirsting

his blood,) did admire his incomparable Wisdom &

Abilities, in his clear, & brave defence, he made for

himself, notwithstanding they gave fresh charges each

day, which he had never heard of, nor had he any time

Spared, to give in his answer, but was set upon with

new blood hounds, as their fancies pleased,
