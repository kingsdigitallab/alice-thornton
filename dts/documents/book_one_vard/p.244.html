Upon my Sad condition, & Sickness that befell me by

the Slanders Raised against me: July 20th 1668.

Notwithstanding all my true & earnest endeavours

to show my great commiseration, & Charity, affection &

love I had towards this Gentlewoman . which not only was

showed to her as my bosom friend. in whose friendship

I rejoiced & thought my self happy in being instrum-

entall to doe any thing serviceable to her in faithfulness &

affection. as well in testimonies of gratitude for what

kindness I received in my former sicknesses, & 2 Child.

; as also of her low Estate; being comd into my Sisters family

There was nothing awanting in my family, or my self

which she might not freely command. witnessed by all People

that knew my actions & would speake nothing but truth.

nor any thing displeased her. but I took it don to my self

yet had I never till now; cause to bewail my sad fortune

in her disaffection, nor felt the sad consequence of the falling

of from that League of amity & friendship which was of a long

time even after 6 years acquaintance, contracted with her

I may now see my folly. when it is too late, to bewail my

Misery; not to foresee That, Possibly, to be true; which I could

not believe. Those which serves there friends for their Private

Ends, may have them turn.
