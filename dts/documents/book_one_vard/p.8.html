A preservation in the Measles.

Of grace, & Religion instilled into me with my milk.

Therefore. shall I begin with the first mention of my

deliverances, that presents it self under the notion of

my first knowledge & remembrance; which are most

worthy of perpetual memory.

And which I hope shall not end with this life, but spring

up in my soul, to an Eternity of Hallelujahs, of praise

& thanksgiving to the Blessed Trinity forever.

Amen. Amen.

Remember to putt the Relation of my deliverance from Death

by a fall cutting my forehead at 3 years old

1629

[+] The first deliverance of that kind was in the year

1630. When I was left at Richmond under the

care, & dear, love of my beloved, Aunt Norton .

upon my father & mothers going to London .

It pleased God to bring me into a very dangerous

weakness, & sickness, upon an accident of a

Surfeit, by eating some beef which was not well boiled

, this causing an extreme vomiting, whose vi-

-olence drove me into great fever, & that into the

measles, & both, brought me so low & weak that

my Aunt . & Sarah Tomlinson (our maid) almost

despaired of my Life.

But it pleased the Lord my God in great mercy, to

hear the hearty prayers & requests, of my Aunt for me.
