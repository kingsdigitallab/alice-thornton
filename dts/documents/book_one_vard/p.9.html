9 From the Small Pox.

That I was spared from death at that time, and

by his blessing, upon the use of good means was

recovered of my health perfectly again.

Oh that I may have my life given me for a blessing.

and that I may live to the Glory of his holy name, who

hath saved me from death.

And that I may grow in Grace, & in the knowledge of Jesus

Christ our Lord. being a comfort to my dear Parents

& relations. And that I may dedicate my Childehood

youth. middle. & old Age, (if he shall spare me

so long) to his service & praise, yea even to my lives

end. & that for my Saviours sake alone. the Alpha &

Omega. Amen. I was about 5 years old. then:

A preservation in the Small Pox, 1631.

Being removed to London by my Fathers order with

my brother Christopher . I fell into the smale-pox have-

-ing taken them of him. both of us was sent into Kent

where we lodged at one Mr Baxter s. being kindly usd

with much care in that house. & by the blessing of God I

recovered very soone, nor was I very ill at that time.

I therefore will praise the Lord our God, for my preservation

that did not suffer that dissease to rage, or indanger my

life, but raised me soone again, O set forth his goodness

forever. Amen.
