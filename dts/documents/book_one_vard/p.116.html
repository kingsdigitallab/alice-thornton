1500l in our education & maintenance. as she has

declared by her own relation before wittnesses.

None of us ever having received any thing out of

that part of my fathers Estate, of Kirklington for

the same as was appoynted by my Fathers, last will

& testament. Therefore will I give glory to our God

on high which still has preserved this dear & tender

Mother . there by testifieing his miraculous favours

to the desolate widow, & Children, in all times of

desersion & troubles. And being mindfull of his

Servant my father, in the blessing powred down upon

his Family. The Lord our God make us ever gratfull

and thankful to his gracious, Majesty for Ever. Amen.

My Cosen mary Yorke , was married to Mr John York
