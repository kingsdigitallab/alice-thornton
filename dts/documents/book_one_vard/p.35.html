And that in him he hoped for Eternal Glory, of his

own free Grace, & mercy. he desired the Lord to for

-give All his Sins, as he freely forgave all the

world. And declaring that he died in the faith

which was professed in the Church of England at that time

being most pure, & holy, & agreeing with Christs

Institution. Praying to God long to continue it

flourishing.

Many other prayers for himself & his wife &

Children. desiring to be accepted of the Lord in

Mercy, according to the Sincerity of his heart.

After devout prayers for him by the Bishop & the solemn

pronunciation of absolution, in order to the Churc

-his command. This dear & sweet Saint freely

yielded up his precious Soul to God, with these

words, into thy hands oh Lord, I commend my Soul

Lord Jesus receive my Spirit Amen.

With which he fell asleep. Which blessed end of his life

being a happy close of his holy Life has I hope received

a full reward of Joy in the Kingdom of Heaven &

struck a most deep impression upon all that knew

him. I pray the Lord continue his memory fresh in

my heart to imitate his virtues, graces & Piety

he departed this life on Thursday the 3d of December

1640 at his own house in Dammaske Streete in

Dublin beeing in Ireland .
