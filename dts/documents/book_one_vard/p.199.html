I had bin further instrumentall to his assistance, in giving

Leave that my 1000l to be received out of Ireland which was

settled on me & my Children by bond before marriage: shoud

go to the satisfaction of his brothers & sisters Portions which

was not yet discharged: & Debts so that having thus disposed of

all my own Portion from my self & Children; together

with their Estate for their Provissions. I thought my

self obleiged in Conscience to desire Mr Thornton should

settle the remainder of his Reall Estate upon his Heir: if

God should give him one by me; & for the subsistance of

his younger Children, in leiue of my fortune: which was

from my father : 2500l from my mother; in Land;

Monies; Plate: goods to the valew of one thousand Pounds

besides the advantage of 8 years table: with our selves: Child

& servants stood in 200l per Annum.

upon the serious consideration of these things: I was in

much deliberation who I should get to doe this charita

-ble office for us: And upon my Adressess to the Lord my

God for direction & other pertinent Petitions my Cosen

Covill first presented my thoughts: who by Mr Thornton

& my self was sent for to Newton ; where he made such

A settlement of his Estate: as was necessary, & sattisfactory

to my husband & my self: with prudence & Equity.

as wee both did approve of & with Mr Thornton s full consent &

desire therein.
