O Let my widowed Condition be a Sanctified Estate.

yea even thou writing upon my heart Holiness unto the Lord

That I may be exemplary in my life & conversation to

thy glory & praise. My Children may be great instruments

of thy praise also. chosen vessels in this crooked Generation

Although my Afflictions be heavy & burdensome to this

weak body. yet I acknowledge them Just, as coming From

thee my strength. my hope. my Joy, & fortress. who dost

with all make away for me to Escape.

Lord be to thy servant as thou wast unto David. who served

thee with a ready mind. Thou art. the giver. The gift is

thy self. O let thy Siritt

[word] SiC ??? i.e., spirit.

dwell in me & mine Richly. let

my latter days be still a separate condition from Evil.

the Evil of sin: & the Evil of punishment as much as thou sest fit

I beseech thee preserve me & my Family from dishonourable

walking. leading us in thy ways. & directing us in our

steps. Make me to walk as an Example before them in Grace

Patience, holiness, humbleness Chastity, Piety, faith & Purity

meekness. & Charity. with a supply of all other Christian

virtues & graces. necessary to lead my life, here & to

make me acceptable in thy sight. & follow my dear savi-or

in this Pilgrimage; towards that Heavenly Jerusalem where

I may forever glorify thy majesty for all. to all Eternity

All these humble requests & Petitions I crave for the only

merits & bloodsheding of my dearest Saviour Jesus

the Righteous, who interceadeth for poor sinners at the Throne

of thy Grace, even for me, & all those that love his appearing.

To whom with the Glorious Father & holy Spirit. Ever Blessed

& Infinitely Glorious Trinity. be All possible praise.

adoration, & thanksgiving of men & Angels. hence forth

& for Ever more. Amen. & Amen.

My dear Husband , went to Malton to my Sister

Portington s on friday the 11th of September 1668 being

in health much as of late pretty well of his infirmity.

he rid into that fair upon Saturday . to Church on Sunday
