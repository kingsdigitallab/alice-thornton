Albeit there wanted not somme, who guided, by self

intresst repinied by any thing which tended to the support

of Mr Thornton & his Children: Especially by me because I was

not of their faction as I was told . Although I may, & doe apeale to

themselves; whether I have not laid out my self &

fortune to secure all Peoples Just rights from us if it were

never so prejudiciall to my own intrest. And so may

hope for a blesing according to my good conscience & in-

tegerity; who having given to my husbands & friends use; & for

his debts which I could no way be capable of in contracting

from my own Children, that which should be for there provision;

so that had I not indevoured some satisfaction; for those

whom God had given me; I had beene unworthy to have

borne the name of a mother; leaveing them in such an un-

settled Estate & sad condition; yea even altogether un-

provided for in this world: shewing my self one of St Paulls

Infidells: He that provideth not for them of his own house

hath denied the faith & is worse then an Infidell;

so then let not others uncharitable displeasure: blame

what was don in this kind; since my dear husband was

pleased to show his love & affection to his faithful wife

& Children; in answer to the (Noble fortune as he called it

that he had with me; whose trouble only, was that his burdens

was so great that he was compelled to lessen his Estate in-

part to free the rest; Nor could he leave more for his yon-

ger children's Maintenance, & Education, & Portions, then

Laistrop ; which was about 100l per. Annum And this did

not sattisifie those peoples, humours, as I was told

by my dear husband ; because it was not Charged

with debts first to precead my Children's Provision
