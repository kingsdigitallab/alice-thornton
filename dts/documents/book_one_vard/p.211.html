A Prayer after Mr Thornton s Recovery: No. 28th 1665.

O what shall I say, or doe to set forth the infinite mercies

& transcendent loving kindness of the Lord our God, who

still in the midst of Judgement remembers mercies, & shewes

his compassion upon us poor Creatures. O Lord God. thou

King of Glory & Power. just are thy corrections & Judge-

ments, whether Spiritual or Temporal. Thou hast chasti

-ised us very sore. & all thy storms are gon over our heads

but surely we have offended & don amiss; either in negli-

-gence or remissness, or forgetfulness of our duties, to thee

or our neighbour. forgetting our deliverances & mercies bestowed

or to give thee the hon.or of all thy works & goodness to us

ever since we have bin in the married Estate. I beseech thee

hear my cries & accept my groans & Tears. Pardon our

sins & accept us to mercy & favour again. That we may

Jointly & severally serve thee unfeignedly. Sanctify I beseech

thy majesty this most sad afflictions upon us both. & give

us grace that to make a good use of this thy hand of sickness

that it may be for our good, & profit of our souls. that we may

say it is good for us, that we have bin afflicted. grant that we

may receive instruction. & thy loving corrections may

make us humble & thankful. And here from the bottom

of my unfeigned heart, I lift up my voice to adore, praise

& Magnify, thy great & Glorious name. Thou O Lord art holy

Just, wise, omnipotent. Almighty. and Infinitely full of

Compassions towards us thy frail Servants. we sin. & thou

chastiseth us into obedience, if thy mercy will not move.

thy Rod must correct. O Let us not Lose the benefit of this

correction. but doe us the best good, of through reformation. &

let us not forget, or slight these grand deliverances of my

husband & my self. and as thou hast given us new lives.

give us also new ??? s to serve thee. O Lord accept this sacrifice

of Praise & thanks even to thee o holy Trinity be all Possible

Glory. & hon.or Power & might ascribed for our preservation

for ever & for ever. Amen. Amen.
