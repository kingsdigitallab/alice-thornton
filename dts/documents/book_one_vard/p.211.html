A Prayer after Mr Thornton s Recovery: No. 28th 1665.

O what shall I say, or doe to set forth the infinite mercies

& transcendant loving kindness of the Lord our God, who

still in the midest of Judgement remembers mercies, & shewes

his compassion upon us poor Creatures. O Lord God. thou

King of Glory & Power. just are thy corrections & Judge-

ments, whether Spirittuall or Temporal. Thou hast chasti

-ised us very sore. & all thy stormes are gon over our heads

but surely we have offended & don amisse; either in negli-

-gence or remissenesse, or forgettfullnesse of our duties, to thee

or our nighbour. forgetting our deliverances & mercies bestowed

or to give thee the hon.or of all thy workes & goodness to us

ever since we have bin in the married Estate. I beseech thee

hear my cries & accept my groanes & Tears. Pardon our

sins & accept us to mercy & favour again. That we may

Joyntly & severaly serve thee unfeinedly. Sanctify I besech

thy majesty this most sad afflictions upon us both. & give

us grace that to make a good use of this thy hand of sickness

that it may be for our good, & profitt of our souls. that we may

say it is good for us, that we have bin afflicted. grant that we

may receive instruction. & thy loving corrections may

make us humble & thankefull. And here from the bottome

of my unfained heart, I lift up my voyce to addore, praise

& Magnify, thy great & Glorious name. Thou O Lord art holy

Just, wise, omnipotent. Almighty. and Infinitly full of

Compassions towards us thy fraile Servants. we sin. & thou

chastiseth us into obedience, if thy mercy will not move.

thy Rod must correct. O Let us not Lose the benefit of this

corection. but doe us the best good, of through reformation. &

let us not forget, or slight these grand deliverances of my

husband & my self. and as thou hast given us new lives.

give us also new ??? s to serve thee. O Lord accept this sacrifice

of Praise & thanks even to thee o holy Trinity be all Posible

Glory. & hon.or Power & might asscribed for our preservations

for ever & for ever. Amen. Amen.
