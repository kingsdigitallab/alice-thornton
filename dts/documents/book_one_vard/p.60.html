Wee therefore O Lord, confess our sins, and are Sorry

for these our misdoings; nay wee are Sorry O Lord

that wee can be no more Sorry. Accept wee desire

thee O Father, the will for the deed, & conform

our wills unto the deeds: Furnish us O Lord with

the sorrow of attrition & contrition, which may work

in us a repentance never to be repented of Namel

lie, (as St Paul teacheth us), a carefulness, which may

work upon our understanding, upon our indigna-

-tion for our sins past, upon our fear, in regard

of sins to come, upon our desire to good things;

upon our emulation to good Persons to imitate

them, upon our revenge, & punishment of our selves

for our sins: for if wee would judge our selves, wee

should not be judged of thee O Lord.

O send us therefore strength wee humbly beseech thee

O lord to punish our Spiritual sins of Pride, contem

-pt of God, wrath, desire of revenge, lusting after

vanities, with a calling to remembrance (with sorrow &

bitterness our lives past, with the thinking upon the

judgements of hell & death, by studying to be pa-

-tient to put up wrongs; to be ready to forgive sins

by setting our desires on good & heavenly things.

And give us grace wee beseech thee O Father to pu-
