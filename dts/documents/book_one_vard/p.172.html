my husband & Children what she had don for us.

considering my husbands Estate needed it, & he was

heir of a great Large Estate of his Fathers & by her

Death that Jointure came in clear to him. All which Estate

would amount to yearely to him in England & Ireland

three thousand Pounds. which she praied God to bless to

him & his Posterity. that they might enjoy it in righteousness

so long as the moone & sun indureth.

And now she said I have don my work & finished my

course, which the Lord had given me to doe, hence forth

I hope is laid up for me a Crown of Glory, which the Lord

shall give me. & not only to me but for all those that love

the Lord Jesus. & his appearing.

About thursday night she sent for her Children to

take her last fairewell in this life, when Mr Thornton

& my self came, with our 2 Children Alice & Kattherine

she desiring my husband to pray with & for her, as he had

don several times, in which she was much pleased & sattisfi

-ed, ever Joyning most devoutly reverently, praing with

her heart & soul in each pettition, finding great Joy

& refreshment upon such occasions.
