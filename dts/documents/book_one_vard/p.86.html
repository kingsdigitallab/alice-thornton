At this time did my dear Mother & whole family

receive grand preservation from the divine Providence

in delivering us from the Arrow that flieth by day, when

as 100ds died so near us, & thousands, fell at noone

day, nay all that town was almost depopulated. how

did our good & great Lord preserve all us at Hipswell

, so that no infection seized upon any one that belonged us.

although the malice of the beggers was great to have
