Upon Munday he was not well, and had Pills given him

as he used to have by my bro. Portington but they did

not work kindly & so had a glister givin him being the

Method ordered by Dr wittie .

On munday he sent for Mr Sinkler . and told him. That

he knew formerly he had bin in much trouble & sadnesse

for his Sins & walked uncomfortably for the want of the

sence of Gods favour, in great fears & doubtings.

But now the Lord was pleased to make himself known

to him to be a reconsiled Father in Christ Jesus. And that

he was at peace with him; he perceaving a great deal of

Joy & comfort inwardly in his soul & mind. Blessing

the Lord for this his infinite mercies. & hoped that he should

bless his name for ever more that he was had bin so troubled

for now he was reconsiled to him again. there, with many

such like expressions. to the great satisfaction of Mr Sinkler

who stayed a good while with him.

Then towards night this sweet Saint of God grew worse

& more heavy & drousie according to that distemper. and

they sent to me for the Dr which came to him on wednesday after

dinner. my dear was then very weak in body. but I

bless God perfect in mind & understanding. Mr Comber

going to see him. he took his leave of him and bid him to

Remember him to his dear wife. bid me be patient &

contented with Gods hand, & to submitt to his will. which he uttrd

as well as he could for his speach being taken. After which he

had his haire taken of by order, being the last remidie. & this

with his own consent. but Alass no remidies, or meadicine

nor Art could prevail. it being the determination of our God

to take him to himself. And yet to mixe this bitter Cup of

Death with the alay of a quiett frame & temper, free from

any torment or signes of pains lieing as if he were in a sweet

sleep. And by degrees growing colder at his feet. & so

dieing upwards, & drawing his breath shorter all the Thursday

morning. And towards Eleven a clock in the fore noone he

Fetcht one litle Sigh and so sweetely resigned up his spirit

in to the hands of his dear Saviour & Redemer Jesus Christ.
