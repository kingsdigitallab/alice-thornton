many years: so that I was holy destitute of an opportunity

to perform that comfortable refreshing duty which my Soul

longed for: & grieved much for the want thereof: And with

all being in such a languishing condition as I formerly

mentioned: with out any spiritual help from men:

After which drawing near with in a month of my delivery

it behooved me to look with a more narrow search into my

Soul & examine how my thoughts & desires were inclined

to accept of death; whose time of life here: very probably

might be short. this was my 7th child which the Lord had given

me to conceive. I had more strong apprehensions of my

change, in regard it was both my husbands mothers num-

ber & my own; This did more lively persuade, that as de

-ath waited at all times: yet more reason for us to expect
