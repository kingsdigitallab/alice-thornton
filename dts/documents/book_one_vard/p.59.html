8. forbidden us all inward grudgings & longing after

Wealth by avarice, or coveting other men's riches: all

fraud, deceit, tricks. in bargaining & selling: All

usury, idleness, niggardise & Profusion of thy good Creaturs

, yet have we incurred the breach of all these thy Heavenly

directions, by not dealing uprightly with our neighbour in

buying & selling, by not caring to save him from damage

by not doing the works of our calling, by not using liberality

of frugality in all our actions.

Com.

9, 10 And wee must Lastly confess O Lord against our Selves

that thou haste enjoined us all plain meaning and

speaking, all taciturnity, grave speech, a moderate use

of the tongue, the bearing of a good opinion of our Neigh-

-bours, & judging, all things done by him to the best:

But contrarily wee have delighted our selves to much

in lying, false testimony prattling, scurrilitie, defa-

-ming, & backbiting, sinister suspicion, and wrongful

judging our Neighbours. And now O Lord, by this

looking glass of thy divine Law, wee see our selves

so strangely deformed, nay, so much defaced, with boils

& ulcers, & wounds, how can wee contain our selves

from sorrow & repentance, till wee have washed away

these sins and obliquities of ours in the fountain and

comfortable stream of his Blood that died for us,

wherein being once dipped, though wee were before as deep

as scarlet, wee shall become as white as snow:
