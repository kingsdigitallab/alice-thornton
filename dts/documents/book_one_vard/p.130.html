Dear as A loving Mother, who hadst a love to me in my

preservation from death, & destruction. in thy divine

wisdom hast thou ordered all things & passages in this

my great sickness, of my Life not laying more on me

then thou gavest me strength to undergo. O Lord this

dispensation of Afflictions & great sicknesses is the way

& means to bring me unto thee, & the fittest for me to in

joy. letting me see thereby thy mighty power to cast

down & raise me up again, even in my desperate cond

-ition when all men had given over to expect my Life

Then did the great Physician of Soul & body raise the one

& heal the other; raising me up again & giving me

strength & setting me on my feet after 6 months sickness

in my bed. I called upon my God, in mine anguish

of spirit, & heaviness, I did complain, & made my

supplications unto my God; the Lord of my Life & Joy.

for my desires was to cleave unto him, That I had offended

& made my supplications unto my Judge. O Lord I

have offended many ways, but thou art he that canst

wash & cleanse my defiled, polluted Soul for who is

there that liveth & sinnest not. As my desires was alone

to cleave on thee, so thou didst send this to me, this sick

nesse unto thy servant. And by degrees did remove the

same in thy due time. Thou heardest my prayers accep

ted my tears of repentance, my sorrows; when death

had compassed me about. Lord heal my soul for I

have sinned. And now o thou most holy one of Israel

blessed be thy glorious name, & magnified forever. that

thou hast put fresh opportunities of praising thee &

serving the Lord in the Land of the Living. Stir up my ???

& soul in true, & unfeigned thankfulness. to thy

divine majesty & never to be unthankful or ingrate

full. or unprofitable. in thy world:
