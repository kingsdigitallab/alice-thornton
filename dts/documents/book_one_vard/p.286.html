And casualties, incident to That feeble and weak

Estate of Infants & Childhoods:

For although there innocency be not capable of offending

others. yet that Innocency & harmlessness is not able to

defend them from Injurious dealings from evil

Persons. neglects, & Brutishness of nurses & careless

ness of others. not to mention those infinite hazards

of over laying; & badness of there food & evil milk

Added to the dreadful malice of Satan who doth

by all means Endeavour to destroy man Kind.

sets on work all his engines against us by more

designs then we can see or be capable to understand

There fore am I for ever bound to bless the Eternal

name of God who hath sent his guardian

Angel to watch over me and mine for my good &

Preservation. ever since I was borne. The number

of his miraculous deliverances are past finding out

yet will I call to mind what I can that he might receive

receav the Glory of. All. There was now a most great

preservation to me when I was but a little Child &

was following my maid Sara watts Tomlinson

who cared

[word] I.e., carried.

my brother Christopher in her Arms

& I took hold of her Cote. my weak hand being

but about 3 years old could not go so fast after her

but my feet stumbled against the Threshold. &

fell upon the corner stone of the hearth in the

Chamber called the Passage chamber which leads to

my dear mothers Chamber. At which time I

broke the Scull of my fore head in the very top.

against the said Rowbe. so grievously about an Inch

long insomuch that the skin of the brain was

seen & in great danger of death being like to

have bleed to death it being so desperate a

wound. But by the Providence of God & my dear

mothers skill & care of me she did make a

perfect Cure only a great scar still remains & will

never be gon. to putt me in fresh mind of my great

obligation to &, deliverance, of Almighty God for my

Life.
