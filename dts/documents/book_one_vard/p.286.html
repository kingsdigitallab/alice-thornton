And casultys, incident to That feble and weak

Estate of Infants & Childehoods:

For altho there innocency be not capable of offending

others. yet that Innocency & harmlessness is not able to

defend them from Injurious dealings from evil

Persons. neglects, & Brutishness of nurrses & caireles

ness of others. not to mention those infinite hazards

of over layeing; & badness of there food & evll milk

Added to the dreadful malice of Satan who doth

by all means Endeavour to destroy man Kinde.

setts on work all his engines against us by more

designs then we can see or be capable to understand

There fore am I for ever bound to bless the Eternal

name of God who hath sent his gaurdian

Angell to watch over me and mine for my good &

Preservation. ever since I was borne. The number

of his miraculous deliverances are past finding out

yet will I call to mind what I can that he might receav

receav the Glory of. All. There was now a most greatt

preservation to me when I was but a little Child &

was following my maid Sara watts Tomlinson

who caired

[word] I.e., carried.

my brother Christopher in her Arms

& I took hold of her Cote. my weak hand being

but about 3 yers old could not go so fast after her

but my feet stumbled against the Thrashhold. &

fell upon the corner stone of the harth in the

Chamber called the Passage chamber which leades to

my dear mothers Chamber. At which time I

broke the Scull of my fore head in the very top.

against the said Rowbe. so greivously about an Inch

long insoe much that the skin of the braine was

seen & in great danger of death being like to

have bleed to death it being so desperate a

wound. But by the Providence of God & my dere

mothers skill & care of me she did make a

perfect Cure only a great scarre still remaines & will

never be gon. to putt me in fresh mind of my great

obleigation to &, deliverance, of Allmighty God for my

Life.
