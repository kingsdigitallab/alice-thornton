This word; & set it home by his Spirit; to stay my

drooping heart; even ready to faint & perish for want

of spiritual food. Behold I was weary with the burden of

Sin. & he Said come: I was faint. & heavy Laden, he

said come: Lord I come, but to whom must I come for relief

come unto me, all yea that are weary & heavy laden. & I

will give you Rest. O Lord if thou art pleased to call all

that labour, & are weary: with there sins; & temptations of

Satan. I see no reason why I should not come with my sad

nesse & burdens, for thou hast promised to give me rest.

Alas o Lord God, I need it & want it, I almost sink und

-er thy heavy hand of displeasure: I cry unto thee, out

of the whales belly of despair: if thou help not. O God

the father of heaven have mercy upon me; O Christ the

Redeemer of the world, have mercy upon me. O holy, blessed

& glorious Spirit have pity on me: O Most glorious Trinity

3 Persons & one God, help thy poor distressed weak hand

maid. Plunged in the depth of sin & misery. for the least with

out repentance, & thy mercy is in it's own nature damnable

And non but thy own Almighty power is able to help

deliver & save me from the Jaws of this Lion; who goes about

seeking to devour & make a prey of my soul. And as thy

wisdom & free grace o blessed Trinity. did set thy self on

work by & through mercy to the sons of men, & by All mighty

Power to find out a way for mans Redemption: and of thy

free grace thou o my dear Saviour suffered the wrath of thy

Father on the Cross to purchase our salvation. So now art thou

pleased to vanquish my grand Enemy by the power of thy

word to me. I believe, (Lord help my unbelief) that thou

art as merciful to me as others, & wilt not exclude me except

I exclude my self, & as thou hast pleased to give me plenty of

Calls & comforts, who in my sad hour was a God of compassion

so thou hast given me grace to come unto thee. I come O Lord to

thee & hang on thy mercy, with all the Powers & faculties of my

heart & soul; for the pardoning my sin & to strengthen my faith

Though weak in body. yet with an unfeigned hope of thy

goodness & sweetest Clemency. that it will not be in vain this

thy word came unto me, this is from thy goodness when I was

in sorrow, to let me see if the time was not past, when thou calls

to come unto thee.
