Is the first time I had the happiness to doe, in this City

O Lord I beseech thee forgive my Sins & unworthiness, my

Childishness, & ignorance, & unpreparednesse. being unfit

to draw near to so holy a great God as thou art. And with

all accept my true & faithful endeavours to come to thy

holy Super, wherein thou offerest to be received by faith

& grace spiritually into my Soul. Grant that I may receive

& apply all thy merits to heal my wounded soul. and

pardon my Sins, as verily as I have bin made partaker

of these outward ordinances. And I beseech thee bless

thy handmaid, & receive and accept this thanksgiving

& praise from my ??? as thou didst from thy servant David

which loved thee from his youth. as I have desired to set God

always before me that I may not offend. Lord give me underst

tanding & I shall live before thee & praise the Lord for all his benefits

afforded to me for soul & body. all which I humbly beg in

the name & for the sake of my dearest saviour his sake, the

Lord Jesus Christ the Righteous. to whom with the holy spirit be

all glory & power, might dominion, thanksgiving &

praise for ever more Amen.

A discourse of passages, & deliverances of my mother &

us 3 Children after her removal from Chester , to Snape

& till we came to Kirklington . 1643, 4 3 4

Aug.

28

1643. From Weschester my dear mother removed with her 3 younger

Children. Alice . Christofer . & John wandesforde . (She having

sent her 2 grand sons home from Chester before the siege)

with these, & several servants & tenants, though with much difficulty

by reason of the interchange of the Kings Armies, & the Parliament

she was brought into the Town of warrington , towards coming

in to Yorkeshire , she finding more favour by reason of the Captains

Civility. & by a pass from Collonel Shittleworth then usual.
