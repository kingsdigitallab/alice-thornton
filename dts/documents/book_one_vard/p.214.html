Of my dangerous sickness: August 8 16th 1666.

After the drinking of Scarbrough waters, Mr Thornton

sent for me to Yorke about business with my Lord Frechvile

in which jorney I thought I received some harm being lately

conceived before as Dr wittie apprehended. so this, together

with a grief that befell me at my return home, about a Settle

ment last made by Colvill for my Children's maintenance

& Portions; which had bin undon with out my consent

& knowledge when Mr Thornton was sick at Steersby ,

for the Chargeing of Mr Raines & Mr Portington Debt of

1400l by mortgage upon Laistrop . in so doing there was

not one penny could be secured for my two Poor Daughters

either maintenance or Portion, till after the payment of

that Debt. & there was but 100l per annum for all: And also

that my brother Christopher Rent Charge out of Ireland was secu-

-red to them also: for 7 years 200l a year.

which condition I could not, but lament; nor had I got a sight

of this new disposittion of that Estate. which I doe believe Mr

Thornton was partly necessitated to doe, in regard, that they

would not be satisfied with the security alone out of Ireland

After this hapened. I fell into a very sad & desperate con

dittion upon satterday the 16th of August: 1666 being then

about 8 weeks gon. the violency thereof continued a long

time after Dr wittie was with me. And for 3 days he did not

expect my life. so that he was compelled to use all his Art for

my preservation. But it pleased God upon the use of his

meadicens, that extremity a little abated, he leaveing me

in a hopefull way of Recovery, only said I must not

expect to be restored till about the latter end of octber

All which time I was in continuall faintings upon the reneuall

of that extremity. So that my loss of Spirits & strength &c

was so great that it was expected I should have fallen

into a deep consumption. And I continued exceeding

feeble & weak till the Candlemas following The chief remidie

which I found for restoreing strength. was a meadicine made
