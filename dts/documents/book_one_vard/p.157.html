A Prayer to God for the Church. & restoration of the King .

november 1659.

O Lord our God, the great & mighty Jehova, which hath

thy eyes open, to see the oppression & hear the cries of all thy

servants in all the ends of the Earth. Let not I humbly be-

seech thy divine Majesty, our misery seeme small unto

thee, which we suffer in this place & nations, by reason of our

Sins, which have bin so great & crieing that they have tired out

thy long suffering & patience, & we are abominable in thy

holy sight, in whose presence the Angels cover their faces

And yet o Lord. whither should we vild creatures go to

be cleansed, or purged: but unto thee the fountaine of an in-

exhaustable goodness & Puritty. We have sinned & don

amisse, O Lord & what shall we say unto thee, oh thou

preserver of men; Oh take away our wickednesse & thou

shallt finde none; Lord thy mercies hast upheld us these

many years of calamities even beyond miraculous, there

fore let our suplicattions come before thee the Judge of all

And make not a full end of thy Church in this nations

whose miseries has bin too long by reason of our sins.

Butt let it apeare that in judgement thou willt remember

mercy & spare us from utter destruction from the hands

of thine & our bloody enemies. O deliver not the soul of

thy turtle dove unto the multitude of her Enemies: and

forget not the Congregattion of the poor for ever, which hast

no dependancy upon man, nor dust & Ashes, butt in thee

alone. the Great & tirrible Lord God. King of heaven & Earth.

Arise O God, maintaine thine own cause: remember

how the foolish man blaspheameth thee daily, forget not

the voyce of thine Enemies; the presumption of them that hate

thee, increaseth ever more and more.
