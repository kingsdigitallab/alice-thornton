After my dear Mothers deceace; I remained at

Hipswell , by reason of my weakness. & trouble

upon her death. having gotten a very dangerous

Cough with watching with my dear Mother, for whom

I could never enough show my infinite duty & affect

tion to such an Excellent Parent.

Also, it was in a great frost, so that I could not well

be removed with Safty of my life till the march following

At which time I was carried to St nickolas , there rem

-aining till I was delivered & well again.

And it was the good pleasure of God to continue

me in the Land of the Living. And to bring forth my

6th Child at St Nickolas .

I was delivered of a very goodly son, after hard

labour & hazardus; yet through great mercy I had

my Life spared & was blessed with a happy child

about 3 or 4 a clock in the morning upon Tusday

the 17th of April. 1660 borne & baptized that day.

That day also was my Child baptized by Mr

Kirtton of Richmond called William after his father

his sureties were my Cosen John yorke : my Cosen

william Norton . and my Cozen James Darcy Lady

of Richmond (died April 28th 1660) buried 29 at

Thus was I blessed with the life & comfort of my dear

childs baptimse with its injoyment of that holy seale of

Regeneration; & my pretty babe was in good health

suckeing his poor mother to whom my good God had

had given the blessing of the breasts as well as the

womb of that child to whom it was no little Sattis

faction. while I enjoyed his Life (burid
