Sooner from the wicked. preserving them safe in

the Grave from all violencies & outrages, both of

sin, & sufferings. & giving into their bosoms

An 100 times more the reward of his grace,

with Glory Everlasting. Peradventure on the contrary

When, as their deaths may prove a curse to

the world, to be left of such Mosesses & Samuel's

which pleaded to god for them & whose Deaths are

too often fore runners of fatal Judgements, to

all hard hearted, & incorrigible sinners.

The Lord God, of mercy. grant to us all of his

Family, that this grand blow in taking our

Head from us may be a warning, to fear & serve

this great God, of our fathers. returning from those

evil ways, & sins, which has offended him least

we perish by iniquity. learn by our Fathers

example to imitate his virtues. being in peace

& unity amongst our selves. that so living in truth

& peace, we may at Last enjoy the God of Peace. Amen.

A Prayer upon my fathers Death.

Oh thou most great & Dreadful Lord God. mighty

in Power, & wisdom. which seest & knowest all things

in heaven & Earth & canst look us into destruction

yet A most gracious & Tender loving father of
