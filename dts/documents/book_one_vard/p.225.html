My daughter Alice Thornton her Small Pox Jan.25 67.

About the 25th of January Naly took Physic to prevent the

Small Pox. when she fell to be very ill pained in her head

& back on the 26th she being very ill & sick in an Aguish

temper the 27 & 28th till the small Pox appeared. being for

2 days in a cold sweat with out heat & in very great da-

-nger of Death. the 29 she was in great hazard yet lay

very patiently & did not talk Idle as usual in such dis

eases. though there was little hopes of her recovery. They being

stroke in through her could sweats; yet was all remedies

used to her for warmth by Hanna . & c. & Cordials by brother

Portington . But on the 29 it pleased the great & gracious

God, he did begin to give us better hopes. the small Pox then

coming out & appear. she was in a warmer temper. not

so sick as formerly. & we by the mercies of God hoped for

a recovery: At the 31 day she fell blind intermitting till

the 5th of february being exceedingly choked in her throat. &

could not swallow, almost any thing with out a Pipe for the

small Pox & streit throat. nor did she get any sleep

till the 3d february After which she got a little & the 4th her Eyes began

to unclose. the 5th saw perfectly. & they blackened. & her

throat mended. & the fever abated: the 8th day of feb she

began to be pretty well. though extremely full & sore. About

the 18th Naly rose out of bed & recruited in strength by degrees

The hair came of & that favour clean taken from her.

But oh that our hearts were enlarged in thankfulness

to the great Lord our God for the preservation of this my

Eldest Child whose special deliverance must not be for-

-gotten to give glory to the great God of Israel which had pity

upon my self, husband . & 3 Children . by restoring their

lives when they were all so night many deaths. O Lord

accept I beseech my grateful & humble ??? which had com-

passion upon thy Servants & our Child. Let us all live to
