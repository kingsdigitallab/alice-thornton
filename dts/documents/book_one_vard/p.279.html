Thanksgiving, Praise, Adoration & Dominion

by all Creatures & things in Heaven & Earth.

by the Tongues of men & Angels archangels.

Cherubims & Seraphims both now & to all Eternity

Humbly concluding these my humble Petitions &

weak prayers & praises in that Perfect form of Prayer

which the grand Bishop of our Souls taught us in his holy

Gospel to pray unto thee in Saying

Our father which art in Heaven:

&c. Amen. Hallelujah.

Amen.

Our Saviours command.

Love not the World: nor the things of the world.

for the fashion of the world passeth away.

Set your affections on heaven above, & not on Earth.

for where your Treasure is there will your heart be also.

The Just shall live by faith.

Mr Thornton s motto & my own.

Nisi Christus Nemo:

Tour pour le' Eglize: None but Christ.

All for the Church God

Anagram.

Christ and his Church, in love so well agr'eed. That he for her, and She for him, has bl'eed. Thus imitate thy Saviour, in his fervent love, And then, thy Joys, my Soul will lasting prove. Oh groundless deeps, O love beyond degree. The offended dies, to set the offender free. But now. The Churches head to heaven is gon Leaving her, here on Earth alone. Much like a Widow in distress Washed in Tears, Tears that express. her daily griefs, with sighs, to be deprived. Of her dear Sovereign; the world denied.
