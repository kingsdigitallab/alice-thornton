Thanksgiving, Praise, Adoration & Dominion

by all Creatures & things in Heaven & Earth.

by the Tounges of men & Angels Arch-Angells.

Cherubims & Seraphimes both now & to all Eternity

Humbly concluding these my humble Pettions &

weak praires & praises in that Perfect forme of Prair

which the grand Bishop of our Souls taught us in his holy

Gospel to pray unto thee in Saying

Our father which art in Heaven:

&c. Amen. Haleluia.

Amen.

Our Saviours command.

Love not the World: nor the things of the world.

for the fashion of the world passeth away.

Set your affections on heaven above, & not on Earth.

for where your Treasure is there will your heart be also.

The Just shall live by faith.

Mr Thornton s motto & my own.

Nisi Christus Nemo:

Tour pour le' Eglize: None but Christ.

All for the Church God

Annagrame.

Christ and his Church, in love so well agr'eed. That he for her, and She for him, has bl'eed. Thus imitate thy Saviour, in his fervent love, And then, thy Joys, my Soul will lasting prove. Oh groundles deeps, O love beyond degree. The offended dies, to set the offender free. But now. The Churches head to heaven is gon Leaveing her, here on Earth alone. Much like a Widow in disstresse Washed in Tears, Tears that express. her dailie greifes, with sighes, to be deprived. Of her dear Soveraine; the world denied.
