Being sequestered through a false oath of his adversarys

suggestion, & his Estate, with all the other appointed for widow

Children & Creditors, of my father seized on for the Parlia

-ment upon that account. This don under the pretence of

Godliness & Religion, because he did not join in such

Practices of Rebellion against The Church of God. &

our Lawful King whom God had commanded to be obeyed

nor could any adhere to Such designs, whose hearts

was sensible of those duties of faith & Allegiance

with out the danger of Eternal damnation. and the curse

of God upon them who separated from the known Laws &

commands of God Almighty, and the Laws of the Land wherein

were happily placed. our peace & Safety.

According to that of the wiseman. My son fear thou God &

the king & meddle not with them that are given to change for

who knows the ruin of them both. Yet not withstand

-ing this threatening; Evil was so Established by a law

That there was no man of Estate which did not lift up

his hand against the Lords Anointed, that could be freed

Either from plundering, sequestration & imprisonment

Robed, or murdered, by secret, or open hostility.

if any gave information against them. As for my brothers

crime, it was for disposing of the Parsonage of kirkling

ton in his own right as heir, & of my Mother as a Gaurd

-ian to him yet under Age being but 19 years old, unto

Mr Siddall , A very Pious godly Minister. but not of

the Presbyterian faction. This living being of too good

a value for a Royalist, was looked upon by one Mr

Nesbitt of the other opinion, & so the more confiding Person.

which could not be invested in to it, tell my brother &c was

made a delinquent.
