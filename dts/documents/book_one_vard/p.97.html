Being sequesterd through a false oath of his adversarys

suggestion, & his Estate, with all the other apoynted for widow

Children & Creaditors, of my father seized on for the Parlia

-ment upon that account. This don under the pretence of

Godlinesse & Religion, because he did not joyne in such

Practices of Rebellion against The Church of God. &

our Lawfull King whom God had commanded to be obeid

nor could any adheare to Such designs, whose hearts

was sencable of those duties of faith & Alleigeance

with out the danger of Eternal damnation. and the curse

of God upon them who seperated from the known Laws &

commands of God Almighty, and the Laws of the Land wherein

were happiely placed. our peace & Safetie.

According to that of the wiseman. My son fear thou God &

the king & medle not with them that are given to change for

who knowes the ruin of them both. Yet not withstand

-ing this threatning; Evil was so Established by a law

That there was no man of Estate which did not lift up

his hand against the Lords Annoynted, that could be freed

Eitther from plundering, sequestration & imprisonment

Robed, or murdered, by secret, or open hostilitie.

if any gave information against them. As for my brothers

crime, it was for disposseing of the Parsonage of kirkling

ton in his own right as heir, & of my Mother as a Gaurd

-ian to him yet under Age being but 19 years old, unto

Mr Siddall , A very Pious godly Minister. but not of

the Priesbyterian faction. This living being of too good

a valew for a Royalast, was looked upon by one Mr

Nesbitt of the other oppinion, & so the more confiding Person.

which could not be invested in to it, tell my brother &c was

made a delinquent.
