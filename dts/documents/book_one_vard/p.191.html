But Lo I asked spiritual: & the Lord gave me great

addittion of temporal mercies, filling my mouth & soul

with abundant gladnesse & praise, when I looked for nothing

but death & destruction. shewing that the mercies of the Eternal

God are not to be measured by the weak apprehensions of men

but admired & adored, in all his wisdom, goodness & free

Grace to poor wanting mortalls.

For he appointing, means blessed the same to me. so that by
