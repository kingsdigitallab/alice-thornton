145 Original Sin wherein we were conceived & borne,

we have committed many Actual Transgressions against

All thy holy Laws & Commandments, so that if thou

shouldest enter into Judgement with us, we could expect

nothing but death & Damnation; But forever blessed

be thy Majesty who hath not left us in this wretched

condition, But hast in thy boundless mercy provided

a Gracious Remedy in sending thy blessed Son Christ

Jesus in to the world for the redemption of mankind, those

whom they callest to the knowledge & true faith in Jesus

Christ. O Lord in the name & Mediation of this our most

Merciful Redeemer, wee humbly beseech thee to blot

out our Transgressions. and be reconciled unto us, that

our sins may not hinder our Prayers from ascending up

to thy Throne of grace, or there to receive a happy

Answer, not only in our own behalf, but for & in the be-

halfe of this thy distressed handmaid in travail of Childe

-birth in which Estate, and punishment for sin she doth

freely freely & cheerfully Submit, to thy wise dispensations

humbly beseeching thee to possess her soul with Christian

Patience in her greatest Extremity. Strengthen her

faith against the assaults of Satan. cherish her hope

in the second Adam Christ Jesus that according to thy

gracious promise he will break his head, by subduing

his power to tempt her to despair of thy mercies for her

deliverances. lay to her heart, dear Lord, those promises

thou hast declared in holy Scriptures to penitent sin-

-ners in their afflictions, And the gracious Performance

thou hast vouchsafed to thy poor handmade in sundry

such Necessities in the birth of her children. Oh wee be-

seech thee proportion her (patience) & pains, according

to that measure of strength it shall please thee to endue her

with, Oh Lord lay no more upon her then thou wilt enable

her to bear. with fortitude & patience.
