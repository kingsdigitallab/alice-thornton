145 Orriginall Sinne wherein we were conceived & borne,

we have committed many Actuall Transgressions against

All thy holy Laws & Commandements, so that if thou

shouldest enter into Judgement with us, we could expect

noething but death & Damnation; But forever blessed

be thy Majesty who hath not left us in this wretched

condition, But hast in thy boundlesse mercy provided

a Gracious Remidy in sending thy bllessed Son Christ

Jesus in to the world for the redemtion of mankinde, those

whom they callest to the knowledge & true faith in Jesus

Christ. O Lord in the name & Mediation of this our most

Mercyfull Redeemer, wee humbly beseech thee to blott

out our Transgressions. and be reconsiled unto us, that

our sins may not hinder our Prayers from assending up

to thy Throne of grace, or there to receive a happy

Answer, not only in our own behalfe, but for & in the be-

halfe of this thy distressed handmaid in travail of Childe

-birth in which Estate, and punishment for sinne she doth

freely freely & chearfully Submitt, to thy wise dispensations

humbly beseeching thee to possess her soul with Christian

Patience in her greatest Extremity. Strengthen her

faith against the assaults of Sattan. cherish her hope

in the second Adam Christ Jesus that according to thy

gracious promise he will breake his head, by subdueing

his power to tempt her to dispaire of thy mercies for her

deliverances. lay to her heart, dear Lord, those promises

thou hast declared in holy Scriptures to penitent sin-

-ners in their afflictions, And the gracious Performance

thou hast vouchsafed to thy poor handemaide in sundry

such Necessityes in the birth of her children. Oh wee be-

seech thee proportion her (patience) & pains, according

to that measure of strength it shall please thee to indue her

with, Oh Lord lay no more upon her then thou wilt inable

her to bear. with fortitude & patience.
