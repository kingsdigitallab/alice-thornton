About this August after our return from Scarbrough

it pleased God to give me much strength & health,

so that I conceived with Child ; which after Mr Thornton

perceived, he with my mother greatly rejoiced; hope-

-ing that I might at length be blessed with a son.

For 4 months together, I enjoyed a great deal of

comfort & health, being much stronger & lively

when I was with my Sons then Daughters, having

great cause to admire the goodness of God, which even

contrary unto hope caused me to recover of that Sad

distemper, wherewith I was afflicted, and giving

me hopes to bring forth a Son to be a Comfort to my

dear husband & us all.

A thanksgiving after affter my recovery.

My song shall be always of the loving kindness of

the Lord: with my mouth will I ever be showing thy

truth from one generation to another, O Lord, the very

heavens shall praise thy wondrous works: & thy

truth in the congregation of the saints. And now thou

O Lord who dost wonders, casting me down in sickness

& weakness, & raising me up again in thy good time

thou hast thereby declared thy power to all People
