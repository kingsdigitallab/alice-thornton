The Widdowes Praieer. for her self & Children.

O Lord our God, the God of our Salvation, who for our

Sakes wert wounded and didest Die, to Redeeme

our Souls from Hell; And wast pleased to lie in the

Grave, That our Sins might be buried by thee.

by an Act of Oblivion. but thou alone of all that ever died

were free amongst the Dead, to show thy Soveranity

& power over all; and of thy own Power didst arise

again with victory & Triumph over hell & the Grave;

O Powerfull, Allmighty, & Omnipotent Jehova, Look

down from thy Throne of Majesty, & Mercy, with a

gracious Eye of favour & compassion. And behold

me the unworthiest of thy Creatures; making my ad-

dresses at thy Throne of Grace: o holy Trinity I humbly

besech thee regard the powrings out of a weak fraile

despised handmaid of thine (yet whose heart is truly

fixed upon thy divine Beauties.) in desires & longings

to be made like thee in holiness & Righteousness for Ever

have mercy upon thy servant, whom thou has made

& preserved by thy might hither to. And behold Lord

lo thine indignation lieth hard upon me, & thou hast

vexced me with all thy Stormes; thy hand presseth me

Sore; My Soul is full of troubles & heavinesse by reason

of my Sins. & my life draweth nigh unto the Grave;

My body in distress my Soul under sader calamities

by the withdrawings of thy Presence. Hide not thou thy

Heavenly face face from me O Lord; unlesse to make me

seeke thee with greater Ardor & Zeale. Oh pardon thy

servants frailties & infirmities in too much sorrow &

greiving for the being deprived of my head & husband, &

let me acknowledge thy hand with submissivenesse & patience

it is thou Lord that art gracious & good to the Children of men

O restore me to thy favour again, to comfort me in my

Sorrows & sadnesses, & now I am brought into a forlorne

& widdowed condition. O Lord I beseech thee give me
