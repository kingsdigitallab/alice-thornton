A Praier After the death of my 3d childe Betty. Thornton

O Lord most high, whoes is the right of Creation &

Governour of all the Earth. I beseech thy gracious Maj

estie to pardon our great & crieing sinns, who hath

Provoaked thee to deprive us of this sweete Childe thy

Blessing to us. let thy favour over looke our offences

& pardon wherein we have don amisse in all accidents

& occurrances of our lives, before, & since we knew each

other, forgive my impatiency, or murmuring at thy

heavy hand. who yet in Judgement hast remembred

mercy & hast not pressed us to destruction. Lord looke

not upon us as in our selves for then noe liveing can stand

in thy preasence. but apeare poluted, defiled, & by

depraved nature Abominable in thy sight. but looke

on us in thy deare Son Jesus Christ, our redeemer. and

pardon all our sinfull compliances towards each other

since our married Estate if there have bin any such as

has displeased thee O Lord, for thou art a God of purity

& holinesse, therefore clence all our hearts, & sanctifie this sad

crosse for our good & thy glory. to which I humbly submit

& freely, seeing thy goodnesse in delivering this my swet

childe from all its Miseries in this world. of Infancy

Childhood, & folly. And blesse thy devine Majestie

who hast taken her a way, before her soule was poluted

with actuall sins. hopeing in my deare Saviour who recea

-ved, such when they brought them unto him on Earth

, that her Soule is acceptable in thy sight, whom thou hast

taken soe early into thy Kingdom. O Lord give us grace
