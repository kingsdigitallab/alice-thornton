4 Then flie a loft on wings of faith, And doe what thy Redeemer saith. Oh follow him stay not behind, For to be drove by every wind. Of triffeling foolish Childeish toyes To interrupt thy Sollid lasting Joyes Which are ever liveing. never Ending There are pleasures worth Commending Come on my Soule hoist up thy highest sails And creepe not still on, like wearie Snailes Power out thy soule by praier to God on high Lay thou fast hold on him untill thou die. Then guide mee Lord, give my Soule, directions. Subdue my passions, curbe my stout Affections Nip thou the bud, before the bloome begins Lord ever keepe me from Presumptuous Sins And make me ever chuse what you Seest best Lord lead me by thy hand into thy Rest.

Amen.
