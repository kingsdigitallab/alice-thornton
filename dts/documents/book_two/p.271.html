It pleased God further to excersize me his

poore servant and handmaide with a very

great and desperate weakness. exceding great

Even to depriving me of motion & speech. & strength

beginning a little affter my Childe was borne

& I laid in bed while all the Company was

gott together to veiew that goodly Childe &

admire him soe large & big newly borne & all

soe fond of him beeing a Son with great Joy

I fell into a most desperate extremity of

flooding, in soe much as it was not hoped

my life should have bin saved, all that night

it was terrible to behold of them was a bout

me bringing me into a most desperate Conditn

without all hopes of life. spirrits. soule. strength

seemd all gon from me; & I freely resinignd
