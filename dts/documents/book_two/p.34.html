And tho he was, not, obleiged to secure Mr

Yet not diserning some persons drift, to be
secured theire owne debt, by Mr Th. out of his own
Estate, perswaded him to do it, first, to nettleton
& next to give security to themselves. which
proved a great Snaire. & gave them opportunity
to seeke sattisfaction from him, & did not looke
