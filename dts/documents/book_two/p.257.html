But beged of him ( Mr Best ) as he was a freind to
my selfe, & Children; haveing non of my owne Relations
to stand for me, but him, & he beeing a Trustee, in that
Deed, soe he would not discover that which he said
he found out; to Mr Thornton where by That Intail
might be cutt of, & settled for other uses.

Uppon which wordes, he did promise, & faithfully
ingage to me uppon his faith. that he would not
betray my cause, to Mr Thornton nor discover the failing in that
Settlement on which Promise I relied nor did thinke
That he would have don soe treatcherously to me
as it did prove affterwards.
