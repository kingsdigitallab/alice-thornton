This my Son was Baptized on Satterday the 20th
of Septembr by Mr Lacocke 1662 att our House in
Easte newton. Being borne on Friday the 19th of sept.
1662 at Easte Newton betwixt the houers of 8 & 9
a Clocke att night. his godfathers was Dr witty
Mr Best. & my Lady Cholmley. Lord make him
Pertakr of the admition of his name to be entred
into the Booke of Life as he is of the holy Baptisme
to brng him into the Church Millitant on Earth. Amn

The fuller discription is Related in my booke of
