But he pressing me to this. and he beeing a

Trustee nominated in that Deed of Co. Colvils

I thought to have his advice about it

whether it were not Drawne soe firme that

it could not be Cutt of with out my Consent

And soe very innocently shewed him that

Deed: which when he had read it. he did tell

me that Mr Th. could cutt of the Intaile which

out my Consent and Charged me by noe
