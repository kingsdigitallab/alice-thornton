As to her Personall Estate, in monneys

Plate, Linning, Beding &c. I have heard Mr

Thornton say he had as good of what my Der

mother Left me to the valew of one Thousand

& my selfe & Children . 2550 or 2600l

Besides which she made a Deed of guift to

Feffeos

i.e. 'feoffees'

in trust of all her Arrears in Ireland due

to her out of my father s Estate of Edough of 300l

per Annum. in lieu of her Joynter. she out living

my d. father 19 yeares There became due to her

which she maide our by Deed the somme of 6000l

which somme or any part of it was not paid but

Remaines in my brother s Estate still./

Togethr with 200l as a Debt my d mothr Paid for

my father s Estate & ought to be paid me by her

will But I know in all these things my poore Brot

has bin imposed uppon & maide beleve not due to be

Paid to my Dere mother . or my selfe.
