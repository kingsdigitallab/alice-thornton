And thus did this dreadfull Cronicle disease

Remaine on me soe long that in all these things

I continued out of one distempr into annothr

for the space of 9 months. with the Relapsses & the

weaknesses that followed. soe that it was from the

6t of august 1652 till May 12th 1653 affter

before I was able to goe out of my Chambr or

had Recruted my strength any degree.

Thus have I Passed the first two yeares of

my married Estate in a most sad and uncomfortall
