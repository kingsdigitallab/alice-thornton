Affter it pleased my good God to restore me
to my health, I was kept much imployed in that
affaire of curing my deare, Child of the Ricketts &
giveing her meadicens for the Convoltions: which I
blesse God did both take effect for her health.

But least I should have too much Ease from that
great worke I was begun & scarcely recovred my
strength betwixt one Childe and annother.
I fell with Childe of my 3d Childe & beeing in a
Pretty good Condition was hopefull I might have
given my Childe sucke which I designed by Gods
helpe to have don (if I had strength:)

About a weeke before my travell I contineud in
much Paines by the Childes heavines beeing in contin
all

expectancy each houer att which time my deare
mother laid sicke in the house of a most sad Cough
& by advice & (Troutbecke came to give her advice
by friends) importunity. by Gods blessing she was
better but could not come to me in my Labour
All Tuesday & that night, & wednesday I was in great
extreamity till the morning feb. 14 halfe an hour
affter 11 a clocke. I was by Gods mercy delivred of a
sweete goodly daughter, & most delicate Childe for which
most infinitt mercy & goodness I humbly bless & Praise
