great Afflictions, on thy handmaide in that I loved

thy Creatures more, or too much with thee my heavnly

father & not setting my affections wholy uppon thee

who hast loved me & gavest thy selfe for me.

O Lord Jesus my Saviour, be mercifull unto

me a greivous sinfull Creature.

My Idle vaine or unprofitable thoughts

in which I have in my youth spent, much of that time

thou gavest me to prepare for heavn; my sining

against, much love, light, mercys; delvrances.

In sinning against thy holy Gospell; Sacraments

thy dictates; the motions of thy holy spirritt, thy

Lawes, & Commandements. In sinning against

All thy Threatnings, delivrances, Judgements.

Against thy gracious returnes of Praiers, & my vowes

& teares: & repentance; in relapses, in to sin, not

withstanding all my vowes, & Resolutions. in neglet

of Prayers, meditations. Sermons, Convictions, & thy

offten frequent Preservations of me, & mine, in

sinning against thy offten & many fold delivrncs

from Dangers, sickneses; Destructions prepared for my

Soule & body. by spirituall & temporall. foes.

vild an unworthy, ungratefull, & forgettfull wretch
