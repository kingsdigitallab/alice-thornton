After my deare mother, & us 3 children,
my brother, Christopher , & brother John & myself
with my two nephews, Tom & Kitt Danbys, came
to England, staied at Chester, no longer then
we could gett safely into yorkshire, we passed
many dangers, & Enimyes: but our God delivred
us out of them. all blessed be his holy name for Evr.
