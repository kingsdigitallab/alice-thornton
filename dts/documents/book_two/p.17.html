I had likewise the great comfort of severall
deare freinds(Entity). which came to see me, & staid somme
time till the extreamity of my Passion of greife
was mittigated (by which I was allmost killd.)
These (I thanke God) was soe fortunate as to
returne much sattisfied of the goodness of God.
who would not suffer me to lye too Long under
the scourge of the Toungs of malicious Persons.
but, even in the midest of this missery, gave me
some releife. least that by the multitude of sorowes
my Life should be swallowed up

By the assistance of these freinds(Entity) did I receave
the benifitt of the Resurection of my good name.
which seemed to be Eclipsed by the false calumnys at
a distance where I had bin traduced. even my
very Enimyes themselves had there mouths
stoped. God being pleased that there consiences
did accuse them & many of them did with tears
acknowledge theire sin in beleiving or hearing of
lies against me. Contrary to there owne knowledg
and begged uppon there knees for Christs sake to
Pardon there wickedness. for they knew I was
innocent. These to whom God had givein the
grace to repent. I prayed the Lord to forgive them.
