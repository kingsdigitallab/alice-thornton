Nor durst they tell me in what a condition my

added to my owe

I.e., own,

extreamity with losse of Blood might

have have extinguished my miserable Life

But removing her in her Cradle into the blew

Parlor a great way off me. least I hearing her sad scriks

should resume my Sorrowes.

These extreamitys did soe lessen my milke that

Tho I began to recrute strength yet I must be subject

to the changes of my condittion

Affter my d. naly was in most miraculous mery

Sic for 'mercy'.



Restored to me the next day. and recruted my strength

with in a fortnight I recovred my milke & was over

Joyed to give my sweete betty Suck which I did & began

to recover to a miracle blesed be my great & gracious

Lord God. who remembred mercy towards me.
