My Lord leaving the booke on the Table

at his goeing to bed. his Son Timmothy knew

somthing of the Subject, that it was of an

Excelent nature, as this, that came from him

thought it would be of great advantage

to himselfe in poynt of instruction beeing a

young man & but comming into the world.

And soe with out my Lords knowledge did

Copy it over him selfe, as well as he could by

nights, when my Lord was asleepe & soe he

satt many nights up the most of it to doe

it, for he durst not have don it if my Lord

had discovred him.

I confess said my Coz:, it was a very bold

part in him to doe it, & what he ought not.

but since it soe fell out that the orriginall

of my Lords Booke was lost: I thinke it was

very well, that we had somthing of him.

tho it be not soe perfectly writt as by day one

might correct the, mistakes that ner N'er? a scoller

where it is not true spelled.

me and said he would correct any thing of that
