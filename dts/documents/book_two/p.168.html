Of King Charles the Second in the yeare 1659.

Together with A full relation of my deare & hon.d

mothers last Sickness and Death Dec. 9th 1659

Page 168 till Page 179 in my first Booke.

Meditations uppon my delivry of my 6th

Childe william Thornton, & of his death.

Aprill 12th 1660 at St Nicholas my Aunt Nort

tons house. beeing my first Childe affter my

Deare mothers Decease. Dec. 9 1659

After my deare Mothers death I remained

still att Hipswell awhile till I could remove

safely by reason of my owne weakness & greife.

and watching with her I had gott a very great

Cough yett I could never doe enough for soe

tender & deare a Parent nor shew my duty

to soe Excelent vertue whose loss all the Country

extreamly lamented.

not be removed safely with my life till march
