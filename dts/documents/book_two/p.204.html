And thou o my sweete Savior did call on me
To Come unto thee all yee that are havy Laden
and Labur under sin and thou would give me
rest,

O Lett me find rest unto my Soule for thy yoke
is Easy & thy burden is light. O Lord this holy
word has come into my soule & suported me
from sinking & cured my Soule & delvred me
from the Rage of hell & Satan lett me I besech thee
ever be under thy wing of Providence & guide
me a right in thy waies That I may nevr fall into
Temptation of sin the world or the Devill who lik
a Roring Lion would have devoured my Soule
& my sweete infant in my wombe .

But o my God & father of mercys in Jesus
Christ doe not leave us to his fury nor lett him
have Powr to devoure me thy handmaide &
servant who thou hast soe wonderfully delvrd
us. but I pray thee o Lord to Preserve this poore
Infant still by thy Powr and Providence that
Sin nor Satan may nevr destroy it nor have
noe part in me or my Child who soe mightily
thou hast presevd
