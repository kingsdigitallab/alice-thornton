hast not cast out my Soule from thy favour.

I humbly beg mercy of thee o holy Jesus for my

selfe & this my Child That she may lve before

thee & be indowed with the grace of thy holy spirritt

and long life if it be thy good Pleasure to serve thee

in this generation to lve to be a Comfort to her

Parents. / O give me allso thy heavnly grace

and wisdome to doe my duty to thee o God & this

my Child in bringing her up in the feare of thee my

heavenly father & shew my humble gratitude for
