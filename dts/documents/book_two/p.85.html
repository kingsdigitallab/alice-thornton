From Ruine: in the yeare 1656

Oh most great; most, gratious, most
Powerfull, & Glorious, Lord who canest do
all things, in heaven; & Earth by whom we
had our Beeing, by whom we now live, & move
& have our beeing; Oh thou that sittest in the
heavens & rulest over all from Etternity, to
Etternity God Blessed for Ever.

Oh my God, what am I poore & sinfull dust
and Ashes, how dare I to presume to come
into thy presence, to speake, or make my Praiers
before soe holly a God as thou art when I considr
thy Glory thy majesty & thy omnipotence.
thy justice againnst, Sinners, thy Purity that
cannot behold sin & inniquity
